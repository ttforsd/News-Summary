## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Italy Seizes €1.3bn from Campari Owner Over Tax Evasion**
    *   Italian police confiscated €1.3bn in shares from Lagfin, the holding company controlling Campari, over alleged tax evasion.
    *   The investigation targets €5.3bn in undeclared capital gains during a 2018-2020 merger.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpq11v5d1rno](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpq11v5d1rno))

*   **China to Ease Chip Export Ban to Europe**
    *   China will loosen its ban on chip exports to Europe, imposed after the Netherlands seized Chinese-owned chipmaker Nexperia.
    *   The move aims to address supply chain concerns that threatened European carmakers.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly44qwgnx0o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly44qwgnx0o))

*   **UK Pledges £420m Energy Bill Cut for Heavy Industry**
    *   The UK government will cut £420m from electricity network charges for about 500 energy-intensive businesses.
    *   This 90% discount on network costs aims to boost competitiveness, but unions criticize "obscene" energy profits.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5ype0gp7lgo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5ype0gp7lgo))

*   **UK Tax Hike Plans Stir Debate Ahead of Budget**
    *   Labour's potential plans to raise council tax for higher bands and increase income tax are causing internal party dissent.
    *   These considerations come ahead of the November Budget as the government seeks to address public finances.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn511903ekvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn511903ekvo))

*   **Apple Reports Strong iPhone Demand, Faces Tariff Impact**
    *   Apple announced a "tremendous global uptake" for its latest iPhones and forecast record holiday season revenue.

    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz6n98exv9zo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz6n98exv9zo))

*   **YouTube TV Drops Disney Channels Amid Fee Dispute**
