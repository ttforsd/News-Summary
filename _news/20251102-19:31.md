## Major Regional Events

*   **Hurricane Melissa Devastation in Jamaica:**
    *   Hurricane Melissa, a Category 5 storm, caused widespread destruction in western Jamaica, including Falmouth, Westmoreland, and St Elizabeth.
    *   The death toll has risen to 28, with expectations of further increases as aid reaches isolated communities.
    *   Many areas are without power, food, or water, with roads blocked by debris.
    *   Impact: Severe humanitarian crisis, major infrastructure damage, long-term recovery challenges for the island.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpv11w8ez10o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpv11w8ez10o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1e34p92q0vo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1e34p92q0vo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c4gj9j66wxno](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c4gj9j66wxno))
*   **Sudan Civil War & Humanitarian Crisis:**
    *   El-Fasher, a key city in western Sudan, fell to the Rapid Support Forces (RSF), displacing over 60,000 people.
    *   Reports describe widespread atrocities, including violence, rape, and mass killings, with many unaccompanied minors fleeing.
    *   Impact: Escalating humanitarian crisis, famine-like conditions, collapse of health services, and challenges for aid delivery.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clyk1nq6v4lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clyk1nq6v4lo))
