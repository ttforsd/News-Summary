## Executive summary

*   **US Supreme Court questions Trump tariffs:** Justices expressed skepticism over the legality of sweeping tariffs, potentially impacting trade policy.
*   **Democrats sweep key US elections:** Victories in New York City mayoral race and gubernatorial contests in Virginia and New Jersey signal continued Democratic strength.
