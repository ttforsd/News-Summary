## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Elon Musk's $1 Trillion Tesla Pay Deal Approved by Shareholders**
    *   Approval: Tesla shareholders voted 75% in favor of Musk's unprecedented compensation package.
    *   Conditions: Payout contingent on reaching ambitious market value and operational targets over 10 years.
    *   Strategy: Musk highlighted Optimus robot and AI as central to Tesla's future.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwyk6kvyxvzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwyk6kvyxvzo))

*   **Boeing Criminal Case for 737 Max Crashes Dismissed**
    *   Ruling: US court granted request to dismiss the criminal fraud conspiracy charge against Boeing.
    *   Disagreement: Judge Reed O'Connor expressed concerns but lacked authority to deny the proposal.
    *   Impact: Major win for Boeing, ending a long legal battle, despite opposition from victims' families.
    *   Terms: Company must hire an independent compliance consultant and commit to new financial compensation.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c891k875x3qo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c891k875x3qo))

*   **US Unveils Deals to Lower Weight-Loss Drug Prices**
    *   Announcement: President Trump announced agreements with Eli Lilly and Novo Nordisk.
    *   Pricing: Aims to cap costs for GLP-1 drugs (Wegovy, Zepbound) between $245 and $350 monthly.
    *   Access: Expands access through Medicare and Medicaid; some tariffs waived for Eli Lilly.
    *   Context: Part of administration's push to align US drug prices with lower international rates.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czxk7k9nd11o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czxk7k9nd11o))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **New Portable Tech Improves Brain Monitoring for Newborns**
    *   Innovation: A "swimming cap" uses light and ultrasound for comprehensive brain monitoring.
    *   Advantage: Portable device allows frequent, comfortable monitoring at the cot-side.
    *   Potential: Could enable earlier diagnosis for cerebral palsy, epilepsy, and learning difficulties.
    *   Outlook: Expected to be available in UK hospitals within a decade.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly42gvrq2ko](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly42gvrq2ko))

*   **Major Trial for Pancreatic Cancer Breath Test Initiated**
    *   Objective: Develop an early detection breath test by identifying volatile organic compounds.
    *   Funding: Pancreatic Cancer UK invested over £1.1 million for a multi-centre validation study.
    *   Goal: Aims for NHS adoption, potentially revolutionizing early diagnosis, which is currently lacking.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp85z27z3l5o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp85z27z3l5o))
