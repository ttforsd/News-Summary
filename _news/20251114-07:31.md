## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **US Government Shutdown Ends After Record Duration**
    *   President Trump signed a funding bill, ending the longest federal shutdown in US history at 43 days.
    *   Impact: Federal workers to receive back pay; flight cuts frozen, and government services to resume.
    *   Context: Democrats felt bruised by the outcome, having received few concessions on healthcare subsidies.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/12/government-shutdown-house-vote-trump.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/12/government-shutdown-house-vote-trump.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz917nvj7qeo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz917nvj7qeo))
*   **Fed Rate Cut Hopes Fade Amid Economic Uncertainty**
    *   Markets are rethinking a December rate cut, with Fed officials expressing apprehension over inflation and data visibility.
    *   Impact: US stocks slumped, and Treasury yields moved higher as rate cut probability dropped to 50%.
    *   Context: A lack of October economic data due to the shutdown complicates Fed's policy decisions.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/13/markets-rethink-december-rate-cut-amid-fed-doubts.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/13/markets-rethink-december-rate-cut-amid-fed-doubts.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/14/cnbc-daily-open-flying-blind-in-markets-and-the-economy.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/14/cnbc-daily-open-flying-blind-in-markets-and-the-economy.html))
*   **Global Tech Stocks Decline, Sparking Valuation Concerns**
    *   Asia-Pacific and Wall Street markets fell sharply, driven by a selloff in tech stocks like Nvidia and Oracle.
    *   SoftBank shares plunged after selling its entire Nvidia stake to fund AI ventures, extending a three-day selloff.
    *   Context: Growing investor worry over high tech valuations and significant capital expenditure commitments in the AI sector.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/14/asia-shares-track-wall-street-declines-as-tech-selloff-deepens-fed-rate-cut-hopes-fade.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/14/asia-shares-track-wall-street-declines-as-tech-selloff-deepens-fed-rate-cut-hopes-fade.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/14/softbank-shares-plunge-over-8percent-extending-selloff-into-third-day.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/14/softbank-shares-plunge-over-8percent-extending-selloff-into-third-day.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/13/wall-street-cools-on-oracles-buildout-plans-as-debt-concerns-mount-ai-sentiment-is-waning.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/13/wall-street-cools-on-oracles-buildout-plans-as-debt-concerns-mount-ai-sentiment-is-waning.html))
*   **China's Economic Slowdown Intensifies**
    *   October data reveals a worsening economy with fixed-asset investment contracting 1.7% in the first ten months.
    *   Housing slump deepened, retail sales growth softened, and industrial output slowed.
    *   Context: Frugal shoppers reduced Singles' Day sales growth to 14.2%, significantly lower than previous years.

    *   The International Energy Agency's new outlook indicates oil demand could grow until 2050 under current policies.
    *   Context: Marks a significant tonal shift, previously forecasting peak demand by 2030 and calling for an end to new fossil fuel investments.
    *   Impact: OPEC welcomed the "rendezvous with reality," while climate concerns over rising global temperatures persist across all IEA scenarios.
