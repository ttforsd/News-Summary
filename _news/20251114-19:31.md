## Major regional Events

*   **Deadly Russian Strikes Hit Kyiv and Ukraine**
    *   Russian drones and missiles targeted Kyiv, killing six people in a residential building and wounding dozens.
    *   Attacks also caused power disruptions and hit an oil terminal in Novorossiysk, Russia.
    *   Ukraine's President Zelensky condemned the "calculated attack" and reported counter-strikes using long-range missiles.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn4jn94gyllo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn4jn94gyllo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c3w9je1d3jno](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c3w9je1d3jno))
*   **UN Orders Probe into Sudan Mass Killings**
    *   The UN Human Rights Council has unanimously backed an independent investigation into mass killings in el-Fasher, Sudan.
    *   Over 150,000 people have been killed and 12 million displaced in Sudan's civil war.
    *   Findings could be shared with the International Criminal Court.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70jd4elgwzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70jd4elgwzo))
*   **South Africa Investigates Mysterious Palestinian Arrivals**
    *   South African President Cyril Ramaphosa ordered an investigation into the "mysterious" arrival of 153 Palestinians from Gaza.
    *   The group, arriving via Nairobi, lacked proper entry stamps and were initially denied entry.
    *   South Africa, a strong supporter of the Palestinian cause, eventually admitted most of the group.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3dn3g4jdxjo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3dn3g4jdxjo))
*   **UK Court Holds Mining Firm Liable for Brazil Disaster**
    *   London's High Court found mining company BHP liable for Brazil's 2015 Samarco dam collapse, which killed 19 people and caused extensive environmental damage.
    *   The civil lawsuit representing 600,000 claimants is valued at up to £36 billion ($48 billion).
    *   BHP plans to appeal the ruling.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8034v2e3l3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8034v2e3l3o))
*   **Tanzania to Probe Election Protest Deaths**
    *   President Samia Suluhu Hassan announced an official investigation into deaths during recent election unrest, urging leniency for those arrested.
    *   The announcement follows calls from the UN Human Rights chief for a transparent inquiry into hundreds of alleged deaths.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gpdxp5vl2o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gpdxp5vl2o))
*   **French Anti-Drug Campaigner's Brother Killed in Marseille**
