    *   A Board of Peace (BoP) will oversee Gaza's governance and reconstruction efforts.

    *   Deliveries are planned by 2035; joint production of interceptor drones starts this year.
    *   Deal aims to significantly boost Kyiv's defense against Russian attacks.
    *   Financial details are pending, with EU funding and frozen Russian assets under consideration.

*   **Saudi Crown Prince to Visit US**

    *   Discussions expected on defense, trade, technology, AI, security, and nuclear energy.
    *   Visit aims to rehabilitate Saudi image and strengthen strategic US alliance.
