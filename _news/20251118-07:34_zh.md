## 科技、技術與健康 (主要發展)

*   **AI發展引發記憶體晶片短缺與勞動力市場變革**
    *   AI熱潮正加劇記憶體晶片短缺，恐影響汽車和智能手機生產，導致價格上漲。
    *   晶片製造商優先供應AI伺服器高利潤晶片，減少對消費性產品的投資。 (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/17/ai-boom-is-fueling-a-memory-chip-shortage-that-could-hit-cars-and-phones.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/17/ai-fuels-memory-chip-shortage-that-could-hit-cars-and-phones.html))
    *   AI亦可能導致勞動力市場出現「平靜期」，企業因AI提升員工生產力而減少招聘入門級職位。
    *   擔憂: 長期可能造成人才管道枯竭，並削弱企業創新能力。 (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/16/why-replacing-junior-staff-with-ai-will-backfire-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/16/why-replacing-young-people-with-ai-will-backfire-.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/17/ai-could-be-causing-quiet-time-in-labor-market-top-trump-economic-aide-hassett-says.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/17/ai-could-be-causing-quiet-time-in-labor-market-top-trump-economic-aide-hassett-says.html))
*   **Nvidia與Arm強化AI晶片整合技術**
    *   Arm宣布其基於Arm技術的中央處理器（CPU）將能與Nvidia的NVLink Fusion技術整合。
    *   Impact: 此合作將簡化Arm架構CPU與Nvidia GPU的搭配使用，有助於超大型企業客製化AI基礎設施。 (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/17/arm-nvidia-nvlink-partnership-custom-cpu.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/17/arm-nvidia-nvlink-partnership-custom-cpu.html))
    *   Nvidia執行長黃仁勳預測，公司在2025及2026年AI晶片訂單總額將達5000億美元。 (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/17/huangs-half-a-trillion-nvidia-forecast-will-come-up-at-q3-earnings.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/17/huangs-half-a-trillion-nvidia-forecast-will-come-up-at-q3-earnings.html))
