## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Ukraine Peace Plan Proposed by US & Russia, Rejected by Kyiv**
    *   A 28-point draft peace plan, reportedly devised by the US and Russia, proposes major concessions from Ukraine.
    *   Plan includes Ukraine ceding parts of Donbas, limiting its military to 600,000 personnel, and pledging not to join NATO.
    *   Ukrainian President Zelensky rejects territorial concessions and sovereignty limits, but is open to "honest work."
    *   Russian President Putin acknowledged the plan as a potential basis for peace but remains uncompromising on Russia's terms.
    *   European allies express concern over Ukraine's exclusion from drafting the proposals.
