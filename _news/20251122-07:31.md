## Daily Global Briefing – November 22, 2025

## Executive summary

*   **Ukraine Peace Plan:** A leaked US-Russia peace plan for Ukraine proposes territorial concessions and military limits, drawing skepticism from Kyiv and Western allies, though Russia views it as a basis for talks.
*   **AI Market Jitters:** Global tech stocks experienced significant volatility, with AI-related shares, including Nvidia and SoftBank, declining amid persistent "AI bubble" concerns.
*   **Japan's Economy:** Japan approved a $135.5 billion stimulus package while its core inflation reached a three-month high, prompting central bank rate hike considerations.
*   **Geopolitical Tensions:** China-Japan relations strained over Taiwan comments, leading to cultural event cancellations. A US military buildup near Venezuela raises regional security concerns.
*   **Social & Humanitarian:** South Africa declared gender-based violence a national disaster, and Nigeria faced mass school kidnappings amidst rising insecurity.
*   **Tech Regulation:** Australia announced a ban on social media for children under 16, a world-first policy aimed at reducing online harm.
*   **Corporate Milestone:** Eli Lilly became the first healthcare company to reach a $1 trillion market capitalization, driven by demand for its weight-loss and diabetes drugs.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Russia Peace Plan for Ukraine Emerges**
    *   A leaked 28-point draft plan, reportedly from US-Russia talks, suggests Ukraine cede Donbas territory and limit its military.
    *   Ukraine would not join NATO but could pursue EU membership.
    *   Plan includes reintegrating Russia into the global economy and recognizing Crimea, Luhansk, Donetsk as *de facto* Russian.
    *   Ukrainian President Zelenskyy and EU officials expressed concerns over terms that impact sovereignty and lack Ukrainian input.
    *   Russian President Putin views the proposals as a potential "basis" for peace.
    *   Impact: Creates diplomatic pressure on Ukraine; unlikely to be accepted in its current form.
    (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cde6yld78d6o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cde6yld78d6o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/21/ukraine-peace-plan-zelenskyy-to-speak-with-trump-on-deal-to-end-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/21/ukraine-peace-plan-zelenskyy-to-speak-with-trump-on-deal-to-end-war.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y9k0mrm2ro](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y9k0mrm2ro))

*   **G20 Summit in South Africa Marked by Absences**
    *   South Africa is hosting the G20, aiming to champion issues for developing nations.
    *   US President Donald Trump is not attending, citing discredited claims of "white genocide."
    *   China's Xi Jinping and Russia's Vladimir Putin are also notably absent.
    *   Impact: Raises questions about multilateralism's effectiveness; EU signed critical minerals deal with South Africa.
    (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c20508y7w5yo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c20508y7w5yo))

*   **US Military Buildup Near Venezuela**
    *   A significant US military presence, including warships and aircraft, has accumulated near Venezuela.
    *   This follows over 20 US airstrikes on alleged drug boats, killing more than 80 people.
    *   Venezuela accuses the US of stoking tensions; US cites self-defense against "narco-terrorists."
    *   Impact: Escalates regional tensions; sparks speculation of potential military action or regime change in Venezuela.
    (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c7810w37vwdo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c7810w37vwdo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgqlqjz30zxo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgqlqjz30zxo))

*   **Venezuela: Opposition Leader Faces Arrest Threat**
    *   Opposition leader María Corina Machado risks being declared a "fugitive" if she leaves Venezuela to accept her Nobel Peace Prize.
    *   She is accused of "conspiracy, incitement of hatred, and terrorism" by the attorney general.
    *   Impact: Highlights continued political repression in Venezuela; challenges international recognition of democratic efforts.
    (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c33mnvy5zppo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c33mnvy5zppo))

*   **US Deporting Iranian Christian Converts**
    *   The US is deporting Iranian Christian converts, whose asylum claims were rejected, back to Iran.
    *   Sensitive religious information was reportedly not removed from their files, raising persecution risks in Iran.
    *   Impact: Contradicts US public stance on protecting Christians abroad; raises human rights concerns regarding asylum process.
    (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdege0k962do](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdege0k962do))

*   **China-Japan Tensions Escalate**

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Australia to Ban Social Media for Under-16s**
    *   Australia plans to ban social media access for children under 16 from December 10, a world-first policy.
    *   Platforms must implement "reasonable steps" for age verification; face large fines for non-compliance.
    *   Impact: Aims to curb exposure to harmful content and cyberbullying; raises concerns about implementation, data privacy, and potential youth isolation.
    (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwyp9d3ddqyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwyp9d3ddqyo))

*   **Amazon Layoffs Impact Engineers Amid AI Focus**
    *   Amazon's recent layoffs included over 1,800 engineers, representing nearly 40% of the total job cuts in key states.
    *   Cuts targeted gaming, advertising, and AI search teams.
    *   Impact: Reflects CEO Jassy’s strategy for a leaner, faster company focused on AI, but raises questions about AI's impact on the tech workforce.
    (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/21/amazon-cut-thousands-of-engineers-in-its-record-layoffs-filings-show.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/21/amazon-cut-thousands-of-engineers-in-its-record-layoffs-filings-show.html))

*   **Robotaxi Industry Reaches "Tipping Point"**
    *   Baidu CEO and Nvidia leaders assert the robotaxi industry has reached a "tipping point" for broader adoption.
    *   Chinese robotaxi companies are expanding internationally faster than US rivals, with Baidu claiming per-vehicle profitability in some areas.
    *   Impact: Signals accelerating global competition and significant growth potential in autonomous vehicle technology.
    (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/20/global-robotaxi-race-heats-up-between-us-and-chinese-rivals.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/20/global-robotaxi-race-heats-up-between-us-and-chinese-rivals.html))

*   **Santorini Earthquake Swarm Explained**
    *   Scientists identified the cause of a 2025 "earthquake swarm" near Santorini as molten rock moving horizontally through Earth's crust.
    *   Over 25,000 tremors were detected over three months, mapped using physics and AI.
    *   Impact: Advances understanding of seismic activity; method could aid in forecasting volcanic eruptions.
    (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz7p783g2j5o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz7p783g2j5o))
