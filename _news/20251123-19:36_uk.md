    *   Questions were raised about the future effectiveness and relevance of the COP process.

    *   Chancellor Reeves is expected to announce tax increases, a rail fare freeze, and potentially scrap the two-child benefit cap.
    *   Concerns exist that proposed Budget policies could disproportionately burden younger generations with national debt.
