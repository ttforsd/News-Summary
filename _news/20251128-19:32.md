## Major regional Events

*   **Hong Kong High-Rise Fire Kills 128, Sparks Outrage**
    *   The death toll from a devastating high-rise fire in Hong Kong reached 128, the city's deadliest in over 70 years.
    *   Eight people were arrested for corruption related to renovation work; three others for manslaughter.
    *   Flammable materials and non-functional fire alarms are blamed for the rapid spread, leading to public anger.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c79xd17dre3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c79xd17dre3o))

    *   This follows a deadly Washington D.C. shooting by an Afghan national, Rahmanullah Lakanwal, now charged with murder.
    *   The Afghan community in the US condemned the attack but urged against broad immigration suspensions.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdxweyy157go](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdxweyy157go), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0q5w8l88dvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0q5w8l88dvo))
