## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **U.S. Tightens Immigration Policies After D.C. Shooting**
    *   The Trump administration has halted all asylum claim decisions and suspended immigration requests for Afghan nationals.
    *   This follows a shooting in Washington D.C. by an Afghan national that killed one National Guard member and injured another.
    *   A review of green cards issued to individuals from 19 "third-world countries" is also underway.
    *   Impact: Significant shift in U.S. immigration stance; potential impact on Afghan evacuees.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdxweyy157go](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdxweyy157go), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0q5w8l88dvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0q5w8l88dvo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpwk2p0d94xo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpwk2p0d94xo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/28/trump-us-to-permanently-pause-migration-from-all-third-world-countries-after-dc-shooting.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/28/trump-us-to-permanently-pause-migration-from-all-third-world-countries-after-dc-shooting.html))

*   **Ukraine's Top Adviser Resigns Amid Corruption Probe and Peace Talks**

    *   Impact: Political instability in Kyiv, potential complications for ongoing peace negotiations.

## Major regional Events

*   **Deadly Fire Engulfs Hong Kong High-Rises**
    *   At least 128 people died in a devastating fire in Hong Kong's Wang Fuk Court residential estate.
    *   Eight people have been arrested for corruption related to renovation works, with three others on manslaughter charges.
    *   Investigation ongoing into flammable materials and non-functional fire alarms contributing to rapid spread.
    *   Impact: City's deadliest fire in over 70 years, prompting widespread anger and calls for accountability. Chinese firms pledged millions in aid.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c79xd17dre3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c79xd17dre3o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn8e5j20g27o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn8e5j20g27o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/27/chinese-firms-rush-to-pledge-millions-in-fire-rescue-as-hong-kong-fire-death-toll-climbs-to-55.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/27/chinese-firms-rush-to-pledge-millions-in-fire-rescue-as-hong-kong-fire-death-toll-climbs-to-55.html))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Goldman Sachs Predicts Gold Will Hit $5K by 2026**
    *   A Goldman Sachs survey found 36% of institutional investors expect gold to reach $5,000 per troy ounce by end-2026.
    *   Gold prices rallied 58.6% year-to-date, surpassing $4,000 for the first time on October 8th, driven by central bank buying and inflation/geopolitical hedging.
    *   Impact: Continued bullish sentiment in the precious metals market amidst global economic uncertainties.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/28/goldman-sachs-investor-poll-gold-can-top-5k-in-2026-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/28/goldman-sachs-investor-poll-gold-can-top-5k-in-2026-.html))

*   **Silver Reaches Record Highs, Poised for Further Growth**
    *   Silver prices peaked at $54.47 per troy ounce in mid-October, a 71% year-on-year rise, due to low supply and high demand.
    *   Mine production has been decreasing, especially in Central and South America, while industrial demand for electric vehicles, AI components, and photovoltaics is increasing.
    *   Impact: Silver is an increasingly important commodity due to its dual role as a precious metal and industrial component, facing ongoing supply constraints.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/29/silver-hit-record-highs-in-2025-and-still-has-further-to-run.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/29/silver-hit-record-highs-in-2025-and-still-has-further-to-run.html))

*   **India's Economy Outperforms Expectations, Faces Regulatory Challenges**

    *   Despite robust growth, Apple is challenging India's antitrust law over a potential $38 billion fine related to in-app purchase policies, using global turnover for calculation.
    *   Impact: Strong economic performance in India, but global tech giants face increasing regulatory scrutiny and potential financial penalties.

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Airbus A320 Fleet Grounded Due to Solar Radiation Glitch**
    *   Thousands of Airbus A320 family aircraft are temporarily grounded worldwide for urgent software updates.
    *   Intense solar radiation was found to corrupt flight control computer data, leading to an "uncommanded pitch down event" in a recent JetBlue flight.
    *   Impact: Global travel disruption, especially during the U.S. Thanksgiving holiday, impacting major airlines and supply chains.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8e9d13x2z7o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8e9d13x2z7o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/29/airbus-recall-disrupts-global-travel-a320-jets-grounded-glitch-linked-solar-flares.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/29/airbus-recall-disrupts-global-travel-a320-jets-grounded-glitch-linked-solar-flares.html))

    *   Impact: Major discovery for planetary science, suggesting Mars joins Earth, Saturn, and Jupiter with atmospheric electrical activity.
