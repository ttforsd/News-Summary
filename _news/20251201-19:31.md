## Major regional Events

*   **Southeast Asian Disasters - Indonesia & Hong Kong:**
    *   **Indonesia Floods:** Death toll surpassed 500, with 500 missing, across Aceh, North Sumatra, and West Sumatra.
        *   Cause: Rare cyclone over Malacca Strait and north-east monsoon.
        *   Impact: 1.4 million people affected, infrastructure destroyed, severe aid access issues.
    *   **Hong Kong High-Rise Fire:** Death toll reached 146, 79 injured, and 150 still missing in Tai Po district.
