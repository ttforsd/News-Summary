## Major regional Events

*   **Ukraine faces widespread power outages and hits to critical infrastructure from Russian strikes.**
    *   Over one million households are without electricity after overnight Russian attacks targeted energy and industrial sites across five regions.
    *   Russian strikes also destroyed medical warehouses, impacting over $200 million in health supplies.

    *   A former Russian official claims Russia can fund the war for at least 2-3 more years despite economic pressure.

    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2dklj3rxvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2dklj3rxvo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2lvplrxpe9o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2lvplrxpe9o))
