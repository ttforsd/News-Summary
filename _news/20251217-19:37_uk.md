## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **UK Inflation Update**
    *   UK inflation fell to 3.2% in November, down from 3.6% in October, exceeding analyst expectations.
        *   Detail: Lower food, alcohol, and clothing prices were key drivers.
        *   Impact: Strengthens likelihood of an interest rate cut by the Bank of England, though prices are still rising overall.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0r9x1xx0x7o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0r9x1xx0x7o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy7vjz3ngy7o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy7vjz3ngy7o))

*   **UK Council Tax Hikes**

        *   Detail: This follows a government funding rebalance shifting cash to more deprived areas.
        *   Impact: Councils with low current rates face larger increases to compensate for reduced government grants.
