## International Relations & Geopolitics

*   **EU Approves €90bn Loan for Ukraine without Russian Asset Use**
    *   European Union leaders struck a deal for a €90bn (£79bn; $105bn) loan to Ukraine for two years.
    *   The agreement avoided using frozen Russian assets, a point of contention among member states.
    *   French President Macron suggested re-engaging with Russian President Putin on discussions.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3e025vyppeo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3e025vyppeo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/19/european-union-ukraine-aid-package-russia-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/19/european-union-ukraine-aid-package-russia-war.html))
*   **US Arms Sale to Taiwan Draws Strong Chinese Condemnation**

    *   China's foreign ministry denounced the move, stating it violates the "one-China principle" and harms US interests.
    *   Taiwan's President Lai Ching-te reiterated plans to boost self-defense capabilities against potential Chinese aggression by 2027.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/18/us-taiwan-china-arms-sale-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/18/us-taiwan-china-arms-sale-.html))
*   **Nigeria Apologizes to Burkina Faso Over Military Jet Incident**

    *   The incident sparked a diplomatic row with the Alliance of Sahel States (AES), which put its air forces on alert.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c74xvp00wj3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c74xvp00wj3o))
*   **NATO Defense Spending Success Credited to Trump**
    *   NATO chief Mark Rutte praised Donald Trump for driving allied nations to increase defense spending.
    *   Members pledged to allocate 5% of their economic output to defense by 2035, citing the "long-term threat posed by Russia."
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2p4vw595jo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2p4vw595jo))
*   **Venezuela Denounces Trump's Proposed Oil Tanker Blockade**
    *   US President Trump ordered a "total and complete" blockade of sanctioned Venezuelan oil tankers.
    *   Venezuela denounced the move as "warmongering threats," accusing the US of seeking to steal its resources.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gej5ezyypo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gej5ezyypo))
