## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Nvidia Acquires Groq Assets in Record Deal:** Nvidia is acquiring assets from AI chip startup Groq for approximately $20 billion in cash, its largest purchase ever.

    *   Market Impact: Groq's founders and senior leaders will join Nvidia, while Groq will continue as an independent company.

    *   Investment Trends: Volatility and high valuations are pushing investors to seek clearer business models and returns.
    *   Hyperscalers: Big Tech companies like Amazon, Microsoft, and Meta are heavily investing in AI infrastructure, benefiting providers such as Nvidia and Broadcom.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/25/how-the-ai-market-could-splinter-in-2026-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/25/how-the-ai-market-could-splinter-in-2026-.html))
