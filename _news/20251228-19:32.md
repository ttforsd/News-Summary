## Major regional Events

    *   Myanmar's military junta began the first phase of a widely criticized "sham" election, nearly five years post-coup.

    *   Impact: The military aims to legitimize its rule, but widespread opposition and fear persist among voters.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy7vly6gm76o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy7vly6gm76o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwyxller1j5o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwyxller1j5o))

    *   Storm Johannes swept across Nordic countries, resulting in three deaths, widespread power outages, and travel disruptions.

    *   Impact: The severe winter weather caused significant infrastructure damage and safety concerns across the region.
