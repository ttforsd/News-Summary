## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Ukraine Peace Deal Progress Amid Thorny Issues:**
    *   US President Trump and Ukrainian President Zelenskyy reported progress in peace talks to end the Russia-Ukraine war.
    *   Detail: Zelenskyy cited 90% agreement on a 20-point plan; Trump noted "close to 95%" on security guarantees.
    *   Context: Territorial disputes remain a "thorny issue," with Russia demanding more Ukrainian land.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c36z615y443o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c36z615y443o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/28/trump-zelenskyy-ukraine.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/28/trump-zelenskyy-ukraine.html))
*   **China Launches Military Drills Around Taiwan:**
    *   China initiated military exercises near Taiwan, simulating the seizure and blockade of key areas.
    *   Detail: Codenamed "Justice Mission 2025," the drills are a warning to "separatist forces."
    *   Impact: Taiwan condemned the drills, deploying forces and missile systems on high alert.
    *   Context: Comes days after the US announced an $11 billion weapons package sale to Taiwan.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c87l7xjp235o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c87l7xjp235o))
*   **Italy Arrests Nine for Alleged Hamas Funding:**
    *   Italian police arrested nine individuals accused of raising approximately €7 million for Hamas.
    *   Detail: Funds were allegedly disguised as humanitarian aid but diverted to the militant group's military wing.
    *   Context: The investigation began after the October 7, 2023, Hamas attack on Israel.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqxq59np394o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqxq59np394o))
*   **Trump Proposes "Trump-Class" Battleship:**
    *   President Trump unveiled plans for a new "Trump-class" battleship, touting it as "100 times more powerful" than any prior vessel.
    *   Impact: Defense experts dismissed the concept as obsolete, a "prestige project" that clashes with modern naval strategy.
    *   Concern: High costs and technical infeasibility were cited as major obstacles.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/26/trump-class-battleship-uss-defiant-golden-fleet-rail-gun-laser-reality-defense-war-experts-skeptical.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/26/trump-class-battleship-uss-defiant-golden-fleet-rail-gun-laser-skeptical.html))

    *   Challenges: Syria faces widespread destruction, economic hardship, and concerns about the interim government.
    *   Context: Some Syrians report feeling subtle pressure to leave Turkey due to changing policies.

    *   Impact: The win ends months of political deadlock, crucial for accessing vital EU funds.
    *   Outlook: Kurti faces the task of improving relations with the EU, US, and Serbia.

    *   Detail: Major political parties are dissolved, leaders jailed, and large areas of the country are excluded from voting.

    *   Context: Activists link the surge to the regime's efforts to instill fear and suppress internal opposition after 2022 protests.

    *   Context: Both leaders face scrutiny over human rights records but maintain complex international relations.

*   **Honduran Remittances Surge Amid US Deportation Fears:**

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **AI's Energy Crunch Drives Data Center Innovation and Policy Dilemmas:**
    *   The immense energy demands of AI are spurring new data center designs, including "data spas" for heat reuse and proposals for orbital servers.
    *   Challenges: Cost, engineering, regulations, and grid capacity are significant hurdles.
    *   Policy Conflict: Europe faces a critical decision point between fostering AI competition and adhering to strict climate goals.
        *   Concern: Fear that green energy will be diverted to power data centers instead of replacing fossil fuels.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/29/future-of-the-cloud-from-spas-to-orbital-space-data-centers.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/29/future-of-the-cloud-from-spas-to-orbital-space-data-centers.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/27/europe-at-fork-in-the-road-between-ai-competition-and-climate.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/27/europe-at-fork-in-the-road-between-ai-competition-and-climate.html))
*   **China Proposes Strict AI Chatbot Regulations:**
    *   China introduced draft rules to control AI chatbot influence on human emotions, particularly regarding suicide and self-harm.
    *   Mandates: Requires human intervention for suicide-related conversations and guardian consent for minors using emotional companionship AI.
    *   Scope: Rules also prohibit gambling, obscene, or violent content generation.
    *   Significance: Represents a global first in regulating AI for emotional safety.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/29/china-ai-chatbot-rules-emotional-influence-suicide-gambling-zai-minimax-talkie-xingye-zhipu.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/29/china-ai-chatbot-rules-emotional-influence-suicide-gambling-zai-minimax-Taikie-xingye-zhipu.html))
*   **UK AI Infrastructure Build-out Faces Hurdles:**
    *   The UK's national AI strategy and infrastructure development are hampered by significant grid connection delays and high energy costs.
    *   Despite billions in investment from tech giants and designated "AI growth zones," implementation lags.
    *   Solution: Microgrids are being explored as a potential alternative for faster power access.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/27/has-the-uks-ai-infrastructure-buildout-been-a-success.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/27/has-the-uks-ai-infrastructure-buildout-been-a-success.html))
*   **Nvidia Acquires Groq AI Chip Assets for $20 Billion:**
    *   Nvidia completed its largest-ever deal, acquiring key AI chip assets and talent from startup Groq for an estimated $20 billion.
    *   Structure: The transaction is structured as a "non-exclusive licensing agreement" rather than a traditional acquisition.
    *   Implication: This approach is perceived by analysts as a strategy to mitigate antitrust scrutiny and solidify Nvidia's market dominance.
