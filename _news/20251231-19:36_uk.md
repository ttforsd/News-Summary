    *   This event fuels ongoing concerns about "hybrid warfare" targeting critical infrastructure in the Baltic Sea region.

    *   EU officials labeled the Russian claims a "deliberate distraction" to impede peace talks.
    *   Ukraine denies involvement; intense drone attacks on Odesa injured civilians and caused power outages.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0q4e4e4ldyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0q4e4e4ldyo))
*   **West Africa-US Diplomatic Rift:** Mali and Burkina Faso enacted reciprocal travel bans against US citizens.

    *   The West African juntas are increasingly strengthening ties with Russia amid strained Western relations.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgrdjx1d1vjo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgrdjx1d1vjo))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Space Manufacturing Advance:** A UK company, Space Forge, successfully tested a prototype factory in orbit, equipped with a 1,000C furnace.
    *   The goal is to produce ultra-pure semiconductors in space, benefiting communications and computing.
    *   Space conditions enable manufacturing of materials up to 4,000 times purer than on Earth.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62vx0pgyrgo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62vx0pgyrgo))
*   **Gene Therapy Milestone:** A five-year-old boy with spinal muscular atrophy (SMA) can now walk independently after receiving Zolgensma gene therapy.
    *   Zolgensma is considered the world's most expensive drug, offered as a one-off treatment.
    *   Over 150 children in England have benefited from this life-changing NHS treatment.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czj0wr012dxo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czj0wr012dxo))
*   **New Weight-Loss Drug Approval:** The US FDA approved a pill form of the weight-loss drug Wegovy.
    *   This is the first such pill approved by the regulator, offering a convenient alternative to injectables.
    *   Clinical trials showed an average weight loss of 16.6% among participants.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce86le5dyxyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce86le5dyxyo))
