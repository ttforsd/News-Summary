*   **China Implements Policies to Boost Birth Rates**
    *   China introduced a 13% sales tax on contraceptives and exempted childcare services from VAT starting January 1.
    *   Objective: Part of a broader effort to counteract a shrinking population and rising costs of raising children.
    *   Impact: Sparks concern about unintended consequences on unwanted pregnancies and HIV rates.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czxpk7r8w9yo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czxpk7r8w9yo))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **FDA Approves Oral Wegovy for Weight Loss**
    *   The US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) approved a pill version of the weight-loss drug Wegovy.
    *   Impact: Offers a new, convenient oral option for weight-loss treatment, previously only available as an injection.
    *   Detail: Trials showed an average weight loss of 16.6%.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce86le5dyxyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce86le5dyxyo))
*   **TikTok Battles AI-Generated Misinformation**
    *   TikTok removed fake AI-generated ads promoting prescription weight-loss drugs from accounts impersonating Boots.
    *   Impact: Highlights the growing challenge for platforms to moderate AI misuse and combat misleading health claims.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c997lkdjdy5o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c997lkdjdy5o))
*   **China Proposes New AI Regulations**
    *   China drafted strict new rules for AI services, focusing on child safety and preventing harmful content.
    *   Detail: Requirements include personalized settings for minors, time limits, and human oversight for discussions of self-harm.
    *   Impact: A significant move to regulate the fast-growing AI sector amidst global concerns about safety.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8dydlmenvro](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8dydlmenvro))
