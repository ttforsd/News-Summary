## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US Military Operation in Venezuela Captures President Maduro**

    *   **Charges:** Both are indicted in New York on narco-terrorism and weapons charges.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Berkshire Hathaway Under New Leadership**
    *   Warren Buffett officially stepped down as CEO of Berkshire Hathaway, marking the end of a six-decade tenure.
    *   **Successor:** Greg Abel has taken over as CEO, with Buffett remaining as Chairman and overseeing capital allocation.
    *   **Market Reaction:** Berkshire Hathaway shares dipped slightly as investors reacted to the leadership change.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/02/berkshire-hathaway-shares-dip-as-warren-buffett-exits-and-greg-abel-era-begins.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/02/berkshire-hathaway-shares-dip-as-warren-buffett-exits-and-greg-abel-era-begins.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/01/warren-buffetts-legacy-a-5-million-percent-return.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/01/warren-buffetts-legacy-a-5-million-percent-return.html))

*   **EV Market Leadership Shifts from Tesla to BYD**
    *   Tesla's fourth-quarter deliveries declined by 16%, contributing to an 8.6% drop in full-year deliveries.
    *   **BYD Dominance:** China's BYD surpassed Tesla in 2025 to become the world's top electric vehicle seller, delivering 2.26 million units.
    *   **Context:** Tesla faces stiff competition and challenges from controversial rhetoric; BYD benefits from strong sales of affordable models.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/02/tesla-tsla-q4-2025-vehicle-deliveries.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/02/tesla-tsla-q4-2025-vehicle-deliveries.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/02/chinas-byd-to-overtake-tesla-as-worlds-top-ev-seller-for-first-time.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/02/chinas-byd-to-overtake-tesla-as-worlds-top-ev-seller-for-first-time.html))

*   **China Restricts Silver Exports as Prices Surge**
    *   China will implement new export controls on silver, formally designating it as a strategic material.
    *   **Impact:** This move, echoing previous rare earth restrictions, is expected to affect global supply chains for industries reliant on silver.
    *   **Market Context:** Silver prices surged by nearly 150% in 2025, influenced by low supply, high demand, and its role as a hedge against a weakening US dollar.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/31/china-silver-export-controls-2026-us-economy-prices-rare-earths-critical-minerals-xag-metals.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/12/31/china-silver-export-controls-2026-us-economy-prices-rare-earths-critical-minerals-xag-metals.html))

*   **Singapore's Economy Sees Strong Q4 Growth**
    *   Singapore's GDP expanded by a robust 5.7% year-on-year in the fourth quarter, its highest since 2021.
    *   **Driver:** Growth was primarily fueled by a significant increase in manufacturing output.
    *   **Outlook:** Full-year GDP reached 4.8%, surpassing official forecasts despite global trade risks.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/02/singapore-fourth-quarter-gdp-jumps-5point7percent-beats-expectations-on-strong-manufacturing.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/02/singapore-fourth-quarter-gdp-jumps-5point7percent-beats-expectations-on-strong-manufacturing.html))

*   **Canadian Micro-Condo Market Faces Downturn**
    *   Canada's micro-condo market is experiencing a significant slump, with unit values plummeting.
    *   **Causes:** Attributed to an oversupply of units, rising interest rates, and recent caps on immigration.
    *   **Outlook:** The market is expected to shift from investor-driven speculation to a greater focus on end-users.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqxq32zzq8eo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqxq32zzq8eo))

*   **KFC and Pizza Hut Operators Merge in India**

    *   **Objective:** The merger aims to accelerate KFC's expansion and boost Pizza Hut's presence in the Indian market.

*   **Growing Opposition to AI Data Centers' Energy Demands**
