## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US Actions in Venezuela Spark Global Concern and Domestic Debate**
    *   US forces captured Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro and his wife, Cilia Flores, in Caracas; both pleaded not guilty to drug trafficking charges in a New York court, with Maduro claiming he was "kidnapped."
    *   Cuba denounced the US action, declared national mourning for 32 Cuban nationals killed, and faces bleak economic prospects without Venezuelan oil.
    *   China condemned the US military strike, calling it a "hegemonic act," but also seeks to protect its billions in economic interests in Venezuela.
    *   Russia is treading carefully, balancing condemnation with potential benefits from US focus shifting away from Ukraine, and the perceived "precedent" for leader removal.
    *   US lawmakers were split along party lines after a classified briefing on Maduro's capture, raising doubts about limits on Trump's military authority.
    *   **Impact:** Redefines US foreign policy in the Western Hemisphere under the "Donroe Doctrine"; creates global uncertainty regarding international law and sovereignty.
