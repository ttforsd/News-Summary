## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US Pursues Greenland Acquisition, Europe Pushes Back:**
    *   The White House confirms discussions, including military options, to acquire Greenland, calling it a "national security priority."
    *   European leaders (UK, France, Germany, Italy, Poland, Spain, Denmark) issued a joint statement supporting Denmark's sovereignty.
    *   Danish PM warned a US military move on Greenland would signify the end of NATO.
    *   A US Senator introduced a resolution to block military force against Greenland.
    *   Impact: Arctic security, potential NATO destabilization, and access to rare earth minerals are key factors.
