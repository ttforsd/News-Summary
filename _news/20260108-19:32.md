## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US Stance on Greenland:** US Vice-President Vance criticized Europe and Denmark for under-investing in Greenland's defense, calling the island critical for US missile defense against Russia and China.

    *   European leaders criticized the move, expressing concerns it would weaken global cooperation on critical issues.

## Major Regional Events

    *   The government has offered a small monthly allowance to citizens and threatened rapid prosecution for "rioters."
