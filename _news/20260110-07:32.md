## Major regional Events

*   **Iran Anti-Government Protests Escalate**
    *   Widespread anti-government protests are ongoing for their 13th consecutive day across Iran, considered the largest in years.
    *   Detail: Protesters are demanding an end to the Islamic Republic and some call for the restoration of the monarchy.
    *   Casualties: Human rights groups report at least 48 protesters and 14 security personnel killed, with over 2,270 arrests.
    *   Suppression: Authorities implemented a near-total internet blackout, hindering information flow.
    *   Official Stance: Supreme Leader Khamenei condemned protesters as "vandals" acting for the US; Iran blames the US for inciting violence.
    *   International Response: European leaders expressed deep concern over violence and urged freedom of expression.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g49djqqjgo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g49djqqjgo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cg7y0579lp8o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cg7y0579lp8o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c23r4yeyxl9o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c23r4yeyxl9o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/09/iran-protests-supreme-leader-khamenei-trump-oil.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/09/iran-protests-supreme-leader-khamenei-trump-oil.html))
*   **Russia Deploys Rare Hypersonic Missile in Ukraine Strikes**
    *   Russia launched its Oreshnik ballistic missile in a massive overnight strike on Ukraine, killing four and injuring 25 in Kyiv.
    *   Weaponry: This is only the second known use of the intermediate-range, hypersonic Oreshnik missile with a fragmenting warhead.
    *   Impact: Strikes targeted energy facilities and civilian infrastructure, causing power disruptions in Kyiv amid cold weather.
    *   Escalation: Ukrainian officials described the strike near the EU/NATO border as a "grave threat."
    *   Retaliation: Ukraine reported counter-strikes causing power outages in Russia's Belgorod and Oryol regions.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgqe9kv9xxvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgqe9kv9xxvo))
*   **Fatal Fire in Swiss Ski Resort Exposes Governance Flaws**
    *   A New Year's Eve bar fire in Crans-Montana, Switzerland, killed 40 young people and injured 116.
    *   Investigation: The local mayor admitted the bar hadn't been inspected since 2019, despite mandatory annual checks.
    *   Impact: The tragedy is prompting a national "soul-searching" regarding Switzerland's devolved governance system and accountability.
    *   Legal: The French owner of the bar has been detained as a potential flight risk.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpwnd22lj54o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpwnd22lj54o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn7jxm7jkg1o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn7jxm7jkg1o))
