## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Iran Warns US of Retaliation Amidst Protests**

    *   Hundreds have been killed and thousands detained in Iran over economic frustrations.
    *   The US President has threatened intervention, considering military and cyber options.

*   **US Pressure on Cuba Intensifies Post-Maduro Capture**
    *   President Trump urged Cuba to "make a deal" or face a halt of Venezuelan oil and money.
    *   This follows the US seizure of a fifth oil tanker carrying sanctioned Venezuelan crude.
    *   Cuba's Foreign Minister affirmed the country's right to import fuel without interference.
    *   This is framed within Trump's "Donroe Doctrine" for US dominance in the Western Hemisphere.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2kv2gn62vo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2kv2gn62vo))
*   **US Reasserts Arctic Interests, Threatens Greenland Action**
    *   President Trump declared the US will take action on Greenland, "whether they like it or not."
    *   Threatens a "hard way" approach if a deal with Denmark for acquisition is not reached.
    *   Justifies action by citing potential Russian or Chinese control of the strategic Arctic island.
    *   This aligns with his "Donroe Doctrine" and broader US-China geopolitical competition in Latin America and the Arctic.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/11/op-ed-trumps-donroe-doctrine-and-china-are-headed-for-latin-american-clash.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/11/op-ed-trumps-donroe-doctrine-and-china-are-headed-for-latin-american-clash.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/09/trump-greenland-military-denmark-nato.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/09/trump-greenland-military-denmark-nato.html))
*   **US Blocks Seizure of Venezuelan Oil Revenue**
    *   President Trump signed an executive order protecting Venezuelan oil revenue in US Treasury accounts.
    *   Aims to prevent private creditors, including ExxonMobil and ConocoPhillips, from seizing funds.
    *   This move follows the capture of Venezuelan leader Nicolas Maduro by US military forces.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/10/trump-venezuela-oil-revenue.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/10/trump-venezuela-oil-revenue.html))
*   **US Oil Executives Hesitant on Venezuelan Investment**
    *   Major US oil companies expressed caution regarding large-scale investments in Venezuela.
    *   ExxonMobil CEO called Venezuela "uninvestable" in its current state due to past asset seizures.
    *   ConocoPhillips emphasized need for debt restructuring and PDVSA reform.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/10/what-the-big-oil-executives-told-trump-about-investing-in-venezuela.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/10/what-the-big-oil-executives-told-trump-about-investing-in-venezuela.html))
