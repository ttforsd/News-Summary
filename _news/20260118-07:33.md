## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-European Standoff over Greenland:**
    *   President Trump announced 10% tariffs, rising to 25% by June, on 8 European allies (Denmark, Norway, Sweden, France, Germany, UK, Netherlands, Finland).
    *   Threat: Tariffs will remain "until a Deal is reached for the Complete and Total purchase of Greenland."
    *   European Response: Leaders condemned the move as "unacceptable" and "economic war"; EU called an emergency meeting.
    *   NATO Impact: Allies dispatched troops to Greenland for "Operation Arctic Endurance," viewing it as bolstering Arctic security, not opposing US acquisition.
    *   Russian View: Russia labeled Trump's push for Greenland as "extraordinary" and "unusual" from an international law standpoint.
