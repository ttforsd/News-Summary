    *   Six people were injured when a maternity hospital in Zaporizhzhia was hit.
    *   These attacks occurred despite Russia agreeing to pause strikes on population centers.

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

    *   This strategy reflects Big Pharma's global balancing act and search for new innovation hubs.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/01/astrazeneca-nyse-listing-china-pharma-pours-15-billion-into-china.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/01/astrazeneca-nyse-listing-china-pharma-pours-15-billion-into-china.html))
