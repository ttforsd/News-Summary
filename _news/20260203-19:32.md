## Major regional Events

*   **Record Russian Missile Strikes on Ukraine:**
    *   Russia launched a record number of ballistic missiles and drones targeting Ukraine's energy sector.
    *   Impact: Power plants in Kyiv and Kharkiv were hit, leaving thousands without heating in -20C.
    *   Ukraine's President Zelensky called for "maximum pressure" on Moscow and more air defense missiles from allies.
    *   NATO Secretary-General Mark Rutte, visiting Kyiv, urged member countries to provide necessary missiles.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpwng25114ro](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpwng25114ro))

*   **Colombia's Persistent Drug War:**
    *   Colombian Jungle Commandos continue to destroy crude cocaine labs in the Amazon region.
    *   Context: US President Trump criticized Colombia's efforts, while President Petro cited record drug seizures. UN reports record-high cocaine production.
    *   Impact: The "never-ending battle" persists, with labs easily rebuilt within a day, affecting criminal group profitability.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c04105ywkkqo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c04105ywkkqo))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **WHO Report on Preventable Cancers:**
    *   A WHO report estimates 37% of annual cancer cases (7 million) are preventable worldwide.
    *   Causes: Infections (HPV, hepatitis), lifestyle choices (smoking, alcohol), and environmental pollutants (air pollution).
    *   Impact: Highlights a "powerful opportunity" to reduce the global cancer burden through targeted interventions.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yd3x7yreno](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yd3x7yreno))

*   **Musk Merges SpaceX and xAI:**
    *   Elon Musk merged his rocket company SpaceX with AI startup xAI in a $1.25 trillion transaction.
    *   Goal: To create "orbital data centers" and a vertically-integrated innovation engine.
    *   Context: xAI's Grok AI faces regulatory probes in France and the UK over harmful sexualized images.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/03/musk-xai-spacex-biggest-merger-ever.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/03/musk-xai-spacex-biggest-merger-ever.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/02/elon-musk-spacex-xai-ipo.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/02/elon-musk-spacex-xai-ipo.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce3ex92557jo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce3ex92557jo))

*   **Spain Bans Social Media for Under-16s:**
    *   Spain became the first European country to ban social media access for individuals under 16.
    *   Measures: Requires platforms to implement effective age-verification systems.
    *   Reason: PM Sanchez cited risks of addiction, abuse, pornography, manipulation, and violence.
    *   Context: Follows a similar ban implemented in Australia.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/03/spain-teen-social-media-ban-tech-giants-australia.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/03/spain-teen-social-media-ban-tech-giants-australia.html))

*   **Pfizer's Monthly Obesity Injection Shows Promise:**
    *   Pfizer's experimental monthly obesity injection (PF'3944) demonstrated significant weight loss in a mid-stage trial.
    *   Impact: Offers a potential, more convenient alternative to existing weekly GLP-1 drugs in the competitive weight-loss market.
    *   Sources: (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/03/pfizers-monthly-obesity-injection-drove-shows-promise-in-trial.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/03/pfizers-monthly-obesity-injection-drove-shows-promise-in-trial.html))
