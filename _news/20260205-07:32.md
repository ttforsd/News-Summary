## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Russia Nuclear Arms Treaty Expires**
    *   The Strategic Arms Reduction Treaty (New START) expired, ending the last major nuclear arms control agreement between the US and Russia.
    *   Impact: UN Secretary General warns of a "grave moment" for international peace, calling for a successor framework.
    *   Context: Both nations are modernizing nuclear forces, raising fears of a new arms race; US wants China included in future talks, Russia wants France and UK.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g31n4ey9go](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g31n4ey9go))

*   **US-China Dialogue Amid Taiwan Tensions**
    *   Chinese leader Xi Jinping urged US President Donald Trump to be "prudent" regarding arms sales to Taiwan, calling it the "most important issue."
    *   Context: This follows a recent $11bn US arms sale to Taiwan. Trump reported discussions on trade, Ukraine, and Iran.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62wpjd3j1zo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62wpjd3j1zo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/chinas-xi-taiwan-trump-trade-talks-iran-russia.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/chinas-xi-taiwan-trump-trade-talks-iran-russia.html))

*   **US Initiatives to Counter China's Mineral Dominance**
    *   The US proposed a new trade bloc for critical minerals with 11 countries, including Mexico, the EU, and Japan.
    *   Objective: To counter China's market dominance through coordinated price floors, tariffs, stockpiling, and common regulations.
    *   Initiatives: Formation of "FORGE" for mineral policy and "Pax Silica" for AI supply chains.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/us-allies-critical-minerals-price-floors-forge-china-rare-earths-ai-chips-pax-silicchina-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/us-allies-critical-minerals-price-floors-forge-china-rare-earths-ai-chips-pax-silicchina-.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/04/us-plans-critical-mineral-price-floors-with-mexico-eu-and-japan.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/04/us-plans-critical-mineral-price-floors-with-mexico-eu-and-japan.html))
