## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Ukraine-Russia Peace Talks & Prisoner Exchange:**
    *   US-brokered peace talks in Abu Dhabi concluded with a prisoner swap of 157 Ukrainians and 157 Russian soldiers.
    *   No breakthrough was achieved on major issues like territorial concessions or security guarantees.
    *   US and Russia agreed to re-establish high-level military-to-military dialogue, suspended since 2021.
    *   Russia renewed attacks on Ukraine's energy sector following a brief pause.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62np09q7l1o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62np09q7l1o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgn2dzwd1do](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgn2dzwd1do))
*   **US-Iran Nuclear Talks:**
    *   The US and Iran agreed to hold nuclear talks in Oman on Friday.
    *   US President Trump warned Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei to be "very worried" and called for talks to cover ballistic missiles, proxy groups, and human rights.
    *   Iran seeks to focus solely on its nuclear program to lift sanctions.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgj2knp4d9o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgj2knp4d9o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/oil-prices-upcoming-us-iran-talks-revive-de-escalation-hopes.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/oil-prices-upcoming-us-iran-talks-revive-de-escalation-hopes.html))
*   **US-China-Taiwan Relations:**
    *   Chinese President Xi Jinping urged US President Trump to be "prudent" regarding arms sales to Taiwan, calling it the "most important issue."
    *   Trump described the call as "excellent" and noted discussions on Russia-Ukraine, Iran, and China's purchase of US soybeans and energy.
    *   The US is forming a "FORGE" partnership to coordinate critical mineral policy among allies, countering China's market dominance.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62wpjd3j1zo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62wpjd3j1zo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/chinas-xi-taiwan-trump-trade-talks-iran-russia.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/chinas-xi-taiwan-trump-trade-talks-iran-russia.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/us-allies-critical-minerals-price-floors-forge-china-rare-earths-ai-chips-pax-silicchina-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/us-allies-critical-minerals-price-floors-forge-china-rare-earths-ai-chips-pax-silicchina-.html))
*   **US-India Trade Deal Discussions:**
    *   Trump announced a trade deal with India to cut US tariffs on Indian goods.
    *   India is expected to lower duties on US goods, replace Russian oil, open farm markets, and buy US goods.
    *   Frictions remain regarding India's protection of sensitive sectors and implications for its relationship with Russia.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/cnbcs-inside-india-newsletter-the-facts-and-frictions-of-the-us-india-trade-deal.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/05/cnbcs-inside-india-newsletter-the-facts-and-frictions-of-the-us-india-trade-deal.html))

## Major regional Events

    *   Prosecutors allege Hamas profited from the resale of smuggled goods.
    *   The charges are part of a wider investigation into an organized crime ring smuggling various items into Gaza.

    *   Lebanon's President condemned the act as a violation of sovereignty and an environmental crime.

    *   Dozens of Muslims were reportedly massacred in Nigeria's Kwara state by jihadists (suspected Boko Haram) for refusing to join them.

    *   Another MSF facility in Pieri was looted, leaving local communities without healthcare.

    *   Ahead of general elections, the student-led National Citizen Party (NCP) formed a multi-party alliance with the Islamist Jamaat-e-Islami.
    *   This comes after a Gen Z uprising toppled former PM Sheikh Hasina, but the youth movement now faces internal divisions and challenges from older established parties.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1m7444nr3yo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1m7444nr3yo))
*   **Japan Battles Overtourism:**

    *   The influx of visitors led to chronic traffic, litter, trespassing, and public defecation, impacting local residents.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1wzrlndzjro](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1wzrlndzjro))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

    *   The European Central Bank also kept policy rates unchanged at 2% for the fifth consecutive meeting.
    *   Both central banks monitor global uncertainty and inflation outlook, with rate cuts expected later in the year.
