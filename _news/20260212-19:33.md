## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **North Korea Designates Heir**
    *   South Korea's intelligence agency believes Kim Jong Un has selected his 13-year-old daughter, Kim Ju Ae, as his successor.
    *   She has increasingly featured prominently in public events, including a recent trip to Beijing.
    *   This is a significant move in North Korea's deeply patriarchal society, despite an alleged older son.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn0e1g7kwglo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn0e1g7kwglo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgvgj7ejqdo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgvgj7ejqdo))

*   **US Immigration Enforcement Surge Ends in Minnesota**
    *   President Trump's border tsar, Tom Homan, announced the conclusion of "Operation Metro Surge" in Minnesota.
    *   The operation resulted in over 4,000 undocumented immigrant arrests but sparked nationwide protests and two citizen deaths.
    *   Minnesota Governor Tim Walz cited "deep damage, generational trauma, and economic ruin."
    *   A debate over Department of Homeland Security funding and demands for ICE reforms continue in Congress.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2lr9w29zwyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2lr9w29zwyo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/homan-trump-ice-immigration-minnesota-minneapolis.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/homan-trump-ice-immigration-minnesota-minneapolis.html))

*   **US House Votes to Override Trump's Canada Tariffs**
    *   The US House passed a resolution (219-211) disapproving of President Trump's tariffs against Canada.
    *   Several Republicans joined Democrats in a rare rebuke, despite Trump threatening electoral consequences.
    *   The measure heads to the Senate, but a presidential veto is widely expected.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/gop-trump-tariffs-canada.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/gop-trump-tariffs-canada.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/cnbc-daily-open-republicans-vote-to-end-trumps-canada-tariffs-in-rare-rebuke.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/cnbc-daily-open-republicans-vote-to-end-trumps-canada-tariffs-in-rare-rebuke.html))

*   **US-Iran Tensions Remain High**
    *   Tensions persist between the US and Iran, even after recent talks in Oman concluded at an impasse.
    *   President Trump has threatened "something very tough" if Iran fails to meet US demands regarding its nuclear and ballistic missile programs.
    *   Analysts express doubt about a military strike, citing inadequate US forces for a prolonged conflict and potential for Iranian retaliation.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/trump-talking-tough-iran-analysts-nuclear-will-us-strike.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/trump-talking-tough-iran-analysts-nuclear-will-us-strike.html))

*   **Russia Dismisses US Tariff Threats Over Cuba Oil**
    *   Russia has dismissed US threats to impose tariffs on countries supplying oil to fuel-starved Cuba, stating it is actively discussing aid.
    *   The Trump administration has intensified pressure, aiming to sanction nations providing oil to the communist-run island.
    *   Cuba is grappling with an energy crisis, implementing fuel rationing and reduced work weeks.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/cuba-fuel-shortage-oil-russia-trump-tariffs.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/cuba-fuel-shortage-oil-russia-trump-tariffs.html))

*   **India-US-Russia Trade Triangle Complicates Matters**
    *   The US recently removed a 25% penalty on India for Russian oil purchases, citing an alleged commitment to stop, which India has not officially confirmed.
    *   India's Russian oil imports increased significantly post-Ukraine war but have decreased since the US penalty.
    *   India maintains "strategic ambiguity," balancing its relationship with Russia due to historic defense ties.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/inside-india-newsletter-india-us-russia-oil-purchases-trade-deal.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/india-us-russia-oil-purchases-trade-deal.html))

## Major regional Events

*   **Mass Shooting Devastates Canadian Town**
    *   An 18-year-old suspect killed eight people, including six children and two family members, in Tumbler Ridge, British Columbia.
    *   The suspect had a history of mental health struggles and prior police visits; firearms previously seized were returned to the family.
    *   The small, tight-knit community is in shock, and British Columbia has declared a day of mourning.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clyz2x4yzqgo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clyz2x4yzqgo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cddn078mnn5o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cddn078mnn5o))

*   **Bangladesh Holds First Election Post-Authoritarian Rule**
    *   Bangladesh held its first parliamentary election since student-led protests ousted former Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina in 2024.
    *   The election pits the centre-right BNP against an Islamist Jamaat-e-Islami-led coalition.
    *   Voters expressed hope for a return to democracy, though Hasina, in exile, questions the election's legitimacy.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy7jdmvn3l0o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy7jdmvn3l0o))

*   **Cyclone Gezani Devastates Madagascar**
    *   Cyclone Gezani made landfall in Madagascar, killing at least 35 people and displacing around 250,000.
    *   The main port city of Toamasina is 75% destroyed, with extensive damage to infrastructure and homes.
    *   Disruptions to food, fuel, and medical supply chains are creating huge humanitarian challenges.
    *   Madagascar's military leader has appealed for international assistance, as this is the second cyclone to hit the island this year.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c931zzzep0eo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c931zzzep0eo))

*   **Former Norwegian PM Charged with Corruption**
    *   Former Norwegian Prime Minister Thorbjørn Jagland has been charged with "gross corruption" related to his ties with sex offender Jeffrey Epstein.
    *   The Council of Europe lifted his diplomatic immunity, enabling the charge.
    *   Emails suggest Epstein covered travel expenses for Jagland and his family, and Jagland sought Epstein's help for a bank loan.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yqr8eggvwo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yqr8eggvwo))

*   **Emirati Business Leader Linked to Epstein Email**
    *   Sultan Ahmed bin Sulayem, a high-profile Emirati business leader and DP World CEO, has been linked to a disturbing 2009 Jeffrey Epstein email referencing a "torture video."
    *   DOJ records reveal years of communications between Sulayem and Epstein on various topics, including explicit content.
    *   Canada's second-largest pension fund has halted future deals with DP World following these revelations.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/epstein-files-sultan-ahmed-bin-sulayem-dp-world-email-dubai-uae-emirati.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/epstein-files-sultan-ahmed-bin-sulayem-dp-world-email-dubai-uae-emirati.html))

*   **Boat Capsizes in Sudan's River Nile**
    *   At least 21 people died after a passenger boat, carrying nearly 30 individuals, capsized in Sudan's River Nile state.
    *   Eyewitnesses reported high waves caused the incident in the Shendi area.
    *   A medical group cited weak regulation of river transport and absence of basic safety requirements.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp851zp41m5o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp851zp41m5o))

*   **US Attorney General's Conduct Questioned**
    *   Attorney General Pam Bondi appeared to have a printout of Rep. Pramila Jayapal's DOJ database search history during a House Judiciary Committee hearing on Epstein files.
    *   Jayapal denounced this as "outrageous" and a violation of the separation of powers.
    *   The incident adds to ongoing controversy regarding the Justice Department's handling of Epstein documents.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/epstein-files-pam-bondi-jayapal-search-history-doj.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/epstein-files-pam-bondi-jayapal-search-history-doj.html))

*   **Kenya to Re-open Somalia Border**
    *   Kenya will re-open its border with Somalia in April, nearly 15 years after its closure due to attacks by the Islamist militant group al-Shabab.
    *   President William Ruto stated the move aims to boost cross-border trade and kinship, with heavy security deployment to ensure safety.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70k4yw049yo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70k4yw049yo))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **US January Payrolls Exceed Expectations**
    *   Nonfarm payrolls increased by 130,000 in January, surpassing the Dow Jones estimate of 55,000.
    *   The unemployment rate edged down to 4.3%; health care and social assistance led job gains.
    *   Average hourly earnings rose 0.4% monthly and 3.7% annually, supporting the Federal Reserve's stance on interest rates.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/jobs-report-january-2026-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/jobs-report-january-2026-.html))

*   **US Importers Face Record Customs Bond Shortfall**
    *   US importers are experiencing a record-breaking $3.6 billion shortfall in customs bond funding for fiscal 2025 due to President Trump's tariffs.
    *   The 27,479 "insufficiencies" highlight that higher tariffs lead to increased duty/tax liabilities requiring larger bonds.
    *   This accompanies a 304% surge in tariff collections to $124 billion year-to-date for the US government.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/trump-tariffs-us-customs-record-bond-funding-issues-flagged.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/trump-tariffs-us-customs-record-bond-funding-issues-flagged.html))

*   **US Housing Market Faces "New Crisis"**
    *   January home sales in the US dropped 8.4% from December, reaching the slowest pace since December 2023.
    *   The median home price hit a record $396,800 for January, but low supply and weak consumer confidence persist.
    *   The National Association of Realtors' chief economist labeled the situation a "new housing crisis."
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/january-homes-sales.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/january-homes-sales.html))

*   **UK Economy Ekes Out Modest Q4 Growth**
    *   The UK economy grew by a modest 0.1% in the fourth quarter of 2025, following similar growth in Q3.
    *   The services sector showed no growth, with manufacturing being the primary driver.
    *   Economists anticipate potential Bank of England interest rate cuts in April to stimulate the economy.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/uk-q4-gdp-economy.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/uk-q4-gdp-economy.html))

*   **Japanese Nikkei 225 Hits Record Highs**
    *   Japan's Nikkei 225 index surpassed 58,000 for the first time, extending its post-election rally driven by political optimism.
    *   Analysts warn the rally may be "fragile," detached from economic fundamentals, and vulnerable to yen shifts and global shocks.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/japan-stocks-record-ai-yen-takaichi-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/japan-stocks-record-ai-yen-takaichi-.html))

*   **Alphabet's 100-Year Bond Raises AI Debt Concerns**
    *   Alphabet issued a rare 100-year sterling bond as part of a $20 billion borrowing drive for AI infrastructure.
    *   Market strategists warn of "late-cycle exuberance" and market froth around AI, citing uncertain long-term demand.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/alphabet-100-year-bond-debt-fears-ai-credit-risk.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/alphabet-100-year-bond-debt-fears-ai-credit-risk.html))

*   **SoftBank's Vision Fund Boosted by OpenAI Investment**
    *   SoftBank's Vision Fund recorded a $2.4 billion gain in the December quarter, largely due to a $4.2 billion uplift from its OpenAI investment.
    *   SoftBank is positioning AI at the core of its strategy, with 60% of its assets in "ASI-oriented investments."
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/softbank-vision-fund-openai.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/softbank-vision-fund-openai.html))

*   **European Markets Close Mixed Post-Earnings**
    *   European stocks saw mixed performance as investors processed a large batch of earnings reports.
    *   Siemens raised its fiscal year outlook, while Hermès reported strong sales.
    *   Conversely, Dutch payments firm Adyen dropped 20%, and Mercedes-Benz saw its profit halve.
    *   UK asset manager Schroders surged 28.5% on news of a $13.5 billion takeover by Nuveen.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/european-markets-stoxx-600-ftse-dax-cac-siemens-loreal-earnings.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/european-markets-stoxx-600-ftse-dax-cac-siemens-loreal-earnings.html))

*   **Singapore Boosts AI and Financial Sector**
    *   Singapore's 2026 Budget introduces new measures to harness AI, including a "Champions of AI" program and tax breaks for AI expenditures.
    *   The country will also inject an additional S$1.5 billion ($1.18 billion) into its Financial Sector Development Fund to boost its stock market.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/singapore-2026-budget-ai-measures-ai-support-workers-companies.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/singapore-2026-budget-ai-measures-ai-support-workers-companies.html))

*   **Restaurant Brands Shares Fall Despite Earnings Beat**
    *   Restaurant Brands International (RBI) reported better-than-expected quarterly earnings and revenue, driven by strong international growth (+6.1%).
    *   However, shares fell 6% due to a slowdown in Burger King's US remodeling plans and higher costs.
    *   Popeyes' same-store sales declined by 4.8%, prompting plans for operational and menu focus.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/restaurant-brands-international-qsr-q4-2025-earnings.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/restaurant-brands-international-qsr-q4-2025-earnings.html))

*   **Dutch Payments Giant Adyen Shares Plummet**
    *   Dutch payments company Adyen saw its stock drop by 20% after issuing a weaker-than-expected revenue growth outlook for 2026.
    *   Forecasted net revenue growth of 20-22% missed analyst expectations of 22.8%, and payment processing volume also fell short.
    *   Slower growth from APAC online retailers and a weaker US dollar contributed to the moderated gains.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/adyen-stock-price-earnings.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/adyen-stock-price-earnings.html))

*   **Mercedes-Benz Profit Halves Amid Tariffs**
    *   German luxury car manufacturer Mercedes-Benz Group reported a 57% drop in full-year operating profit for 2025.
    *   The decline was attributed to $1.2 billion in tariff costs, intense competition from Chinese rivals, and foreign exchange headwinds.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/mercedes-earnings-autos-tariffs-luxury-china.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/mercedes-earnings-autos-tariffs-luxury-china.html))

*   **Chinese Factories Busy Despite Tariffs**
    *   Chinese factories and ports are experiencing a surge in activity ahead of the Lunar New Year, despite President Trump's tariffs.
    *   Major ports recorded a 40% year-on-year increase in container activity, driving up trucking and freight rates.
    *   Many multinational firms continue significant production in China, even while pursuing "China-plus-one" supply chain strategies.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/trump-tariffs-cny-chinese-factories-and-ports-are-buzzing-with-activity.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/trump-tariffs-cny-chinese-factories-and-ports-are-buzzing-with-activity.html))

*   **EU Leaders Criticized for Inaction on Competitiveness**
    *   EU leaders held an "informal retreat" to discuss boosting the bloc's competitiveness, facing criticism for a perceived lack of action on previous proposals.
    *   Past reports by former Italian prime ministers Mario Draghi and Enrico Letta have outlined solutions for economic competitiveness and the Single Market.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/-eu-leaders-competitiveness-informal-retreat-belgium.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/-eu-leaders-competitiveness-informal-retreat-belgium.html))

*   **Netherlands Debates Sustainability of Four-Day Work Week**
    *   The Netherlands has widely adopted a four-day, 32-hour work week, maintaining high productivity per capita, the lowest in the EU.
    *   However, concerns are raised about stagnant productivity growth over the past 15 years, an aging population, and a high proportion of women working part-time.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2y85xdyw3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2y85xdyw3o))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Russia Blocks WhatsApp**
    *   Russia has ordered a block on Meta-owned WhatsApp, citing non-compliance with Russian law.
    *   The Kremlin is actively promoting its state-developed Max app as an alternative, which WhatsApp labels as a "state-owned surveillance app."
    *   This action follows increasing restrictions on Telegram and is part of Russia's broader effort to control internet services.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clygd10pg5lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clygd10pg5lo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/russia-whatsapp-meta-max.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/russia-whatsapp-meta-max.html))

*   **Elon Musk's xAI Undergoes Reorganization**
    *   Elon Musk announced a reorganization of his artificial intelligence venture, xAI, involving "parting ways with some people" to improve execution speed.
    *   This follows the recent departures of several xAI co-founders and the company's acquisition by SpaceX.
    *   xAI faces regulatory probes in multiple jurisdictions over Grok's alleged role in enabling deepfake child porn.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/musk-announces-xai-re-org-following-key-departures-spacex-merger.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/11/musk-announces-xai-re-org-following-key-departures-spacex-merger.html))

*   **Chinese AI Stocks Rally on New Model Releases**
    *   Chinese artificial intelligence stocks surged, with Zhipu AI gaining 30% and MiniMax 13.7%, following new model releases.
    *   Zhipu's GLM-5 boasts enhanced coding capabilities, while MiniMax's M2.5 offers improved AI agent tools.
    *   Premier Li Qiang called for a comprehensive push for "scaled and commercialized application of AI" in China.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/chinese-ai-stocks-new-model-and-agent-releases-zhipu-minimax.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/chinese-ai-stocks-new-model-and-agent-releases-zhipu-minimax.html))

*   **FTC Asks Apple to Investigate News Censorship**
    *   FTC Chair Andrew Ferguson has urged Apple CEO Tim Cook to investigate reports of alleged conservative content censorship on Apple News.
    *   The request follows a study by the conservative Media Research Center, which found right-leaning outlets were not featured in a sample of news stories.
    *   The FTC is assessing whether such suppression constitutes a "material omission" against consumer expectations.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/12/ftc-tim-cook-apple-news-censorship.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/02/12/ftc-tim-cook-apple-news-censorship.html))
