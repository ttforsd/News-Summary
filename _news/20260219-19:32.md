## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Tensions Intensify Amid Nuclear Talks and Military Buildup.**

    *   The US has surged military forces, including two aircraft carriers (USS Abraham Lincoln, USS Gerald R Ford), to the Middle East.

    *   US officials are reportedly considering a "massive" military campaign against Iran if diplomacy fails.

## Major regional Events

*   **UN Fact-Finding Mission Reports "Hallmarks of Genocide" in Sudan.**
    *   A UN mission found evidence pointing to genocide by the paramilitary Rapid Support Forces (RSF) in El-Fasher, Darfur.
    *   The report cited prolonged siege, starvation, mass killings, rape, torture, and destruction of Zaghawa and Fur communities.
    *   The UK Foreign Secretary pledged to take the findings to the UN Security Council, calling for accountability and an end to arms flows.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpqw74d81jqo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpqw74d81jqo))

*   **South Korea's Former President Yoon Suk Yeol Jailed for Life Over Martial Law Attempt.**
    *   Yoon Suk Yeol was sentenced to life imprisonment for leading an "insurrection" by attempting to impose military rule in December 2024.
    *   The court found his actions fundamentally damaged South Korea's democracy.
    *   Several other top officials, including the former defense minister, also received lengthy prison sentences.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx28y8xd1vjo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx28y8xd1vjo)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/19/former-south-korean-president-yoon-suk-yeol-gets-life-sentence-over-martial-law-attempt.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/19/south-korea-ex-president-yoon-suk-yeol-life-sentence-guilty-insurrection-death-sentence-martial-law-verdict.html))

*   **Gaza Relief Efforts Begin Amidst Hamas Reassertion of Control.**
    *   Trump's "Board of Peace" announced over $7 billion in pledges for Gaza relief and reconstruction.
    *   Israel's Prime Minister reiterated that reconstruction depends on Hamas' disarmament.
    *   Hamas is reasserting control over security, tax revenue, and government services in Gaza, despite heavy losses in the war.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp8rke05kv4o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp8rke05kv4o)), ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c98g1klxnpxo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c98g1klxnpxo))

*   **Russia-Ukraine Peace Talks Stalled, Starlink Cutoff Impacts Russian Forces.**

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **US Trade Deficit Remains High Despite Tariff Policies.**
    *   The US trade deficit totaled $901.5 billion in 2025, a minimal change from the previous year.
    *   This indicates President Trump's aggressive tariffs did little to close the overall trade gap.
    *   Largest deficits were recorded with the European Union, China, and Mexico.
