## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US Tariffs Policy Shift**
    *   The Supreme Court struck down President Trump's sweeping global tariffs (6-3 decision).
    *   Court ruled President exceeded powers under the International Emergency Economic Powers Act (IEEPA).
    *   US may now owe an estimated $175 billion in refunds to importers.
    *   Trump immediately imposed new, temporary global tariffs, raising them from 10% to 15%.
    *   These new tariffs, under Section 122 of the Trade Act, apply broadly including to the UK, India, and EU.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/21/trump-to-hike-global-tariffs-to-15-from-10-effective-immediately.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/21/trump-to-hike-global-tariffs-to-15-from-10-effective-immediately.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/supreme-court-trump-tariffs-ruling.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/supreme-court-trump-tariffs-ruling.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/supreme-court-trump-tariffs-us-refunds.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/supreme-court-trump-tariffs-us-refunds.html))
*   **US-Iran Tensions Escalating**
    *   President Trump is considering a limited military strike on Iran over its nuclear program.
    *   Decision expected in "10 days" amidst a significant US military buildup in the Middle East.
    *   Ongoing talks in Switzerland show progress but US core demands remain unmet.
    *   Oil prices remained stable despite strike considerations.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdxzk4gvzp0o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdxzk4gvzp0o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/trump-says-hes-considering-limited-military-strike-against-iran.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/trump-says-hes-considering-limited-military-strike-against-iran.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/oil-prices-stable-as-trump-considers-limited-military-strike-against-iran.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/oil-prices-trump-us-iran-conflict-strikes-energy.html))

## Major Regional Events

*   **Iran Anti-Government Protests Resume**
    *   Students at several universities, including Sharif University, staged large-scale anti-government rallies.
    *   Protesters chanted against Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei.
    *   These are the first major demonstrations since a deadly crackdown last month that reportedly killed 6,159.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yj2kzkrj0o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yj2kzkrj0o))
*   **Israel-Lebanon Tensions Intensify**
    *   Israeli air strikes killed at least 10 people in eastern Lebanon's Bekaa Valley.
    *   Eight of the dead were confirmed Hezbollah members, including a senior commander.
    *   Israel stated targets were Hezbollah command centers, while a Hamas center in Ain al-Helweh camp was also hit.
    *   Lebanon's PM warns of "one-sided war of attrition" despite a November 2024 ceasefire.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvg8914dkl0o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvg8914dkl0o))
*   **West Bank Violence and US Citizen Death**
    *   A 19-year-old Palestinian-American man, Nasrallah Abu Siyam, was shot dead by an Israeli settler near Ramallah.
    *   Family demands accountability; US expects "full, thorough, and transparent investigation."
    *   This marks the sixth American citizen killed by Israeli settlers or soldiers in the West Bank in two years.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgk7z15gnpo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgk7z15gnpo))
*   **Venezuelan Political Prisoners Freed**
    *   More than 1,500 political prisoners have applied for amnesty under a new law, with hundreds already released.
    *   The US had urged Venezuela to expedite the release of political prisoners.
    *   The new law is criticized for excluding individuals who advocated for foreign intervention.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c93w9g2dvn5o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c93w9g2dvn5o))
*   **French Political Landscape Shift**
    *   The killing of nationalist student Quentin Deranque by suspected far-left militants in Lyon sparks political fallout.
    *   Condemnation targets the radical-left La France Insoumise (LFI) party, potentially "de-demonising" the far-right National Rally (RN).
    *   This shift could have significant implications for upcoming municipal elections and the 2027 national elections.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cnv61zv534eo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cnv61zv534eo))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **US GDP Growth Slows Unexpectedly**
    *   Fourth-quarter US GDP increased by only 1.4% (annualized rate), falling short of the 2.5% estimate.
    *   This slowdown was driven by weaker consumer spending and a sharp decline in government spending due to the shutdown.
    *   The core personal consumption expenditures (PCE) price index remained firm at 3% in December.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/pce-inflation-december-2025.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/pce-inflation-december-2025.html))
*   **Private Credit Market Jitters**
    *   Blue Owl Capital restricted investor withdrawals from one of its retail-focused debt funds after selling $1.4 billion in loan assets.
    *   This move intensified fears about liquidity and a potential "canary in the coal mine" scenario for the private credit market.
    *   Concerns focus on risks from illiquid loans, aggressive debt structures, and investor demands.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/canary-in-the-coal-mine-blue-owl-liquidity-curbs-fuel-fears-private-credit-bubble-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/canary-in-the-coal-mine-blue-owl-liquidity-curbs-fuel-fears-private-credit-bubble-.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/illiquid-loans-investor-demands-blue-owls-software-lending-triggers-another-quake-in-private-credit.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/20/illiquid-loans-investor-demands-blue-owls-software-lending-triggers-another-quake-in-private-credit.html))
*   **Berkshire Hathaway's Q4 Portfolio Adjustments**
    *   Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway was a net seller of stocks in his final quarter as CEO.
    *   The firm significantly reduced its stakes in Apple, Bank of America, and Amazon.com.
    *   Key purchases included increased positions in Chevron and Chubb, and a new, smaller stake in The New York Times Company.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/21/berkshire-was-a-net-seller-of-stocks-in-buffetts-final-quarter-as-ceo.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/21/berkshire-was-a-net-seller-of-stocks-in-buffetts-final-quarter-as-ceo.html))
