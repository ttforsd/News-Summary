## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **US Government Blacklists Anthropic AI, OpenAI Secures Pentagon Deal:**
    *   President Trump ordered federal agencies to cease using Anthropic's AI technology.
    *   Anthropic refused to grant the US military unrestricted access to its AI tools, citing ethical concerns over autonomous weapons and mass surveillance.
    *   The Defense Secretary designated Anthropic a "supply chain risk" for national security.
    *   Meanwhile, OpenAI reached an agreement with the Pentagon to deploy its models in classified networks.
    *   OpenAI's deal includes safeguards against mass surveillance and fully autonomous weapons.
