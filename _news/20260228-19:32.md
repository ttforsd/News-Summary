## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **US Government Blacklists Anthropic, OpenAI Secures Pentagon Deal:**
    *   President Trump ordered all federal agencies to immediately stop using AI developer Anthropic's technology.
    *   This follows Anthropic's refusal to grant the Pentagon "unfettered access" to its AI tools, citing concerns over "mass surveillance" and "fully autonomous weapons."
    *   Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth designated Anthropic a "supply chain risk to national security."
    *   Hours later, OpenAI CEO Sam Altman announced a deal with the Pentagon to deploy its models in their classified network, with agreed-upon restrictions on autonomous weapons and mass domestic surveillance.
    *   Anthropic plans to challenge its designation in court.
    *   *Sources:* ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn48jj3y8ezo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn48jj3y8ezo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/openai-strikes-deal-with-pentagon-hours-after-rival-anthropic-was-blacklisted-by-trump.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/openai-strikes-deal-with-pentagon-hours-after-rival-anthropic-was-blacklisted-by-trump.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/26/anthropic-pentagon-ai-amodei.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/26/anthropic-pentagon-ai-amodei.html))
