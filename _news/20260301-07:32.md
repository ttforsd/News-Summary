## Executive summary

*   **Iran-Israel-US Conflict Escalates:** Iran's Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei was killed in US and Israeli air strikes on Tehran, confirmed by Iranian state TV, triggering widespread retaliatory attacks by Iran across the Middle East.
*   **Regional Retaliation & Casualties:** Iran launched missiles and drones at Israel and US military bases in Bahrain, Kuwait, Qatar, and the UAE, hitting targets including a Dubai hotel and causing casualties in multiple locations.
*   **Global Market Jitters:** The conflict raises significant concerns about global oil supply disruptions through the Strait of Hormuz, potentially leading to a recession, causing markets to brace for instability.
*   **AI Policy Showdown in US:** President Trump blacklisted AI developer Anthropic for refusing military access to its tools for surveillance and autonomous weapons, while rival OpenAI secured a deal with the Pentagon under strict safeguards.
*   **AI's Impact on Employment:** Tech company Block announced layoffs of nearly half its workforce (4,000 employees) due to AI-driven efficiency gains, signaling a potential trend of widespread job displacement across industries.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Israel Strikes and Iranian Supreme Leader's Death:**
    *   Ayatollah Ali Khamenei was killed in US and Israeli air strikes in Iran, confirmed by Iranian state TV.
    *   Impact: Ends his three-decade rule, creating uncertainty for Iran and the wider region.
    *   Succession: Iran's constitution tasks the Assembly of Experts (88 senior clerics) with choosing a new Supreme Leader.

    *   UN Secretary General condemned the military escalation, warning of risks to global peace.
    *   US and Israeli Ambassadors defended their actions at the UN Security Council.
    *   Russia condemned the strikes; Oman (a mediator) expressed dismay, urging US de-escalation.
    *   EU leaders called for a negotiated solution and distanced themselves from the strikes.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yj9kr31y7o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yj9kr31y7o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/28/tehran-strikes-back-at-gulf-states-after-us-israel-launch-massive-attack-on-iran.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/28/tehran-strikes-back-at-gulf-states-after-us-israel-launch-massive-attack-on-iran.html))

    *   Trump indicated willingness for military action if Iran doesn't agree to a nuclear deal, despite no final decision.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/28/tehran-strikes-back-at-gulf-states-after-us-israel-launch-massive-attack-on-iran.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/28/tehran-strikes-back-at-gulf-states-after-us-israel-launch-massive-attack-on-iran.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/trump-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/trump-iran-war.html))
