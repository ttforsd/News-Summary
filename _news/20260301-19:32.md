## Executive summary

*   **Middle East Conflict Intensifies:** Iran's Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei was killed in joint US-Israeli strikes, triggering widespread retaliatory missile and drone attacks by Iran across Israel and Gulf states. Civilian casualties are high in Iran.
*   **Global Oil Markets on Alert:** Crude oil prices are expected to surge as fears of prolonged disruptions to Strait of Hormuz shipping escalate, with tanker traffic already halted.
*   **AI Policy Shift in US:** The Trump administration blacklisted Anthropic AI for refusing military unrestricted access to its tools, while OpenAI simultaneously struck a deal with the Pentagon, agreeing to safeguards.
*   **Smartphone Market Downturn:** The global smartphone market faces a projected "sharpest decline on record" in 2026 due to a severe memory chip shortage and rising prices.
*   **India's Economic Growth & AI Investment:** India reported stronger-than-expected GDP growth and announced significant investment in AI infrastructure with Nvidia's support.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Iran-US/Israel Conflict Escalates Dramatically**
    *   Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei was killed in coordinated US-Israeli airstrikes across Iran.
    *   US President Trump stated the objective is "eliminating imminent threats" and encouraging "regime change" in Iran.
    *   Iran confirmed Khamenei's death and announced Alireza Arafi as interim Supreme Leader, with the Assembly of Experts to determine succession.
    *   **Impact:** The strikes aimed to cripple Iran's command structure; internal power shifts are expected.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2lgvg5rl1o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2lgvg5rl1o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2dyz6p3weo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2dyz6p3weo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c86y5540vnno](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c86y5540vnno), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3gdnpjvj4ko](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3gdnpjvj4ko), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/iran-khamenei-dead-us-israel-strike-trump-netanyahu.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/iran-khamenei-dead-us-israel-strike-trump-netanyahu.html))
*   **US-Iran Nuclear Talks Collapse Amid Rising Tensions**
    *   The latest "most intense" round of US-Iran nuclear talks concluded with no deal.
    *   US demanded destruction of Iran's main nuclear sites and a permanent end to enrichment; Iran rejected these terms.
    *   **Impact:** Failure of diplomacy suggests continued military pressure, increasing regional instability.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/us-iran-nuclear-talks-oil-middle-east.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/us-iran-nuclear-talks-oil-middle-east.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/trump-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/trump-iran-war.html))
*   **Cuba Faces Intensified US Economic Pressure**
    *   The Trump administration has escalated its economic embargo on Cuba, threatening tariffs on nations supplying oil to the island.
    *   **Impact:** This policy, aiming for regime change, is causing severe humanitarian crisis and fuel scarcity in Cuba.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9q5wdeg4j8o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9q5wdeg4j8o))

## Major regional Events

*   **Iran Strikes Back Across Middle East**
    *   Iran launched missile and drone attacks on Israel, killing 9 and injuring 27 in Beit Shemesh.
    *   Retaliatory strikes hit US military bases and assets in Gulf states: UAE (1 dead, 7 injured, airports damaged), Kuwait (1 dead, 32 injured, airport hit), Bahrain (US Fifth Fleet HQ hit, airport damaged), Qatar, Jordan, and Oman.
    *   **Impact:** Widespread civilian casualties and damage, dramatically widening the conflict's geographic footprint.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c363zkp1pgxo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c363zkp1pgxo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2dyz6p3weo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2dyz6p3weo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/28/trump-us-military-iran-strikes-middle-east-oil.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/28/trump-us-military-iran-strikes-middle-east-oil.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/us-israel-attacks-strikes-iran-retaliate-china-russia-allies.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/us-israel-attacks-strikes-iran-retaliate-china-russia-allies.html))

    *   **Impact:** Iran blames US and Israel for civilian casualties, raising humanitarian concerns.

    *   Cross-border violence escalated with Pakistani airstrikes on militant hideouts in Afghanistan.
    *   Afghan Taliban retaliated with ground operations and drone attacks.
    *   **Impact:** Trade between the two nations is closed, affecting regional stability and humanitarian situation.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil Markets Brace for Significant Volatility**
    *   Crude oil prices are anticipated to jump sharply as markets reopen due to Middle East conflict.
    *   **Impact:** Fears of a prolonged disruption to the Strait of Hormuz (a critical shipping choke point for 1/3 of seaborne oil) could push Brent crude above $100/barrel. Tanker traffic is already halted.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/crude-oil-futures-iran.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/crude-oil-futures-iran.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/experts-weigh-potential-scenarios-for-oil-if-strait-of-hormuz-closes.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/experts-weigh-potential-scenarios-for-oil-if-strait-of-hormuz-closes.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/28/iran-us-attack-oil-market-economy.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/28/iran-us-attack-oil-market-economy.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/us-israel-attacks-strikes-iran-retaliate-china-russia-allies.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/us-israel-attacks-strikes-iran-retaliate-china-russia-allies.html))
*   **Global Travel Disruptions**
    *   Thousands of flights to and from the Middle East have been cancelled or rerouted due to airspace closures.
    *   **Impact:** Major disruptions for international travelers, affecting hubs like Dubai, Tel Aviv, and Doha.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c363zkp1pgxo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c363zkp1pgxo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/travel-iran-attack-middle-east-flights.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/travel-iran-attack-middle-east-flights.html))
*   **US Inflation Higher Than Expected**
    *   US core producer price index (PPI) rose 0.8% in January, exceeding the 0.3% forecast.
    *   **Impact:** Suggests inflation pressures persist, potentially influencing the Federal Reserve's interest rate decisions.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/ppi-january-2026-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/ppi-january-2026-.html))
*   **UBS Downgrades US Stock Market Outlook**
    *   UBS downgraded US equities to "benchmark," citing concerns over a weakening dollar, stretched valuations, and policy turbulence.
    *   **Impact:** Indicates a potential shift of capital towards foreign markets due to better valuations and a weaker dollar.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/ubs-downgrades-the-us-stock-market-heres-what-has-the-investment-bank-worried.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/ubs-downgrades-the-us-stock-market-heres-what-has-the-investment-bank-worried.html))
*   **India's Robust Economic Growth**
    *   India's economy grew 7.8% in the December quarter, surpassing expectations, with full-year 2026 growth projected at 7.6%.
    *   **Impact:** Driven by strong performance in manufacturing, private consumption, and fixed capital formation.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/india-gdp-december-quarter-economy.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/india-gdp-december-quarter-economy.html))
*   **Norway's Sovereign Wealth Fund Reports Strong Gains**
    *   The world's largest sovereign wealth fund (Norway's $2 trillion oil fund) posted a 15% annual return ($248 billion profit).
    *   **Impact:** Gains were driven by global equities, particularly US technology and financials, and renewables infrastructure investments.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/norway-sovereign-wealth-fund.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/norway-sovereign-wealth-fund.html))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **US Government AI Policy and Industry Shake-up**
    *   President Trump ordered federal agencies to stop using Anthropic AI, labeling it a "supply chain risk."
    *   **Context:** Anthropic refused to grant the US military unrestricted access to its AI tools, citing concerns about mass surveillance and autonomous weapons.
    *   OpenAI simultaneously announced a deal with the Pentagon to deploy its models on classified networks.
    *   **Context:** OpenAI agreed to specific safeguards against domestic mass surveillance and autonomous weapons use.
    *   **Impact:** Highlights growing tensions between AI companies' ethical stances and government demands for military applications.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn48jj3y8ezo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn48jj3y8ezo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/openai-strikes-deal-with-pentagon-hours-after-rival-anthropic-was-blacklisted-by-trump.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/openai-strikes-deal-with-pentagon-hours-after-rival-anthropic-was-blacklisted-by-trump.html))
*   **Smartphone Market Faces Record Decline**
    *   The global smartphone market is projected to see its "sharpest decline on record" in 2026 (12-13% drop).
    *   **Impact:** Driven by a severe memory chip shortage, with supply redirected to AI data centers, leading to higher device prices and longer replacement cycles.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/smartphone-market-poised-for-sharpest-decline-on-record-in-2026-according-to-reports-memory-chip-data-center-ai-samsung-apple-google-meta.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/smartphone-market-poised-for-sharpest-decline-on-record-in-2026-according-to-reports-memory-chip-data-center-ai-samsung-apple-google-meta.html))
*   **AI Spurs Tech Layoffs and Infrastructure Investment**
    *   Block (Jack Dorsey's payments company) announced nearly 50% workforce reduction (4,000 jobs) linked to AI adoption.
    *   **Impact:** CEO Dorsey expects widespread industry layoffs as companies embrace "intelligence tools" for efficiency.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/block-layoffs-ai-jack-dorsey-jobs.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/block-layoffs-ai-jack-dorsey-jobs.html))
    *   India's Yotta Data Services is building a $2 billion AI hub with Nvidia chips, planning an IPO within 12 months.
    *   **Impact:** India's data center capacity is projected to double by 2028, positioning it as a key AI partner.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/india-yotta-nvidia-ai-gpu-demand-data-centers-investment.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/27/india-yotta-nvidia-ai-gpu-demand-data-centers-investment.html))
*   **WhatsApp Privacy Policy Challenged in India**
    *   India's Supreme Court is scrutinizing WhatsApp's 2021 privacy policy.
    *   **Impact:** WhatsApp will allow Indian users to opt out of sharing data with Meta for advertising by March 16.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp81wegj123o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp81wegj123o))
*   **Chinese Tech Advances in Robotics**
    *   Honor showcased a "Robot Phone" with a robotic camera arm and teased a humanoid robot.
    *   **Impact:** Reflects a broader trend of Chinese electronics companies expanding into robotics development.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/honor-robot-phone-magic-v6-foldable-launch-mwc.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/01/honor-robot-phone-magic-v6-foldable-launch-mwc.html))
