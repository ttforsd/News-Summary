## Executive summary

*   **Middle East conflict intensifies:** US-Israeli strikes killed Iran's Supreme Leader, triggering widespread Iranian retaliation across Israel and Gulf states.
*   **Global oil markets volatile:** Oil prices surged, and shipping through the Strait of Hormuz faced disruptions, leading to fears of a 1970s-style energy shock.
*   **Flight disruptions widespread:** Thousands of flights to/from the Middle East were canceled due to airspace closures and security concerns.
*   **North Korea succession debate:** Kim Jong Un's daughter, Ju Ae, is increasingly seen as a potential heir, sparking internal debate.
*   **African Sahel security worsening:** Kidnappings of foreigners, especially Chinese workers, significantly increased in Mali and Niger by an al-Qaeda affiliate.
*   **US inflation concerns rise:** Wholesale prices in the US increased more than expected in January, suggesting persistent inflation.
