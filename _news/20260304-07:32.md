## Major regional Events

*   **Iran Conflict: Death of Supreme Leader and Humanitarian Concerns**

    *   A transitional leadership council is formed; the Assembly of Experts will select a new leader.
    *   Tehran residents are stocking up on essentials amid ongoing strikes and rising food prices.
    *   Iran has banned the export of all food and agriculture products; internet blackouts persist.

## Global Economic & Market Movers
