## Executive summary

*   **Middle East Conflict Intensifies:** Mojtaba Khamenei named Iran's new Supreme Leader as US and Israel launch fresh strikes on central Iran and oil facilities.
*   **Global Energy Crisis:** Oil prices spiked significantly, with the Strait of Hormuz effectively closed; G7 nations are discussing strategic oil reserve releases.
*   **Economic Volatility:** European and Asian markets closed lower; South Korea imposed a fuel price cap. China reports rising inflation but is well-positioned for oil shocks.
*   **AI Sector Under Scrutiny:** Anthropic sues the US government over a Pentagon blacklist, while prediction markets face backlash for Iran war wagers.
*   **European Security Shifts:** Croatia reinstates compulsory military service, and debates over UK military bases in Cyprus intensify after a drone strike.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Iran's New Supreme Leader and Escalating Conflict:**
    *   Mojtaba Khamenei, son of the late Ayatollah Ali Khamenei (killed in US-Israeli strikes), has been chosen as Iran's new Supreme Leader.
    *   He is expected to continue hardline policies, with Israel declaring any successor a potential target.
    *   The US has ordered non-essential diplomatic staff to evacuate Saudi Arabia as the conflict broadens.
    *   Iran's Foreign Ministry warns oil tankers to be "very careful" traversing the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c78xxg05w0zo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c78xxg05w0zo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/iran-war-oil-tankers-strait-of-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/iran-war-oil-tankers-strait-of-hormuz.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/iran-war-updates-oil-brent-wti-crude-110-gulf-gcc-uae-iraq-saudi-arabia-kurds.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/iran-war-updates-oil-brent-wti-crude-110-gulf-gcc-uae-iraq-saudi-arabia-kurds.html))
*   **India's Role in Iranian Naval Activity:**
    *   India granted sanctuary to an Iranian warship, Iris Lavan, after Iran sought permission for three vessels to dock.
    *   This occurred just days before another Iranian ship, Iris Dena, was sunk by a US submarine off Sri Lanka.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2e4yxj0pd3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2e4yxj0pd3o))
*   **US-China Diplomatic Preparations:**
    *   China's top diplomat confirmed preparations for a late March meeting between Presidents Trump and Xi Jinping.
    *   China urged de-escalation in the Iran conflict and warned against protectionist trade policies.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/08/china-says-thorough-preparations-needed-ahead-of-trump-xi-meeting.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/08/china-says-thorough-preparations-needed-ahead-of-trump-xi-meeting.html))
*   **UN Humanitarian Concerns in Middle East:**
    *   The UN humanitarian chief warned of "grave peril" for civilians due to the Iran war.
    *   Concerns were raised about rising extremism and regional polarization, urging immediate de-escalation.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c9wnevqljd0o](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c9wnevqljd0o))

## Major regional Events

*   **Europe's Shifting Security Landscape:**
    *   Croatia reinstated compulsory military service for teenagers, with training including drone and cyberwarfare skills.
    *   This move is driven by the proximity of the Ukraine war and regional instability, prompting similar considerations in Serbia.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c93j2l32lzgo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c93j2l32lzgo))
*   **Cyprus Debates UK Military Bases:**
    *   A drone strike on the UK's RAF Akrotiri in Cyprus reignited debate over British military presence.
    *   Some Cypriots view the bases as a security risk, especially after reports of US spy plane use.
    *   Protesters demand removal and transparency, while the UK asserts the bases are vital for regional safety.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2r1xldl1ko](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2r1xldl1ko))
*   **Guinea's Political Crackdown:**
    *   Guinea's opposition leader called for "direct resistance" after authorities dissolved 40 political parties.
    *   President Doumbouya is accused of suppressing democratic freedoms ahead of legislative elections.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjrqg80y57po](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjrqg80y57po))
*   **Nepal's Election Upset:**
    *   Rapper-turned-politician Balendra Shah unseated Nepal's former Prime Minister.
    *   His party is now poised for an election victory, reflecting a youth-led push for change.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c93w6y0lnd9o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c93w6y0lnd9o))
*   **Istanbul Mayor Faces Political Trial:**
    *   Istanbul Mayor Ekrem Imamoglu is on trial for mass corruption, facing a potential 2,430-year jail term.
    *   His opposition party and rights groups allege the trial is politically motivated by President Erdogan.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c204ymjnn80o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c204ymjnn80o))
*   **Oslo US Embassy Explosion Investigation:**
    *   Norwegian police released images of a suspect linked to an explosion at the US embassy in Oslo.
    *   The blast was caused by an improvised device, with terrorism being a leading hypothesis.
    *   Investigators are reviewing a deleted video reportedly featuring Iran's assassinated Supreme Leader.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce8wk563473o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce8wk563473o))

    *   Iran is experiencing a "regime-imposed national internet blackout" extending into its second week.
    *   The outage isolates the public while officials retain access, contributing to the "fog of war."

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Energy Market Crisis from Iran War:**
    *   Oil prices surged to nearly $120 a barrel before moderating, but remain above $100.
    *   The Strait of Hormuz closure represents the largest oil supply disruption in history.
    *   Kuwait, Iraq, and the UAE are cutting oil production due to the impassable Strait.
    *   The Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) market is also highly vulnerable, with Qatar halting output.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/the-us-iran-war-is-the-biggest-oil-supply-disruption-in-history.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/the-us-iran-war-is-the-biggest-oil-supply-disruption-in-history.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/08/crude-oil-prices-today-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/08/crude-oil-prices-today-iran-war.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/theres-another-energy-market-that-may-get-hit-harder-than-oil-by-strait-of-hormuz-closure.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/theres-another-energy-market-that-may-get-hit-harder-than-oil-by-strait-of-hormuz-closure.html))
*   **G7 Response to Energy Supply Risks:**
    *   G7 nations are ready to take "necessary measures" to support global energy supplies.
    *   Finance ministers and the IEA discussed options for stabilizing oil prices but held back on releasing strategic crude reserves.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c79542n0grwo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c79542n0grwo))
*   **Central Bank and Market Impacts:**
    *   The Iran war has likely postponed anticipated Bank of England interest rate cuts.
    *   Rising energy prices are expected to push up consumer bills after July in the UK.
    *   US 10-year Treasury yields saw limited change amidst inflation and recession concerns.
    *   European and Asian stock markets (e.g., Kospi, Nikkei 225) closed lower, experiencing high volatility.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/iran-war-bank-of-england-march-april-rate-cut-inflation-interest-rates.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/iran-war-bank-of-england-march-april-rate-cut-inflation-interest-rates.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/us-treasury-yields-investors-monitor-iran-war-and-soaring-oil-price.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/us-treasury-yields-investors-monitor-iran-war-and-soaring-oil-price.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/european-markets-stoxx-600-ftse-cac-dax-iran-latest.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/european-markets-stoxx-600-ftse-cac-dax-iran-latest.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/06/kospi-south-koreas-stock-market-volatility.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/06/kospi-south-koreas-stock-market-volatility.html))
*   **South Korea's Fuel Price Cap:**
    *   South Korea imposed a fuel price cap for the first time in 30 years due to surging oil prices.
    *   The government pledged to expand its market stabilization program and diversify energy import sources.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/south-korea-fuel-price-cap-oil-price-surging.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/south-korea-fuel-price-cap-oil-price-surging.html))
*   **China's Economic Outlook:**
    *   China's consumer inflation hit a three-year high in February (1.3%), boosted by holiday spending.
    *   The country set its lowest GDP growth target in decades (4.5-5%) for 2026, citing global uncertainty and domestic challenges.
    *   Despite tensions, China is relatively well-positioned for oil shocks due to large reserves and diversified energy.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/china-february-inflation-cpi-producer-prices-stimulus-economy-ppi-factory-consumer.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/china-february-inflation-cpi-producer-prices-stimulus-economy-ppi-factory-consumer.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/06/china-economy-gdp-growth-target-lowest-in-decades-tariffs-deflation-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/06/china-economy-gdp-growth-target-lowest-in-decades-tariffs-deflation-.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/china-oil-shock-iran-war-hormuz-energy-transition.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/china-oil-shock-iran-war-hormuz-energy-transition.html))
*   **US Democrats Challenge Russian Oil Sales:**
    *   Congressional Democrats are demanding the reversal of Russian oil sales to India.
    *   A temporary waiver by the Trump administration to ease global oil prices is criticized for aiding Russia.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/bessent-russian-oil-sales-india.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/bessent-russian-oil-sales-india.html))
*   **West African Cocoa Farmers in Crisis:**
    *   Cocoa farmers in Ghana and Ivory Coast face severe financial hardship, unpaid for months.
    *   State-set prices for cocoa beans are much higher than plummeting international market rates.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c93jdk1yy3zo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c93jdk1yy3zo))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **AI Firm Sues US Government:**
    *   AI developer Anthropic is suing the US government after the Pentagon blacklisted it as a "supply chain risk."
    *   The lawsuit alleges "unprecedented" actions jeopardizing revenue and reputation.
    *   The dispute stems from disagreements over military use of Anthropic's AI models, though they are reportedly still used in the Iran operation.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/anthropic-trump-claude-ai-supply-chain-risk.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/anthropic-trump-claude-ai-supply-chain-risk.html))
*   **AI Data Center Startup Secures Major Funding:**
    *   UK-based AI data center startup Nscale raised $2 billion at a $14.6 billion valuation, with Nvidia's backing.
    *   The company is rapidly expanding its AI infrastructure and cloud computing services.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/nscale-ai-data-center-nvidia-raise.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/nscale-ai-data-center-nvidia-raise.html))
*   **Autonomous Vehicle Expansion:**
    *   Amazon's Zoox is expanding its robotaxi testing to Dallas and Phoenix.
    *   The expansion aims to test autonomous technology in diverse weather and urban environments.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/amazon-zoox-robotaxi-testing-phoenix-dallas.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/amazon-zoox-robotaxi-testing-phoenix-dallas.html))

    *   Prediction markets are under fire for allowing wagers related to the Iran war, including nuclear detonation timing.

*   **Chinese EV Makers' Cost Advantage:**
    *   Chinese EV manufacturers, like BYD, gain a significant cost edge over Western rivals through extensive vertical integration.
    *   This strategy, producing up to 80% of core components in-house, leads to higher profit margins and lower prices.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/06/western-automakers-outsourced-supply-chains-for-decades-now-chinese-rivals-have-the-cost-edge.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/06/western-automakers-outsourced-supply-chains-for-decades-now-chinese-rivals-have-the-cost-edge.html))
