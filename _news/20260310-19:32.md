## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Conflict & Diplomacy:**
    *   President Trump gave mixed messages on the Iran war, claiming it is "very complete" but also vowing "to go further."
    *   Defense Secretary Hegseth announced Tuesday would be the "most intense day of strikes" on Iran.
    *   Objectives include destroying missile capabilities, the Iranian Navy, and denying nuclear weapons.
    *   Russia denied sharing intelligence with Iran during calls with President Trump, contradicting US official reports.

    *   Lebanese President Joseph Aoun criticized Hezbollah for acting against national interests.
    *   Israel has shown little interest in negotiations, reiterating calls for Hezbollah disarmament.

## Major regional Events

*   **Iran Conflict Escalation:**
    *   Israeli and US attacks continued across Iran for over 10 days, with explosions rocking Tehran and Karaj.
    *   Strikes caused power cuts and widespread fear among residents; 1,761 people reportedly killed.
    *   Satellite images confirmed ongoing fires at oil facilities, leading to "black rain" and "unprecedented" pollution in Tehran.
    *   WHO expressed "great concerns" over severe health impacts from contaminated air and water.
    *   Video analysis confirmed a US Tomahawk missile hit a military base near an Iranian primary school, where 168 people died.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cev7llekwllo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cev7llekwllo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqxd1nv3re2o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqxd1nv3re2o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvg548lyjnyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvg548lyjnyo))
*   **Beirut Under Attack:**
    *   An Israeli strike hit a Ramada Plaza hotel in central Beirut, killing four alleged Iranian diplomats or Quds Force operatives.
    *   This marked the first time the bombing campaign reached the city center, escalating regional tensions.
    *   The conflict has displaced nearly 700,000 people in Lebanon; 486 killed by Israeli attacks.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2k8y7vy78no](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2k8y7vy78no))
*   **Iranian Leadership Transition:**
    *   Mojtaba Khamenei, 56, was named Iran's new Supreme Leader following his father's assassination.
    *   His appointment signals continued hardline governance, with close ties to the IRGC.
    *   US President Trump had previously declared Mojtaba "unacceptable" as a successor.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwykdn0yn4wo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwykdn0yn4wo))
*   **Attacks on US Diplomatic Posts:**
    *   Canada police are investigating shots fired at the US consulate in Toronto, calling it a "national security incident."
    *   This incident follows other recent attacks on Jewish synagogues and an Iranian-Canadian critic's gym in Toronto.
    *   Norwegian police are also investigating an explosion outside the US embassy in Oslo, exploring potential links to the Middle East conflict.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy8lrq9rg2o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy8lrq9rg2o))
*   **Madagascar Political Turmoil:**
    *   Madagascar's military ruler, Colonel Michael Randrianirina, unexpectedly dissolved the government.
    *   The Prime Minister and entire cabinet were dismissed without an immediate reason.
    *   Randrianirina seized power in October following youth-led protests over power and water shortages.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c39wywm298lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c39wywm298lo))
*   **Cuba Fuel Crisis:**
    *   President Trump reiterated his threat of a "friendly takeover" of Cuba amidst its deepening fuel crisis.
    *   The US previously cut Cuba off from Venezuelan oil, exacerbating economic woes and rationing.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/iran-war-cuba-crisis-fuel-energy-trump-us-havana.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/iran-war-cuba-crisis-fuel-energy-trump-us-havana.html))
*   **India's Diplomatic Balance:**
    *   India faces a difficult diplomatic balancing act due to the Iran war's impact on oil supplies.
    *   China urges stronger BRICS cooperation, while US offers waivers for Russian oil purchases.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/china-iran-oil-crisis-brics-diplomacy-us-china.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/india-iran-oil-crisis-brics-diplomacy-us-china.html))
