## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Iran-US-Israel Conflict Continues**
    *   Israel launched an air strike in central Beirut, killing five alleged Iranian Quds Force operatives.
        *   Impact: Marks a significant expansion of operations into urban areas, raising regional escalation concerns.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2k8y7vy78no](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2k8y7vy78no))
    *   US-Israeli attacks continue in Iran, with a US Tomahawk missile confirmed to have hit a military base near an Iranian primary school.
        *   Details: 168 people, including 110 children, were reportedly killed near the Shajareh Tayebeh primary school in Minab.
        *   Impact: Raises questions about civilian casualties and US operational intent; Iran blames US and Israel.

        *   Details: US forces reportedly sank 16 Iranian minelayers near the Strait of Hormuz.
        *   Impact: Contradictory messaging adds to market volatility and geopolitical uncertainty.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c93j2dw72q9o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c93j2dw72q9o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/11/us-strikes-iran-mine-ships-strait-of-hormuz-war-oil.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/11/us-strikes-iran-mine-ships-strait-of-hormuz-war-oil.html))
    *   Iran defends its strikes on Gulf neighbors, claiming US military assets in the region are "legitimate targets."
        *   Impact: Gulf states express a "huge trust gap" and condemn Iranian actions, fearing prolonged diplomatic strain.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/iran-strikes-gulf-neighbors-us-assets-oil-infrastructure.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/iran-strikes-gulf-neighbors-us-assets-oil-infrastructure.html))
    *   Russia denies sharing intelligence with Iran regarding the conflict, as stated by President Putin to President Trump.
        *   Impact: Russia seeks to project itself as a potential mediator, aiming for diplomatic and economic gains.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gjyg0djvmo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gjyg0djvmo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/iran-war-trump-russia-intelligence-witkoff.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/iran-war-trump-russia-intelligence-witkoff.html))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil Market Volatility and Supply Concerns**
    *   Oil prices remain volatile, with Brent crude futures hovering below $90/barrel.
        *   Details: Prices surged to nearly $120/barrel due to Middle East conflict fears but retreated on prospects of emergency reserve releases.
        *   Impact: Global markets are on edge as the IEA considers a historic release of strategic oil reserves.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/11/oil-price-today-crude-wti-us-iran-war-strait-hormuz-iea.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/11/oil-price-today-crude-wti-us-iran-war-strait-hormuz-iea.html))

        *   Impact: Highlights the fragility of global oil supply chains and potential for prolonged economic disruption.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/saudi-aramco-2025-earnings-q4-profit-beat-oil-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/iran-war-threatens-catastrophic-consequences-for-the-oil-market-aramco-ceo-says.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/cgrzk874171o](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/cgrzk874171o))

        *   Details: China is reportedly stockpiling oil, with Iranian crude shipments significantly lower than pre-war levels.
        *   Impact: China's energy security strategy is tested by Middle East tensions; India's economic balancing act.

        *   Details: India, a major LPG importer, prioritizes household supply, threatening closure of many businesses.
        *   Impact: Demonstrates widespread economic ripple effects of regional conflicts on essential goods and services.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/-iran-war-lpg-disruption-india-restaurants.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/10/-iran-war-lpg-disruption-india-restaurants.html))
