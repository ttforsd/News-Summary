## Executive summary

*   The **Middle East conflict** continues to escalate, with US forces striking Iranian military targets on Kharg Island and Iran threatening further retaliation, impacting Gulf states and global oil shipping.
*   **Global energy markets** remain highly volatile, with Brent crude prices holding above $100/barrel despite the largest-ever strategic oil release and a temporary US waiver on Russian oil.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Energy Market Volatility & Oil Prices:**
    *   Brent crude oil prices closed above $100 per barrel for a second consecutive day, with market skepticism about measures to reduce prices.
        *   Context: This surge occurred despite the IEA's record release of 400 million barrels from emergency stockpiles and a temporary US waiver on Russian oil purchases.
        *   Impact: Analysts warn the supply gap (9M bpd due to Strait closure) dwarfs release capacity (1.4M bpd), forecasting Brent prices up to $135/barrel.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/13/oil-100-price-brent-wti-trump-iran-war-surrender-khamenei.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/13/oil-100-price-brent-wti-trump-iran-war-surrender-khamenei.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/14/the-biggest-release-of-emergency-oil-stockpiles-in-history-was-announced-why-crude-may-keep-rising.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/14/the-biggest-release-of-emergency-oil-stockpiles-in-history-was-announced-why-crude-may-keep-rising.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1d6l67122wo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1d6l67122wo))
    *   Saudi Arabia's East-West pipeline and the UAE's ADCOP are being utilized to bypass the Strait of Hormuz, with capacities of 7M bpd and 1.5-1.8M bpd respectively.
        *   Context: These pipelines partially offset the Strait's closure, but regional conflict risks infrastructure damage, as seen with the Ruwais refinery closure.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/12/strait-of-hormuz-oil-pipelines-iran-war-saudi-arabia-uae.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/12/strait-of-hormuz-oil-pipelines-iran-war-saudi-arabia-uae.html))
    *   India's Prime Minister Modi initiated talks with Iran due to severe energy crunch fears, with 28 Indian ships and 800 seafarers stranded in the Strait.
        *   Impact: India, reliant on the Strait for 50% of its crude and most LPG, faces potential inflation spikes and has increased Russian oil imports.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/13/indias-modi-reaches-out-to-iran-as-energy-crunch-fears-grip-the-south-asian-nation-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/13/indias-modi-reaches-out-to-iran-as-energy-crunch-fears-grip-the-south-asian-nation-.html))
    *   Gold prices remain largely unmoved by the Iran conflict, despite its traditional safe-haven status.
        *   Context: Factors include a stronger dollar, higher Treasury yields, and resurging inflation fears, although long-term forecasts remain bullish.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/12/gold-iran-conflict-where-next-markets.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/12/gold-iran-conflict-where-next-markets.html))
