## Major regional Events

*   **Iran-Israel Conflict Escalates:**
    *   Iran's security chief Ali Larijani and Basij force head Gholamreza Soleimani were killed in Israeli air strikes.
    *   Iran confirmed the deaths and launched retaliatory missile and drone attacks on Israeli and US assets in the Middle East.
    *   Tehran residents report severe repression, security checkpoints, and internet restrictions amidst escalating airstrikes.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil Market Volatility and Prices:**
    *   Oil prices remain high, hovering around $100 a barrel, due to Iran war disruptions in the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   US gas prices surged, posing a political challenge for President Trump.
    *   The volatility has attracted record retail investment in oil-linked ETFs, leading to "meme-style" trading.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70k29914q4o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70k29914q4o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/17/oil-prices-brent-wti-uae-energy-attacks-us-crude-inventories-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/17/oil-prices-brent-wti-uae-energy-infrastructure-hormuz.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/16/oil-crude-iran-war-retailers-meme.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/16/oil-crude-iran-war-retailers-meme.html))
*   **Shipping Bottlenecks in Strait of Hormuz:**
    *   Iran's de facto blockade has reduced shipping traffic to a trickle, with few non-Iranian oil cargoes passing through.
    *   China, India, Greece, Pakistan, and Turkey have seen some vessels transit under negotiated safe passages.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/hormuz-bottleneck-vessel-tanker-tracker-shipping-strait-of-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/hormuz-bottleneck-vessel-tanker-tracker-shipping-strait-of-hormuz.html))
*   **Asian Countries Conserve Fuel:**
    *   Sri Lanka declared Wednesdays a public holiday to conserve fuel; other Asian nations adopted similar austerity measures.
    *   India's piped natural gas supply is vulnerable, with half its LNG imports from Qatar relying on the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g5n58rlnzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g5n58rlnzo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj6dl175w01o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj6dl175w01o))
*   **Global Hedge Funds Suffer Losses:**
    *   Hedge funds recorded their worst losses since "Liberation Day" (Trump's 2025 tariff announcement) due to Iran war turmoil.
    *   Equity and emerging market exposures, and bets against the US dollar, have unwound rapidly.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/hedge-funds-suffer-worst-losses-since-liberation-day-on-iran-war-turmoil.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/hedge-funds-suffer-worst-losses-since-liberation-day-on-iran-war-turmoil.html))
*   **Central Bank Decisions Loom:**
    *   The US Federal Reserve is expected to keep interest rates unchanged, with markets focused on Chair Powell's guidance on future policy.
    *   European markets anticipate higher open, awaiting EU inflation data and upcoming monetary policy decisions from ECB, BoE, and others.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/european-markets-stoxx-600-ftse-dax-cac-iran-news-oil-prices.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/european-markets-stoxx-600-ftse-dax-cac-iran-news-oil-prices.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/17/the-fed-issues-its-latest-interest-rate-decision-wednesday-heres-what-to-expect.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/17/the-fed-issues-its-latest-interest-rate-decision-wednesday-heres-what-to-expect.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/17/treasury-yields-middle-east-tensions-fed-decision.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/17/treasury-yields-middle-east-tensions-fed-decision.html))
