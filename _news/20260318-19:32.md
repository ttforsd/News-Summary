## Executive summary

*   **Middle East Escalation:** Israel claimed strikes killed multiple senior Iranian officials, including the Intelligence Minister. Iran retaliated with drone and missile attacks on Israel and US assets, causing casualties and targeting energy infrastructure.
*   **Global Energy Disruption:** Oil prices surged towards $110 a barrel following attacks on Iranian gas fields and threats to Gulf energy facilities. The Strait of Hormuz remains largely disrupted, prompting the US to waive the Jones Act for domestic shipping.
*   **US Economic Outlook:** The Federal Reserve held interest rates steady, noting "uncertain" impacts from the Iran war and higher-than-expected wholesale inflation in February.
*   **AI Developments:** Nvidia's CEO lauded OpenClaw as "the next ChatGPT," boosting Chinese AI stocks. Meta is shifting focus from its metaverse VR platform, Horizon Worlds, to prioritize AI.
*   **Humanitarian Concerns:** A Pakistani air strike in Kabul killed over 100 at a drug rehab center. An oil spill blamed on a Russian strike in Ukraine has cut water supply in Moldova, and a Russian tanker carrying LNG is adrift in the Mediterranean, posing environmental risks.
