## Executive summary
*   Middle East conflict escalated with Israeli strikes on Iran's gas field, prompting Iranian retaliation against Qatar's LNG facilities.
*   Oil prices surged above $110/barrel; Strait of Hormuz remains disrupted, causing global energy supply concerns.
*   US Federal Reserve and Bank of Japan held interest rates steady amid inflation fears fueled by the conflict.
*   Israel assassinated Iran's Intelligence Minister Esmail Khatib, the third high-ranking official killed in two days.
*   European chip buyers face increased costs and delays as the Iran war impacts air freight routes.
*   China actively promotes widespread AI adoption and develops its data center infrastructure.

    *   President Trump threatened to "massively blow up" Iran's South Pars gas field if attacks on Qatar's energy facilities continue.
    *   Trump denied prior knowledge of Israel's initial strike on Iran's gas field.
    *   US Director of National Intelligence stated Iran's regime is "intact but largely degraded" by recent attacks.

## Major regional Events

*   **Middle East Conflict Escalation**
    *   Israel confirmed killing Iran's Intelligence Minister Esmail Khatib in an air strike.
    *   This marks the third high-ranking Iranian official assassinated in two days.
    *   Iran's President Pezeshkian condemned the assassinations as "cowardly."
    *   Iran's Parliament Speaker stated an "eye for an eye" approach is now active.
    *   Tehran residents describe heightened fear and total repression under current conditions.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2d1dz14k51o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2d1dz14k51o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/iran-war-intelligence-esmail-khatib-israel-middle-east-crisis.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/iran-war-intelligence-esmail-khatib-israel-middle-east-crisis.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn9e0wrglgdo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn9e0wrglgdo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/cnbc-daily-open-iran-vows-eye-for-eye-in-energy-attack-escalation.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/cnbc-daily-open-iran-vows-eye-for-eye-in-energy-attack-escalation.html))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Energy Market Volatility**
    *   Oil prices (Brent crude) surged over 4% to $112/barrel after strikes on energy facilities.
    *   Israel attacked Iran's South Pars gas field; Iran retaliated by hitting Qatar's Ras Laffan LNG terminal.
    *   The Strait of Hormuz blockade severely limits global oil and gas shipments, with only trickle traffic.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c78x83lpgngo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c78x83lpgngo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/oil-jumps-iran-strikes-qatar-lng-facility-supply-worries.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/oil-jumps-iran-strikes-qatar-lng-facility-supply-worries.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/hormuz-bottleneck-vessel-tanker-tracker-shipping-strait-of-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/hormuz-bottleneck-vessel-tanker-tracker-shipping-strait-of-hormuz.html))
*   **Central Bank Decisions**
    *   The US Federal Reserve held benchmark interest rates steady (3.5%-3.75%).
    *   Fed cited "uncertain" impacts from the Iran war and higher-than-expected inflation.
    *   The Bank of Japan kept rates at 0.75%, warning of upward inflation pressure from crude oil prices.
    *   European Central Bank, Bank of England, and others are also expected to hold rates steady.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c36364d06x3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c36364d06x3o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/fed-interest-rate-decision-march-2026.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/fed-interest-rate-decision-march-2026.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/bank-of-japan-keeps-rates-steady-as-expected-warns-iran-war-may-push-up-inflation.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/bank-of-japan-keeps-rates-steady-as-expected-warns-iran-war-may-push-up-inflation.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/europe-s-central-banks-are-no-longer-in-a-good-place-as-iran-war-upends-forecasts.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/europe-s-central-banks-are-no-longer-in-a-good-place-as-iran-war-upends-forecasts.html))
*   **Global Supply Chain Impacts**
    *   European chip buyers face higher costs and delays as the Iran war disrupts Middle East air freight.
    *   Global air freight capacity for high-value electronics is down approximately 9%.
    *   Qatar's disrupted LNG facilities threaten helium supply, crucial for semiconductor manufacturing.
    *   Aluminum prices surged to near 4-year highs due to Middle East supply disruptions.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/europe-chip-buyers-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/europe-chip-buyers-iran-war.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/tech-stocks-qatar-attack-semiconductor-supply-chain-lng-helium.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/tech-stocks-qatar-attack-semiconductor-supply-chain-lng-helium.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/why-aluminum-surged-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/why-aluminum-surged-iran-war.html))
*   **US Economic Measures & Data**
    *   President Trump temporarily waived the Jones Act for 60 days to stabilize oil markets.
    *   This allows non-US vessels to carry fuel between US ports.
    *   US wholesale prices (PPI) rose 0.7% in February, higher than expected.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/trump-jones-act-oil-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/trump-jones-act-oil-iran-war.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/ppi-inflation-february-2026.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/ppi-inflation-february-2026.html))
*   **Asian Market Performance & China's Energy Resilience**
    *   Asia-Pacific markets dipped, tracking Wall Street losses amid energy worries.
    *   South Korean Kospi and Japan's Nikkei saw significant declines in tech stocks.
    *   China, the world's largest oil importer, is better prepared due to large reserves and renewables.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/asia-markets-live-boj-nikkei-kospi-hang-seng.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/asia-markets-live-boj-nikkei-kospi-hang-seng.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/tech-stocks-qatar-attack-semiconductor-supply-chain-lng-helium.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/tech-stocks-qatar-attack-semiconductor-supply-chain-lng-helium.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cyv9lzn0816o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cyv9lzn0816o))
