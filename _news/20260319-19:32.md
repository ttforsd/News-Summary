## International Relations & Diplomacy

    *   US President Trump publicly claimed no prior knowledge of an Israeli strike on Iran's South Pars gas field. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8x7leknlywo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8x7leknlywo))

(Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/oil-jumps-iran-strikes-qatar-lng-facility-supply-worries.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/oil-jumps-iran-strikes-qatar-lng-facility-supply-worries.html))
