## Executive summary

*   **Middle East Conflict Escalates with Economic Fallout:** Israel and Iran's mutual strikes on energy infrastructure continue, causing significant disruptions to global oil and gas markets, including the Strait of Hormuz.
*   **Geopolitical Tensions Resurface:** Denmark reportedly planned defensive measures against a potential US invasion of Greenland, highlighting underlying allied strains.
*   **Global Economic Instability:** The Iran conflict is slowing India's IPO market and creating inflationary pressures in Japan, while European central banks hold rates due to the uncertain outlook.
*   **Supply Chain Disruptions:** The war threatens global helium supply, crucial for semiconductors, and impacts air freight for European chip buyers.
*   **AI and Tech Developments:** Alibaba significantly reduced its workforce amid a pivot to AI, and US prosecutors charged employees for smuggling Nvidia chips to China.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil and Gas Market Volatility:**
    *   Dubai crude oil prices hit a record $166/barrel, Brent touched $119, WTI around $94.
    *   Cause: Iranian attacks on energy infrastructure and Strait of Hormuz closure.
    *   Impact: Citi and Nomura cut India's Nifty 50 target; Saudi officials foresee $180+ oil if disruptions persist.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/166-a-barrel-middle-east-oil-gives-clue-to-where-all-prices-could-be-headed-if-iran-war-drags-on.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/166-a-barrel-middle-east-oil-gives-clue-to-where-all-prices-could-be-headed-if-iran-war-drags-on.html)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/oil-jumps-iran-strikes-qatar-lng-facility-supply-worries.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/oil-jumps-iran-strikes-qatar-lng-facility-supply-worries.html)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/iran-war-dampen-most-active-ipo-market.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/iran-war-dampen-most-active-ipo-market.html))
*   **Central Banks Hold Rates Amidst Uncertainty:**
    *   European Central Bank, Bank of England, Swiss National Bank, and Sweden's Riksbank kept interest rates steady.
    *   Rationale: War in Iran creates "significantly more uncertain" outlook for inflation and growth.
    *   Impact: Market predictions now lean towards potential rate hikes later in 2026; bond yields soar.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/ecb-boe-swiss-national-bank-riksbank-interest-rate-decisions.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/ecb-boe-swiss-national-bank-riksbank-interest-rate-decisions.html)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/government-bonds-face-perfect-storm-as-iran-war-rattles-europes-central-banks.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/government-bonds-face-perfect-storm-as-iran-war-rattles-europes-central-banks.html))
*   **India's IPO Market Slows:**
    *   Payments app PhonePe halted listing plans; other tech startups defer IPOs.
    *   Cause: Investor appetite weakens due to Iran war fallout, slowing economic growth, and poor recent returns.
    *   Impact: Indian benchmark indices dropped over 12% since January; liquidity drained.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/iran-war-dampen-most-active-ipo-market.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/iran-war-dampen-most-active-ipo-market.html))
*   **Japan's Inflation Concerns:**
    *   Iran conflict risks "cost-push" inflation from higher energy costs, not desired wage-driven increases.
    *   BOJ's dilemma: Raise rates to curb inflation or hold to sustain growth.
    *   Impact: Oil price spikes could add 0.3%-0.7% to CPI.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/iran-war-japan-inflation-boj-oil-prices.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/iran-war-japan-inflation-boj-oil-prices.html))
*   **Fertilizer Shortage Threatens US Farm States:**
    *   Strait of Hormuz shutdown is driving up fertilizer prices ahead of planting season.
    *   Impact: Looming shortage threatens crop yields, potentially raising food prices, and impacting midterm elections.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/iran-war-induced-fertilizer-shortage-threatens-republicans-in-farm-states-ahead-of-midterms.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/iran-war-induced-fertilizer-shortage-threatens-republicans-in-farm-states-ahead-of-midterms.html))
*   **Gold and Silver Sell-off:**
    *   Gold shed 5%, silver 10% amidst inflation fears and broad market risk-off sentiment.
    *   Impact: Reflects investors liquidating perceived safe-haven assets and heightened volatility.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/gold-and-silver-sell-off-as-inflation-fears-grip-global-markets.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/gold-and-silver-sell-off-as-inflation-fears-grip-global-markets.html))
*   **India's Gold Loan Market Booming:**
    *   Gold loans surged, driven by tighter banking rules for unsecured lending and higher bullion prices.
    *   Investment: Global PE firm Bain Capital acquired a 41.7% stake in Manappuram Finance.
    *   Impact: Gold-backed lending is India's fastest-growing retail credit category, indicating financial stress for some.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/gold-loan-demand-rises-india.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/gold-loan-demand-rises-india.html))
