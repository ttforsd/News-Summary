## Executive summary

*   **Middle East Tensions Escalate:** Iran launched ballistic missiles near Israeli nuclear sites, injuring 180, and targeted a British-American base in the Indian Ocean.
*   **US Diplomatic Posture:** President Trump issued threats of striking Iranian power plants and invoked emergency powers for $23 billion in arms sales to Gulf allies.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Iran-Israel Conflict Escalation**
    *   Iranian ballistic missiles injured 180 people in Arad and Dimona, Israel, near a nuclear facility.
    *   This was in response to an alleged attack on Iran's Natanz nuclear facility.
    *   Israel reports intercepting 92% of 400 missiles fired by Iran since February 28.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9qdvnv13qdo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9qdvnv13qdo))
*   **Iranian Missile Threat to British-American Base**
    *   Iran targeted the joint British-American Diego Garcia military base with two ballistic missiles.
    *   One missile failed, and the other was intercepted, according to UK officials.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yljdgwppzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yljdgwppzo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/22/uk-confirms-iran-fired-two-missiles-at-british-american-base.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/22/uk-confirms-iran-fired-two-missiles-at-british-american-base.html))
*   **US Strategy and Threats in Middle East**
    *   President Trump threatened to "obliterate" Iranian power plants if the Strait of Hormuz is not fully reopened within 48 hours.
    *   Iran countered, threatening to target regional energy facilities if its power plants are hit.
    *   Trump indicated the US might "wind down" military operations but stressed other nations should police the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   The US administration approved $23 billion in emergency arms sales to UAE, Kuwait, and Jordan to bolster defenses.
    *   Trump also cited Pearl Harbor in front of the Japanese PM to defend US attack secrecy, seeking allied support for the Strait.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/21/trump-threatens-attack-on-iran-power-plants-if-strait-of-hormuz-isnt-reopened.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/21/trump-threatens-attack-on-iran-power-plants-if-strait-of-hormuz-isnt-reopened.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/trump-says-he-doesnt-want-iran-war-ceasefire-but-is-considering-winding-down-military-ops.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/trump-says-he-doesnt-want-iran-war-ceasefire-but-is-considering-winding-down-military-ops.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/trump-invokes-emergency-powers-with-23-billion-in-gulf-arms-sales-as-iran-war-wages-on-wsj-report.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/trump-invokes-emergency-powers-with-23-billion-in-gulf-arms-sales-as-iran-war-wages-on-wsj-report.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/trump-pearl-harbor-japan-takaichi-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/trump-pearl-harbor-japan-takaichi-iran-war.html))
*   **Ukraine War Developments**
    *   Russian attacks killed six people across four Ukrainian regions in the last 24 hours.
    *   Ukraine and US negotiators are discussing how to end Russia's invasion, including eased US sanctions on Russian oil.
    *   Kenya will grant amnesty to its citizens who illegally fought for Russia in Ukraine.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/crl18nwjzzzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/crl18nwjzzzo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj94jmj2p28o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj94jmj2p28o))
*   **US-Cuba Relations Worsen**
    *   Cuba experienced a national power grid collapse for the second time in a week, affecting over 10 million.
    *   This crisis is exacerbated by the ongoing US fuel blockade and aging infrastructure.
    *   The US Treasury prohibited Cuba from receiving Russian crude, despite two tankers reportedly heading to the island.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvg0l44yxrvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvg0l44yxrvo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/us-says-cuba-is-prohibited-from-taking-russian-oil-as-two-tankers-head-to-island.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/us-says-cuba-is-prohibited-from-taking-russian-oil-as-two-tankers-head-to-island.html))

## Major regional Events

*   **West Bank Settler Attacks**
    *   Extremist Israeli settlers attacked Palestinian villages in the occupied West Bank, burning homes and properties.
    *   The violence followed the death of a teenage settler.
    *   The UN reports six Palestinians killed by settlers since March 1.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cew719w80g7o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cew719w80g7o))
*   **Sudan Hospital Attack**
    *   A drone attack on el-Daein Teaching Hospital in Sudan killed 64 people, including 13 children, two nurses, and a doctor.
    *   The RSF blames the army; the army denies responsibility, urging an independent investigation.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cr715xynkvvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cr715xynkvvo))
*   **Qatar Military Helicopter Crash**
    *   A military helicopter crashed in Qatari territorial waters during a training exercise, killing all seven on board.
    *   Victims included four Qatari military personnel, one Turkish serviceman, and two Turkish defense technicians.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2v4k0xn9no](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2v4k0xn9no))
*   **French Mayoral Elections**
    *   France held mayoral elections, with significant focus on Paris possibly shifting to the right.
    *   Hard-right parties are poised for victory in Nice.
    *   Left-wing alliances face criticism, particularly for alleged antisemitism.

*   **Hawaii Faces Severe Flooding**
    *   Thousands were evacuated in Hawaii due to severe storms and flash floods, marking the worst flooding in 20 years.
    *   Parts of the state received 40-50 inches of rain in 10 days; more rain is expected.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c20qj970gwro](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c20qj970gwro))
*   **Nigeria Conflict Continues**
    *   Deadly ethnic and religious violence persists in Nigeria's Plateau state between Christian farmers and Muslim Fulani herders.
    *   Lack of security and justice perpetuates a cycle of reprisals, hindering peace efforts.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clyv1j8ne7ko](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clyv1j8ne7ko))
*   **Canada Secularism Law Heads to Supreme Court**
    *   Quebec's secularism law, banning religious attire for some public workers, faces a Supreme Court constitutional challenge.
    *   Muslim women are particularly affected, with many considering leaving the province.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ceqw5j5lx32o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ceqw5j5lx32o))
*   **Italy Constitutional Referendum**
    *   Italy is voting on judicial reforms that would separate career paths for judges and prosecutors, and create new disciplinary bodies.
    *   The referendum is seen by many as a de facto vote on PM Giorgia Meloni's government.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm29n1lvp64o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm29n1lvp64o))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Central Banks and Inflation Outlook**
    *   European Central Bank (ECB) and Bank of England (BoE) held interest rates steady.
    *   However, major banks now forecast up to three ECB rate hikes this year due to inflation risks from the Iran conflict.
    *   UK government borrowing costs hit their highest level since 2008 (10-year gilts at 5%) on inflation fears.
    *   US Treasury yields climbed as investors anticipate Fed rate cuts are off the table due to Middle East war-driven inflation.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/interest-rates-bonds-bank-of-england-european-central-bank-investors-iran-war-ecb-inflation.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/19/interest-rates-bonds-bank-of-england-european-central-bank-investors-iran-war-ecb-inflation.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/uk-gilt-market-interest-rates-boe-inflation-reeves.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/uk-gilt-market-interest-rates-boe-inflation-reeves.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/banks-eye-three-ecb-rate-hikes-this-year.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/banks-eye-three-ecb-rate-hikes-this-year.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/us-treasury-yields-edge-higher-as-iran-war-drives-inflation-pressure.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/us-treasury-yields-edge-higher-as-iran-war-drives-inflation-pressure.html))
*   **Commodity Market Volatility**
    *   Gold prices dropped nearly 10% this week, marking its worst weekly rout since 2011, influenced by the Iran war's economic implications.
    *   Silver futures also tumbled over 14% this week.
    *   Oil prices remain elevated, surging over 40% since the Iran war began on February 28.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/gold-silver-price-metal-market-oil-brent-wti-futures-iran-war-inflation.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/gold-silver-price-metal-market-oil-brent-wti-futures-iran-war-inflation.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/gold-drops-nearly-10-in-worst-weekly-rout-since-2011.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/gold-drops-nearly-10-in-worst-weekly-rout-since-2011.html))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **OpenAI Strategic Shifts**
    *   OpenAI is pivoting its data center strategy, opting to procure cloud capacity from partners rather than building massive centers itself, ahead of a potential IPO.
    *   This shift reflects tempering of ambitious growth plans due to spending concerns.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/22/openai-data-center-pivot-underscores-wall-street-ipo-concerns.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/22/openai-data-center-pivot-underscores-wall-street-ipo-concerns.html))
    *   OpenAI is moving away from its "Instant Checkout" feature in ChatGPT for online shopping, towards dedicated retailer apps.
    *   This acknowledges challenges in direct transaction enablement, focusing instead on product discovery.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/openai-agentic-shopping-etsy-shopify-walmart-amazon.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/open-ai-agentic-shopping-etsy-shopify-walmart-amazon.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/openai-desktop-super-app-chatgpt-browser-codex.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/openai-desktop-super-app-chatgpt-browser-codex.html))
*   **AI Infrastructure & Labor Market**
    *   The boom in AI data center construction is creating a new, lucrative career path for skilled trade workers like electricians and technicians.
    *   However, a severe scarcity of this specialized talent is a major constraint on global tech growth.
    *   Nvidia's CEO Jensen Huang proposed offering "AI tokens" on top of salary to engineers, envisioning AI agents outnumbering human workers.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/ai-data-center-buildout-jobs-salary-skilled-traders-worker-shortage.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/18/ai-data-center-buildout-jobs-salary-skilled-traders-worker-shortage.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/nvidia-ai-agents-tokens-human-workers-engineer-jobs-unemployment-jensen-huang.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/20/nvidia-ai-agents-tokens-human-workers-engineer-jobs-unemployment-jensen-huang.html))
*   **Space Economy Investment**
    *   Low Earth Orbit (LEO) is attracting billions in investment for satellites and space data centers from companies like SpaceX, Nvidia, and Amazon.
    *   This rapid expansion is outpacing current regulatory frameworks.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/22/why-low-earth-orbit-is-attracting-billions-in-investment.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/22/why-low-earth-orbit-is-attracting-billions-in-investment.html))
*   **US National AI Policy**
