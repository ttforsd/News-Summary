## Executive Summary

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Tensions and Strait of Hormuz Ultimatum**
    *   US President Trump issued a 48-hour ultimatum for Iran to fully reopen the Strait of Hormuz or face strikes on its power plants.
    *   Iran retaliated, threatening to target regional energy infrastructure, desalination facilities, and financial entities holding US Treasury bonds if attacked.
    *   The conflict has closed the Strait of Hormuz to most shipping, causing global oil price volatility and energy crisis fears.
    *   UK confirmed Iran fired two ballistic missiles at the joint British-American Diego Garcia base in the Indian Ocean, which failed to reach their target.
