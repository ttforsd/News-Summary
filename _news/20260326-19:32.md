## Executive summary

*   **Global Economy Slowdown:** The OECD revised global growth forecasts downwards due to the Iran war, with the UK facing the largest economic hit among developed nations.
*   **US-Iran Tensions Escalating:** Diplomatic efforts are mired in conflicting reports on peace talks, with President Trump suggesting Iran offered oil access, while Iran denies direct talks and Israel announces killing an Iranian naval chief.
*   **Social Media Accountability:** A US jury delivered a landmark verdict finding Meta and Google liable for intentionally creating addictive platforms that harmed child mental health.
*   **Olympic Transgender Policy Shift:** From 2028, women's Olympic sports will be exclusively for biological females, determined by an SRY gene test.
*   **US-China Summit Confirmed:** President Trump and President Xi Jinping will meet in Beijing in May, a meeting delayed due to Middle East events.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Conflict and Diplomatic Confusion:**
    *   President Trump claimed Iran permitted 10 oil tankers through the Strait of Hormuz as a goodwill gesture.
    *   Trump asserted "very substantial talks" with Iran are underway via Pakistan.
    *   Iran's foreign minister denies direct negotiations with the US, despite reviewing an American peace proposal.
    *   Iran reportedly rejected a US ceasefire, offering its own terms including sovereignty over the Strait of Hormuz and war reparations.
    *   **Impact:** The US is deploying thousands more troops to the Middle East, seen as increased leverage.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/trump-iran-war-oil-strait-of-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/trump-iran-war-oil-strait-of-hormuz.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/trump-iran-negotiations.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/trump-iran-negotiations.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/25/iran-war-us-trump.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/25/iran-war-us-trump.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/what-comes-next-three-attack-scenarios-as-us-sends-thousands-more-troops-to-middle-east.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/what-comes-next-three-attack-scenarios-as-us-sends-thousands-more-troops-to-middle-east.html))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Iran War's Global Economic Impact:**
    *   The OECD forecasted a significant economic hit from the Iran war, downgrading global growth and raising inflation projections.
    *   The UK is expected to be particularly vulnerable, with projected 4% inflation and 0.5% GDP growth for 2026.
    *   US inflation is also forecast to hit 4.2% this year, higher than the Fed's estimate.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/uk-could-be-worse-hit-from-iran-war-of-all-major-economies-oecd.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/uk-could-be-worse-hit-from-iran-war-of-all-major-economies-oecd.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/global-forecasting-group-sees-us-inflation-at-4point2percent-this-year-much-higher-than-fed-estimate.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/global-forecasting-group-sees-us-inflation-at-4point2percent-this-year-much-higher-than-fed-estimate.html))
    *   Retailers like Next and H&M warned of potential price increases if the Iran war prolongs.
    *   Rising fuel and freight costs are impacting supply chains globally.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/retail-next-hm-stock-price-hikes-iran-war-middle-east-costs.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/retail-firms-warn-of-price-hikes-if-iran-war-extends-for-months.html))
    *   The oil market is in "backwardation," suggesting traders anticipate a short-lived surge in prices, though some fear underpricing risks.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/market-trends-oil-futures-backwardation.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/the-oil-market-is-in-backwardation-heres-what-that-means-for-energy-prices.html))
    *   Goldman Sachs warned that the Iran war's fallout would be long-lasting due to infrastructure damage, even with a quick resolution.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/25/goldman-sachs-lloyd-blankfein-iran-war-oil-price-spike-energy-crisis-resolution.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/25/goldman-sachs-lloyd-blankfein-iran-war-fallout-is-going-to-last-even-if-theres-a-resolution-tomorrow.html))
    *   Higher US gas prices, driven by the Iran conflict, could offset the positive impact of larger tax refunds.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/high-gas-prices-iran-war-tax-refunds.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/higher-gas-prices-from-iran-war-could-offset-bigger-tax-refunds-from-trumps-big-beautiful-bill.html))
*   **Philippines Energy Crisis:**
    *   The Philippines declared a national energy emergency due to severe fuel price surges and supply disruptions from the Iran war.
    *   Transport workers in Manila initiated a two-day strike, demanding fuel tax reductions and wage increases.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4glxn24gd6o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4glxn24gd6o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq8wkq1n9epo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq8wkq1n9epo))
*   **Strait of Hormuz Toll Proposal:**
    *   Iran is reportedly drafting legislation to charge tolls for safe passage through the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   This move aims to formalize Iranian control over the critical global shipping route.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/iran-plans-tolls-on-ships-passing-through-strait-of-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/iran-plans-tolls-on-ships-passing-through-strait-of-hormuz.html))
*   **Aviation Industry Fuel Cost Measures:**
    *   British Airways plans to incentivize pilots with bonuses for reducing aircraft fuel consumption, effective next year.
    *   This initiative reflects wider airline struggles with soaring jet fuel prices due to the Middle East conflict.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/british-airways-reward-pilots-cutting-fuel-as-airlines-tackle-higher-costs.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/british-airways-to-reward-pilots-for-cutting-fuel-as-airlines-tackle-higher-costs.html))
    *   The Middle East conflict has severely disrupted global aviation, necessitating flight reroutes and causing long-term concerns for the "Gulf model" of air travel.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn08x9lw0pzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn08x9lw0pzo))
*   **Global Fertilizer Supply Chain Disruption:**
    *   The blockage of the Strait of Hormuz is causing significant disruption to the global fertilizer market.
    *   Approximately one-third of the world's seaborne fertilizer trade passes through the strait, leading to price spikes and warnings of food insecurity.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/25/fertilizer-price-iran-war-food-security-inflation-urea-potash-nitrogen-farmers.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/25/its-not-just-oil-and-gas-the-strait-of-hormuz-blockage-is-rattling-another-vital-commodity.html))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Olympic Women's Sport Eligibility:**
    *   The International Olympic Committee announced that women's categories in Olympic sports will be limited to biological females from 2028.
    *   Eligibility will be determined by a "once-in-a-lifetime" SRY gene test, excluding transgender women who have undergone male puberty.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.bbc.com/sport/olympics/articles/cdj7dgvlj0no](https://www.bbc.com/sport/olympics/articles/cdj7dgvlj0no))
*   **Social Media Addiction Liability:**
    *   A US jury ruled Meta (Instagram) and Google (YouTube) liable for intentionally designing addictive platforms.
    *   The verdict awarded $6 million in damages to a young woman who suffered mental health issues, with both companies planning appeals.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c87wd0d84jqo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c87wd0d84jqo))
*   **Meta's AI Data Center Expansion:**
    *   Meta increased its investment in an AI data center in West Texas to $10 billion, a sixfold increase.
    *   The facility aims for 1 gigawatt capacity by 2028 and will create 300 new jobs.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/meta-to-spend-10-billion-on-ai-data-center-in-el-paso-1gw-by-2028.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/meta-boosts-investment-in-west-texas-ai-data-center-by-over-sixfold-to-10-billion.html))
*   **Google AI Memory Efficiency:**
    *   Google's new "TurboQuant" compression method could reduce memory requirements for large language models by six times.
    *   This breakthrough is causing pressure on memory chip stocks, as investors fear reduced demand.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/google-ai-turboquant-memory-chip-stocks-samsung-micron.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/a-google-ai-breakthrough-is-pressuring-memory-chip-stocks-from-samsung-to-micron.html))
*   **Apple's US Manufacturing Program:**
    *   Apple expanded its American Manufacturing Program with a $400 million investment and four new partners.
    *   The initiative aims to bolster domestic production of key components and materials for Apple products.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/apple-american-manufacturing-program-trump.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/apple-expands-american-manufacturing-program-with-four-new-partners.html))
*   **GPS Interference in Persian Gulf:**
    *   Significant interference with GPS and other location-based services is disrupting navigation in the Persian Gulf.
    *   This electronic warfare is a defensive tactic against drones and missiles, but poses risks to civilian vessels and aircraft.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/electronic-warfare-in-the-persian-gulf-how-gps-interference-is-disrupting-the-middle-east.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/electronic-warfare-in-the-persian-gulf-how-gps-interference-is-disrupting-the-middle-east.html))
*   **India's Transgender Rights Bill:**
    *   India's parliament passed an amendment bill altering the legal recognition of transgender people, leading to protests.
    *   Critics argue the bill undermines the right to self-identification and could exclude many non-binary individuals.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czx9de4ygl7o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czx9de4ygl7o))
