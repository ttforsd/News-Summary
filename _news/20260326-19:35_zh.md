## 全球經濟與市場動態

*   **能源市場與通脹壓力**
    *   伊朗戰爭對全球經濟造成衝擊，經合組織（OECD）預計英國將是主要經濟體中受影響最嚴重的國家之一。
        *   經合組織將英國2026年通脹預期上調至4%，而增長預期則降至0.5%。
        *   英國對全球能源價格衝擊的敏感度高於許多其他國家。
        *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/iran-war-will-spare-no-major-economy-says-oecd--but-the-uk-is-more-vulnerable-than-others.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/iran-war-will-spare-no-major-economy-says-oecd--but-the-uk-is-more-vulnerable-than-others.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/global-forecasting-group-sees-us-inflation-at-4point2percent-this-year-much-higher-than-fed-estimate.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/global-forecasting-group-sees-us-inflation-at-4point2percent-this-year-much-higher-than-fed-estimate.html))

        *   能源基礎設施的損壞將導致持續的壓力。
        *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/25/goldman-sachs-lloyd-blankfein-iran-war-fallout-is-going-to-last-even-if-theres-a-resolution-tomorrow.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/25/goldman-sachs-lloyd-blankfein-iran-war-fallout-is-going-to-last-even-if-theres-a-resolution-tomorrow.html))

        *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/retail-next-hm-stock-price-hikes-iran-war-middle-east-costs.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/26/retail-next-hm-stock-price-hikes-iran-war-middle-east-costs.html))

    *   石油巨頭警告，若伊朗戰爭持續，全球將面臨能源短缺。
