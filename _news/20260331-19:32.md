## Major regional Events

*   **Israel-Lebanon Border Tensions**
    *   Israel announced plans to establish a security buffer zone in southern Lebanon, extending up to the Litani River.
    *   Detail: This involves maintaining security control and demolishing homes in Lebanese border villages.
    *   Impact: Lebanon, the UN, EU nations, and Canada have strongly criticized these plans as a severe violation of Lebanese sovereignty.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yx8knpr5no](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yx8knpr5no))
*   **Israel Approves Death Penalty for Terror Attacks**
    *   Israel's parliament has passed a law allowing the death penalty for Palestinians convicted of deadly terror attacks in military courts.
    *   Impact: This legislation has drawn strong condemnation from the UN Human Rights Chief, European countries, the Palestinian Authority, and Amnesty International, who call it discriminatory and a potential war crime.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp8dkd6lnjdo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp8dkd6lnjdo))
*   **Iran War Military Operations**
    *   US President Trump is reportedly considering a military operation to seize Iran's Kharg Island, a crucial oil export terminal.
    *   Detail: This move would aim to pressure Iran over its control of the Strait of Hormuz, but carries high risks and potential casualties.
    *   The UK's RAF jets are conducting defensive missions from Cyprus against Iranian drones.
    *   Detail: The RAF Akrotiri base in Cyprus was previously hit by a drone, prompting strengthened air defenses with additional assets.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c937gd1vq7xo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c937gd1vq7xo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdrmg6x2rgxo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdrmg6x2rgxo))
*   **Iran's Alleged Use of Child Soldiers**
    *   Reports and eyewitness accounts claim Iran is deploying children, some as young as 12, in security roles during the war.
    *   Detail: An 11-year-old boy was reportedly killed while manning a checkpoint in Tehran.
    *   Impact: Human Rights Watch condemns this as a grave violation of children's rights and a war crime, indicating regime desperation.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9wqgjn7x89o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9wqgjn7x89o))
*   **Escalating Gang Violence in Haiti**
    *   Recent attacks by the Gran Grif gang in rural Jean-Denis, Haiti, have killed at least 16 people and displaced 6,000.
    *   Impact: This intensifies concerns about widespread violence and shifting allegiances among local vigilante groups, ahead of a new UN-backed police force deployment in April.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cql76l4221qo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cql76l4221qo))
*   **Widespread Sexual Violence in Sudan**
    *   Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) reports that sexual violence is an "everyday" reality in parts of Sudan, including areas not in active conflict.
    *   Detail: The assaults are overwhelmingly perpetrated by armed men, often with extreme brutality, systematically targeting non-Arab communities.
    *   Impact: MSF emphasizes that recorded cases are only a fraction of the true scale due to pervasive insecurity, stigma, and lack of support services.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5ywgn2n3nqo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5ywgn2n3nqo))
*   **European Defense Tech Boosted by Iran War**
    *   European defense technology startups are experiencing a surge in demand from Middle Eastern governments for drone and missile interceptor systems.
    *   Detail: Companies like Frankenburg and Uforce are accelerating commercial discussions and expanding hiring in the region to meet this demand.
    *   Impact: This reflects a strategic shift by Gulf states to bolster defenses against Iranian attacks and highlights the growing market for advanced military tech.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/31/european-defense-tech-startups-iran-war-gulf-states.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/31/european-defense-tech-startups-iran-war-gulf-states.html))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

    *   Impact: Governments globally are implementing various measures, including fuel subsidies, rationing, and public transport incentives, to alleviate the economic strain on consumers.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/31/us-gasoline-hits-4-per-gallon-highest-since-2022-as-iran-war-drives-up-fuel-prices-more-than-30.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/31/us-gasoline-hits-4-per-gallon-highest-since-2022-as-iran-war-drives-up-fuel-prices-more-than-30.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjr9yr73xyeo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjr9yr73xyeo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/cn08w9ye38yo](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/cn08w9ye38yo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/31/euro-zone-inflation-smashes-through-ecb-target-to-2point5percent-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/31/euro-zone-inflation-smashes-through-ecb-target-to-2point5percent-.html))
