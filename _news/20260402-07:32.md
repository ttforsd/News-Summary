## Executive summary

*   **Iran War Escalation:** US President Trump delivered a hawkish address on the Iran war, threatening "extremely hard" hits for the next 2-3 weeks, despite claiming objectives were nearing completion. This dampened earlier hopes for a ceasefire and sent oil prices soaring.
*   **Artemis II Mission Launched:** NASA successfully launched its Artemis II crewed mission on a 10-day journey around the Moon, a critical step towards future lunar landings and Mars missions.
*   **Israel Intensifies Lebanon Attacks:** Israel expanded its strikes in Lebanon, hitting areas beyond Hezbollah control, including parts of Beirut, and announced plans for a buffer security zone.
*   **Indonesia Hit by Major Earthquake:** A 7.4 magnitude earthquake struck off Indonesia's Ternate island, causing one fatality and prompting brief tsunami warnings.
*   **China Robotaxi Malfunction:** Baidu's Apollo Go robotaxi fleet experienced a mass malfunction in Wuhan, halting traffic and reportedly causing collisions, raising safety concerns for autonomous vehicles.
*   **Oracle Announces Major Job Cuts:** Tech giant Oracle initiated "significant" global job cuts, impacting thousands, as the company ramps up investment in AI infrastructure.
*   **US Supreme Court Skeptical on Birthright Citizenship:** The Supreme Court appeared doubtful of President Trump's executive order aimed at limiting birthright citizenship for children of undocumented immigrants.
