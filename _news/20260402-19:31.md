## Executive summary

*   **Geopolitical tensions dominate:** The Iran-Israel war continues, impacting global oil markets and leading to diplomatic criticism of the US approach. Iran threatens major tech companies.

## Major Regional Events

*   **Iran-Israel War escalates with direct economic targets.**
    *   Iran's two largest steel plants were shut down due to US-Israeli air strikes.
    *   A highway bridge from Tehran to Karaj was also hit, killing two people.
    *   Iran's Revolutionary Guard (IRGC) threatened attacks on US tech companies operating in the Middle East.
    *   The IRGC targeted an Amazon cloud computing center in Bahrain in response.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1w4pdexxqpo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1w4pdexxqpo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/01/iran-irgc-nvidia-appple-attack-threat.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/01/iran-irgc-nvidia-appple-attack-threat.html))
*   **Humanitarian concerns rise in Iran amid conflict.**
    *   Iranian Nobel Peace Prize winner Narges Mohammadi suffered a suspected heart attack in prison.
    *   Her family fears for her life amid deteriorating health and prison conditions.
    *   Human rights lawyer Nasrin Sotoudeh was also arrested in Tehran.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdj7xye8knxo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdj7xye8knxo))
*   **Over 1,800 civilians killed in Burkina Faso since junta seized power.**
    *   Human Rights Watch (HRW) reports widespread "war crimes and crimes against humanity."
    *   Most killings (1,255) are attributed to the military and allied militias (VDP); others to Islamist militants.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ckgr639007zo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ckgr639007zo))
*   **Peter Magyar emerges as a significant political challenger in Hungary.**
    *   The former Fidesz insider is campaigning against Viktor Orban's rule on anti-corruption and economic improvement.
    *   Magyar's new party, Tisza, won 29.6% of the vote in recent European elections.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c78l7vyylgqo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c78l7vyylgqo))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil prices surge and markets react to Iran war uncertainty.**
    *   US oil prices soared 10% after Trump vowed "extremely hard" hits on Iran.
    *   The Strait of Hormuz blockade causes global oil supply disruption and rising fuel prices.
    *   Iran and Oman are drafting a protocol to "monitor transit" in the Strait, easing some market fears.
    *   IEA warns April oil supply crunch will worsen; considering further strategic reserve releases.
    *   Analysts fear oil demand destruction and potential energy rationing if the conflict persists.
