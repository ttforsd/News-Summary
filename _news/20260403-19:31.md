## Executive Summary

*   **Iran-US Conflict Escalates:** President Trump threatened further infrastructure destruction in Iran, leading to market volatility and soaring oil prices. An airstrike on a Karaj bridge reportedly killed eight civilians.
*   **US Leadership Changes:** Attorney General Pam Bondi and Army Chief of Staff Randy George were removed amidst administrative reshuffles.
*   **Myanmar & Burkina Faso Political Shifts:** Myanmar's coup leader consolidated power as president, while Burkina Faso's military ruler rejected democracy and extended his rule.
*   **Global Economic Impact:** US jobs unexpectedly surged in March, but inflation fears rose globally due to high oil prices, leading to new surcharges and market uncertainty.
*   **Tech & Space Advances:** The Artemis II crew sent back Earth images; Chinese chip firms saw record revenues driven by AI; Microsoft announced major AI investments in Japan.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Iran-US Conflict Intensifies:** President Trump threatened to "hit" Iran "extremely hard" and destroy more civilian infrastructure (bridges, power plants), aiming to bring them "back to the Stone Ages."
    *   This follows a US airstrike that destroyed a newly built bridge in Karaj, Iran, reportedly killing eight people and injuring 100.
    *   Over 100 international law experts expressed "profound concern" over alleged violations by the US, Israel, and Iran, citing attacks on civilians and "no quarter" rhetoric.
    *   Trump's administration maintains a fluctuating timeline for the conflict, recently projecting an end in "two to three weeks."
    *   Iran has denied Trump's claim of a ceasefire request and vowed continued defense against aggression.
    *   The UN Security Council failed to pass a resolution authorizing military action against Iran, due to vetoes by China, Russia, and France.
    *   (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy91x2n29nlo), [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp86mrj40e2o), [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g4e6z9960o), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/03/trump-threatens-to-destroy-iranian-infrastructure-saying-its-government-knows-what-has-to-be-done.html), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/trumps-iran-war-speech-oil-price-strait-hormuz.html), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/cnbc-daily-open-trump-threatens-to-put-iran-in-stone-ages-dampening-ceasefire-hopes-.html))
*   **Strait of Hormuz Situation:** A French-owned container ship and a Japanese gas ship successfully transited the Strait of Hormuz, a first for a major Western European firm since the conflict.
    *   Iran and Oman are reportedly drafting a protocol to jointly "monitor transit" through the critical waterway.
    *   (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjd8275jrrko), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/iran-and-oman-drafting-protocol-to-monitor-hormuz-strait-traffic-irna.html))
*   **US Executive Branch Shake-up:** President Trump removed Attorney General Pam Bondi from her post, appointing her deputy Todd Blanche as acting Attorney General.
    *   Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth also asked Army Chief of Staff Randy George to step down, replacing him with Gen. Christopher LaNeve.
    *   These moves are part of ongoing leadership changes within the Trump administration.
    *   (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce843ge47z4o), [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn8d63v058zo), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/trump-fires-attorney-general-pam-bondi.html))
*   **US Immigration Policy in Africa:** The US deported eight individuals of African origin to Uganda under a bilateral migration deal.
    *   The Uganda Law Society criticized the process as "illegal and dehumanizing."
    *   (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8ej43z8yw4o))
*   **Cuba's Prisoner Release:** Cuba announced it would release 2,010 prisoners as a "humanitarian and sovereign gesture."
    *   The release comes amid continued political and economic pressure from the US, including an oil embargo.
    *   (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy3r3w4zl8o))

## Major Regional Events

*   **Myanmar Coup Leader Becomes President:** General Min Aung Hlaing, who led the 2021 coup, was elected president by a military-aligned parliament.
    *   This formalizes a "civilian rule in name only" after five years of civil war and economic devastation.
    *   Resistance groups continue to oppose the military-backed government.
    *   (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1782nk0018o))
*   **Burkina Faso Rejects Democracy:** Military ruler Capt. Ibrahim Traoré stated democracy "kills" and is "not for us," extending military rule by five years.
    *   He banned political parties, calling them divisive, and criticized Western democratic models.
    *   Human Rights Watch reported over 1,800 civilian deaths since his 2023 coup.
    *   (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly0zp1xgz3o))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil Market Upheaval:** Global oil prices surged, with Brent crude spot price hitting $141.36 per barrel (highest since 2008) and futures rising to $109, following increased US-Iran tensions.
    *   Asian and European stock markets plunged, reflecting fears of prolonged conflict and continued disruption to the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   Analysts warn of potential "demand destruction" and fuel rationing globally if the conflict persists.
    *   (Source: [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/03/trump-threatens-to-destroy-iranian-infrastructure-saying-its-government-knows-what-has-to-be-done.html), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/trumps-iran-war-speech-oil-price-strait-hormuz.html), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/oil-demand-destruction-trump-energy-price-gasoline-ration-iran.html), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/brent-oil-spot-price-for-actual-cargo-soars-to-141-highest-level-since-2008-financial-crisis.html), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/trump-iran-escalation-asian-stocks-oil-prices-markets.html))
*   **US Labor Market Strong:** US employers added an unexpected 178,000 jobs in March, lowering the unemployment rate to 4.3%.
    *   This robust performance may encourage the Federal Reserve to maintain its cautious stance on interest rate cuts despite inflation concerns.
    *   (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/crk165g4vv3o), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/03/us-payrolls-rose-by-178000-in-march-more-than-expected-unemployment-at-43.html))
*   **Rising Inflationary Pressures:** India's bottled water and beer industries face significant cost increases due to surging crude oil prices, affecting PET resin and natural gas supply.
    *   Amazon will implement a 3.5% fuel and logistics surcharge for third-party sellers in the US and Canada starting April 17, citing elevated energy costs from the Iran war.
    *   (Source: [BBC](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5ywqky5dd7o), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/amazon-add-3point5percent-fuel-and-logistics-surcharge-for-sellers-amid-iran-war.html))
*   **US Trade Policy & Global Investment Shifts:** One year after Trump's "liberation day" tariffs, global investors are reconsidering US asset allocation due to policy unpredictability, favoring diversification into other markets.
    *   The Trump administration imposed up to 100% tariffs on branded imported drugs from companies not agreeing to lower US prices, aiming to boost domestic drug manufacturing.
    *   (Source: [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/one-year-on-from-trumps-liberation-day-global-investors-are-rethinking-american-exceptionalism.html), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/trump-pharmaceutical-tariffs-100percent.html))
*   **Tesla Deliveries Disappoint:** Tesla's stock saw its steepest drop of 2026 after reporting Q1 vehicle deliveries of 358,023, missing analyst estimates and marking a 14% quarter-over-quarter decline.
    *   This reflects pressures on global EV demand and Tesla's strategic shift towards AI and robotics.
    *   (Source: [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/tesla-tsla-q1-2026-vehicle-delivery-production.html))
*   **Private Credit Market Redemptions:** Blue Owl capped redemption requests at 5% for two of its private credit funds after receiving high withdrawal demands, partly attributing this to "AI-related disruption to software companies."
    *   (Source: [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/blue-owl-private-credit-funds-redemptions-requests.html))
