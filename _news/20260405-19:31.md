## Major regional Events

    *   Ukraine reported retaliatory strikes on Russian military industrial complex facilities.
    *   President Zelensky's offer of an Easter truce was reportedly unheeded by Moscow.

    *   German males aged 17-45 now require military approval for stays abroad exceeding three months.
    *   This measure is part of the Military Service Modernisation Act, aiming to enhance national defense.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvg3nr83xyvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvg3nr83xyvo))
*   **Cameroon President to Appoint First Vice-President in Decades**
    *   Cameroon's 93-year-old President Paul Biya will appoint a vice-president, a role scrapped in 1972.
