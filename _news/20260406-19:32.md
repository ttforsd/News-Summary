## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil Prices Soar Amid Hormuz Strait Crisis**
    *   Brent crude spot prices reached $141.36 per barrel, their highest level since the 2008 financial crisis.
    *   **Cause:** Iran's effective closure of the Strait of Hormuz, a critical global oil transit route, has created a severe physical supply shortage.
    *   **Impact:** Oil futures prices, though rising, are still understating the actual market tightness. Global fuel and jet fuel prices have surged.
    *   **Response:** OPEC+ agreed to a symbolic production increase, but actual delivery of oil remains constrained by the Strait's closure. Kuwaiti oil facilities have also reported drone attacks.
    *   **Outlook:** Analysts warn of potentially higher oil prices if the Strait remains closed, with significant economic consequences globally.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/05/crude-oil-prices-iran-war-strait-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/05/crude-oil-prices-iran-war-strait-hormuz.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/dated-brent-oil-price-actual-cargo-highest-level-2008.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/02/dated-brent-oil-price-actual-cargo-highest-level-2008.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c3r3vw4j38do](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c3r3vw4j38do), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/06/asia-markets-nikkei-225-crude-oil-trump-iran-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/06/asia-markets-nikkei-225-crude-oil-trump-iran-hormuz.html))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Artemis II Mission Progresses with Lunar Observations**
    *   The Artemis II crew has successfully completed its translunar injection burn, becoming the first humans to leave Earth orbit since 1972.
    *   **Observations:** Astronauts observed and photographed the Moon's far side, including the Orientale basin, marking the first "human eyes" view of the entire feature.
    *   **Context:** This 10-day mission is a crucial preparatory step for a potential lunar landing by 2028 and serves as a significant milestone in the US space race.
    *   **Future:** Development is underway for continuous communication coverage for future lunar missions to eliminate signal blackouts.

*   **Bangladesh Faces Lethal Measles Outbreak**
    *   Bangladesh has launched an emergency measles and rubella vaccination campaign.
    *   **Crisis:** Over 100 suspected child deaths and 7,500 cases have been reported in the past month.
    *   **Causes:** The outbreak is attributed to gaps in routine vaccination programs, exacerbated by disruptions from Covid-19 and political instability. A significant number of affected infants are under nine months old, prior to routine vaccination eligibility.
    *   **Response:** The campaign targets over 1.2 million children aged 6 months to 5 years, prioritizing high-risk areas like Dhaka and Rohingya refugee camps.
