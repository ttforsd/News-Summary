## Executive summary

*   **Iran War Escalation:** President Trump issued a final ultimatum to Iran, threatening devastating strikes on infrastructure by Tuesday night if the Strait of Hormuz is not reopened. US-Israeli forces have already targeted Iranian infrastructure, drawing international condemnation.
*   **Humanitarian Crisis in Gaza:** The WHO has suspended medical evacuations from Gaza after a contractor was killed by Israeli troops, severely impacting patient care.
*   **US-China Tech Race:** The US continues to lead in AI software, while China dominates AI hardware. New US export curbs on chipmaking tools are impacting Chinese firms and Dutch supplier ASML.
*   **Geopolitical Alignments:** US Vice President Vance openly backed Hungary's Viktor Orbán ahead of elections, criticizing EU interference. Taiwan's opposition leader visited China, aiming to ease cross-strait tensions.
*   **Economic Volatility:** Global oil prices surged amid Iran war threats and Strait of Hormuz disruptions. The IMF warns of "higher prices and slower growth" globally due to the conflict.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Iran War Ultimatum and Strikes**
    *   President Trump issued an explicit threat to decimate Iran's bridges and power plants by Tuesday night if the Strait of Hormuz is not reopened.
    *   He warned that "a whole civilization will die tonight" and confirmed US forces struck military targets on Kharg Island.
    *   Iran rejected proposals for a temporary ceasefire, demanding a permanent end to the war.
    *   The UK has refused to allow US use of British bases for strikes on Iranian civilian infrastructure.
    *   US-Israeli strikes have already targeted Iranian steel plants, bridges, a pharmaceutical plant, universities, and religious sites, leading to warnings of potential war crimes from UN officials.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2w0v19gw8o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2w0v19gw8o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/07/iran-war-trump-deadline.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/07/iran-war-trump-deadline.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/06/trump-iran-war-ceasefire-hormuz-strait.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/06/trump-iran-war-ceasefire-hormuz-strait.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/07/cnbc-daily-open-trump-says-iran-can-be-taken-out-in-one-night.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/07/cnbc-daily-open-trump-says-iran-can-be-taken-out-in-one-night.html))
*   **US Diplomatic Push in Hungary**
    *   US Vice President JD Vance visited Budapest to support Prime Minister Viktor Orbán's re-election bid.
    *   Vance criticized the EU for "election interference" against Orbán.
    *   President Trump also praised Orbán via speakerphone at a rally.
    *   Hungary's close ties with Russia and its veto of EU funding for Ukraine remain contentious.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

    *   Global oil prices surged, with US crude oil for May gaining over 1% to $113.87/barrel.
    *   The IMF warns of "higher prices and slower growth," preparing to cut global growth forecasts.
    *   Shipping through the Strait of Hormuz, a critical energy artery, remains disrupted, causing supply shocks.
    *   Airlines are cutting flights and raising fares as jet fuel prices have nearly doubled.
    *   Danone CEO anticipates potential food price increases due to inflationary pressures from the conflict.
    *   AWS data centers in Bahrain and UAE were damaged by drone strikes, impacting regional services.
