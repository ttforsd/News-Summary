## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Ceasefire: Fragile Truce Announced Amid Breaches**
    *   US President Trump announced a two-week ceasefire with Iran, contingent on the "complete, immediate, and safe opening" of the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   Iran's parliamentary speaker, Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf, accused the US of breaching the deal, citing continued Israeli strikes in Lebanon, a drone in Iranian airspace, and denial of Iran's right to enrich uranium.

    *   Negotiations between the US and Iran are scheduled to begin in Islamabad on Friday, with VP Vance, Special Envoy Steve Witkoff, and Jared Kushner leading the US team.

## Global Economic & Market Movers
