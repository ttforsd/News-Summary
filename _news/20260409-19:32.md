## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil Market Volatility and Strait of Hormuz:**
    *   Global oil prices have resumed their climb due to doubts over the US-Iran ceasefire holding, with Brent crude reaching $99/barrel.
    *   The Strait of Hormuz remains heavily restricted by Iran, which is demanding permission for vessels and reportedly plans to impose tolls, impacting global oil and gas supplies.
    *   Only a handful of vessels (11 in two days compared to 138 daily pre-conflict) have passed through the strait, creating a backlog of approximately 800 ships.
    *   UAE oil CEO stated the Strait is "not open" and Iran's conditions are "coercion."
    *   Brent crude spot prices are significantly higher ($124.68/barrel) than futures contracts, indicating deep and prolonged supply disruption.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq6j0rnvlzeo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq6j0rnvlzeo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3w39lg84w2o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3w39lg84w2o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/the-strait-of-hormuz-is-not-open-as-iran-controls-access-after-ceasefire-uae-oil-ceo-says.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/the-strait-of-hormuz-is-not-open-as-iran-controls-access-after-ceasefire-uae-oil-ceo-says.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/hormuz-strait-oil-traffic-way-down-after-ceasefire-hassett-says-even-one-tanker-is-big-deal.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/hormuz-strait-oil-traffic-way-down-after-ceasefire-hassett-says-even-one-tanker-is-big-deal.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/08/dated-brent-oil-price-iran-war-ceasefire-strait-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/08/dated-brent-oil-price-iran-war-ceasefire-strait-hormuz.html))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Artemis II Mission Success:**
    *   The Artemis II crew is returning to Earth after a highly successful lunar flyby, having broken the Apollo 13 distance record.
    *   The mission extensively tested the Orion spacecraft and human interaction, with systems performing better than expected.
    *   The successful mission boosts confidence in NASA's ambitious goal of a Moon landing by 2028.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70dr45dj1lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70dr45dj1lo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj60nkd8nrko](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj60nkd8nrko))
*   **US-China AI Competition:**
    *   The US leads in AI "brains" (chatbots, Large Language Models, microchips) through firms like Nvidia, while China excels in AI "bodies" (robotics, humanoids).
    *   China's DeepSeek chatbot challenges US LLM dominance by achieving comparable capabilities at significantly lower costs, leveraging an open-source approach.
    *   Both nations are aggressively pursuing the combination of advanced AI brains with robotics, seen in systems like Boston Dynamics' Spot and Ukraine's Gogol-M drones.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c145enxln0go](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c145enxln0go))
*   **OpenAI UK Project Halted:**
    *   OpenAI has paused its massive "Stargate" data center project in the UK.
    *   The decision is attributed to high energy costs and ongoing regulatory concerns in the UK regarding AI development and data usage.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/openai-halts-uk-stargate-project-amid-regulatory-and-energy-price-concerns.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/openai-halts-uk-stargate-project-amid-regulatory-and-energy-price-concerns.html))
*   **AI Data Center Boom:**
    *   The boom in AI data centers, with global spending potentially reaching $7 trillion by 2030, is "stress testing" insurers due to massive capital influx and complex financing.
    *   The industry faces challenges from high-value concentration, energy demands, and rapidly evolving technology.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/06/ai-data-center-boom-stress-tests-insurers-as-private-capital-floods-in.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/06/ai-data-center-boom-stress-tests-insurers-as-private-capital-floods-in.html))
*   **Anthropic Blacklisting by Pentagon:**
    *   A federal appeals court denied Anthropic's request to temporarily block the Pentagon's blacklisting of the AI company as a supply chain risk.
    *   The Department of Defense designated Anthropic's technology as a national security threat.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/08/anthropic-pentagon-court-ruling-supply-chain-risk.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/08/anthropic-pentagon-court-ruling-supply-chain-risk.html))
*   **Meta Debuts New AI Model:**
    *   Meta launched "Muse Spark," its first major AI model from the new Meta Superintelligence Labs, aiming to regain momentum in the competitive AI market.
    *   The company is significantly increasing its AI infrastructure spending, with a capital expenditure of $115-135 billion projected for 2026.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/08/meta-debuts-first-major-ai-model-attempting-to-catch-google-openai-after-spending-billions.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/08/meta-debuts-first-major-ai-model-attempting-to-catch-google-openai-after-spending-billions.html))

    *   Greece plans to ban social media access for individuals under 15 years old starting next year.
    *   This policy aims to address rising anxiety and sleep problems among youth, as well as the "addictive design" of social media platforms.
    *   Greece is advocating for a common EU framework for age verification and bans for minors.
