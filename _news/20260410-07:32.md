## Executive summary

*   **Middle East Ceasefire Under Strain:** The US-Iran two-week ceasefire is fragile, with Israel continuing strikes on Lebanon, prompting Iranian threats and disputes over the Strait of Hormuz's accessibility.
*   **Ukraine-Russia Truce for Easter:** Russia and Ukraine have agreed to a temporary ceasefire for Orthodox Easter, though skepticism remains about its durability.
*   **UK Undersea Infrastructure Threat:** The UK reported Russian covert submarine operations near critical undersea cables and pipelines, warning of serious consequences for any damage.
*   **Global Oil Market Volatility:** Oil prices remain high and volatile due to ongoing Strait of Hormuz restrictions and attacks on Saudi oil facilities, impacting global and regional economies.
*   **AI Race Heats Up:** Major tech companies are investing heavily in new AI models beyond traditional LLMs, while regulatory and cost concerns impact AI infrastructure projects.
*   **Consumer Tech Obsolescence & Innovation:** Amazon is ending support for older Kindles, prompting user concern, while BYD and KFC partner on fast EV charging in China.
