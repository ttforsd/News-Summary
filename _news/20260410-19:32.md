## Major Regional Events

*   **Deadly Israeli Strikes in Lebanon Undermine Ceasefire**
    *   Israel conducted a 10-minute blitz of over 100 targets in Lebanon, killing at least 303 people and wounding 1,150.
    *   This occurred hours after the US announced a two-week ceasefire in the Middle East.
    *   Iran and Pakistan stated the ceasefire included Lebanon, while Israel and the US maintained it did not.
    *   **Impact:** Deepens humanitarian crisis in Lebanon, escalates regional tensions, and directly threatens peace talks.

    *   **Impact:** Exacerbates Israeli-Palestinian conflict and raises concerns about escalating violence in the West Bank.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czrekrzzex1o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czrekrzzex1o))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Strait of Hormuz Closure Fuels Energy Market Stress**
    *   The Strait of Hormuz remains largely closed despite the ceasefire, creating acute stress in the physical oil market.
    *   Dated Brent crude prices reflect scarcity, trading at a significant premium to futures contracts.
    *   Trump accuses Iran of potentially charging fees for passage, calling it a violation of the ceasefire.
    *   **Impact:** High oil prices, supply chain disruptions, and calls for investigations into suspicious trading activity before ceasefire announcements.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/oil-price-wti-brent-saudi-pipeline-attack-middle-east-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/oil-price-wti-brent-saudi-pipeline-attack-middle-east-war.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/what-this-real-world-oil-price-says-about-the-level-of-stress-in-the-energy-market.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/what-this-real-world-oil-price-says-about-the-level-of-stress-in-the-energy-market.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cge0xre3d27o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cge0xre3d27o))
*   **Saudi Oil Output Slashed by Iranian Attacks**
    *   Iranian attacks on Saudi Arabia's East-West pipeline cut throughput by 700,000 barrels per day.
    *   Strikes on Manifa and Khurais production facilities further reduced Saudi oil output by 600,000 bpd.
    *   **Impact:** Exacerbates global oil supply disruptions caused by the Strait of Hormuz closure, pushing prices higher.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/iran-war-oil-saudi-arabia-east-west-pipeline.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/iran-war-oil-saudi-arabia-east-west-pipeline.html))
*   **Global Inflation Rises Due to Energy Costs**
    *   US Consumer Price Index (CPI) rose 0.9% in March, with annual inflation hitting 3.3%, largely driven by a 21.2% surge in gasoline prices.
    *   German inflation accelerated to 2.8% in March, also attributed to rising energy costs from the Iran war.
    *   **Impact:** Consumers face higher living costs, central banks face pressure on monetary policy decisions, and energy concerns influence political discourse.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/consumer-prices-rose-3-3-in-march-as-energy-prices-spiked-due-to-iran-conflict.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/consumer-prices-rose-3-3-in-march-as-energy-prices-spiked-due-to-iran-conflict.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/european-stocks-us-iran-ceasefire-uncertainty.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/european-stocks-us-iran-ceasefire-uncertainty.html))
*   **Consumer Sentiment Plunges Amidst War Fears**
    *   US consumer confidence hit a record low in April, with inflation expectations spiking to 4.8% annually, according to a University of Michigan survey.
    *   **Impact:** Reflects public concern over the economic fallout of the Iran war, potentially affecting consumer spending.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/consumer-sentiment-inflation-fears-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/consumer-sentiment-inflation-fears-iran-war.html))
*   **Europe Faces Imminent Jet Fuel Shortage**
    *   ACI Europe warns of jet fuel shortages within three weeks if the Strait of Hormuz remains closed, impacting summer travel.
    *   Airlines, including Lufthansa and Ryanair, are considering flight cancellations and capacity reductions due to soaring fuel prices.
