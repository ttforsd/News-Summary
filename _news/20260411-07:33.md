## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Peace Talks Begin in Pakistan**
    *   US Vice-President JD Vance leads a delegation for historic talks with Iran's Parliamentary Speaker Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf.
    *   Iran demands Israel halt Lebanon attacks and release of $120 billion frozen assets as preconditions.
    *   US President Trump expresses frustration over Iran's continued blockage of the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   Key sticking points include Lebanon ceasefire, Strait of Hormuz control, Iran's nuclear program, and regional allies.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn9qzl12537o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn9qzl12537o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1d9z1q2997o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1d9z1q2997o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2wyn8wdz0o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2wyn8wdz0o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/iran-war-vance-negotiations-trump-oil-hormuz-strait.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/iran-war-vance-negotiations-trump-oil-hormuz-strait.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/negotiating-points-hardest-us-iran-ceasefire-lebanon.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/negotiating-points-hardest-us-iran-ceasefire-lebanon.html))
*   **China's Role in Iran Ceasefire Efforts**
    *   Beijing's diplomatic push for an Iran ceasefire is motivated by economic stability and export concerns.
    *   China, a major oil importer, seeks to mitigate rapid energy cost increases and global economic downturns.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/china-iran-ceasefire-role-energy-economy-strategy.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/china-iran-ceasefire-role-energy-economy-strategy.html))
*   **US-EU Tensions Over Big Tech Regulation**
    *   The European Commission has imposed over $7 billion in fines on US Big Tech since 2024.
    *   Trump administration criticizes EU fines as hostility to innovation, threatening tariffs in response.
    *   EU asserts fines enforce competition laws and protect consumers.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/the-trump-administration-is-getting-angry-as-eu-big-tech-fines-top-7-billion-in-2-years.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/the-trump-administration-is-getting-angry-as-eu-big-tech-fines-top-7-billion-in-2-years.html))

    *   KMT seeks to resume broad exchanges, while stalling Taiwan's defense budget.

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Artemis II Mission Success, Future Challenges**
    *   NASA's Artemis II mission successfully completed its uncrewed Moon flyby and returned safely.
    *   The agency aims for a crewed lunar landing by 2028 and a Moon base shortly after.
    *   Significant hurdles include delays in commercial lander development and complex in-orbit refueling.
    *   China also plans to land an astronaut on the Moon by 2030, potentially ahead of Artemis III.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj0v119zp19o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj0v119zp19o))
*   **AI Security Discussed by US Officials and Tech Leaders**
    *   VP Vance and Treasury Secretary Bessent held calls with CEOs from Anthropic, xAI, Google, OpenAI, Microsoft, and cybersecurity firms.
    *   Discussions focused on AI model security, safe deployment, and cyberattack responses, particularly before Anthropic's Mythos model release.
    *   Fed Chair Powell also met with major US bank CEOs regarding Mythos's potential cyber risks to the financial system.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/vance-bessent-questioned-tech-giants-on-ai-security-before-anthropics-mythos-release.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/vance-bessent-us-bank-ceos-anthropic-mythos-ai-cyber.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/powell-bessent-us-bank-ceos-anthropic-mythos-ai-cyber.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/powell-bessent-us-bank-ceos-anthropic-mythos-ai-cyber.html))
