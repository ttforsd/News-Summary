## Global Economic & Market Movers
*   **Consumer Sentiment and Inflation:**
    *   US consumer confidence hit a record low in April, primarily due to the Iran war and surging energy prices.
    *   Detail: March CPI rose 3.3% year-over-year, with gasoline prices up 21.2%.
    *   Impact: Inflation concerns continue to weigh on economic outlook, complicating Fed policy.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/consumer-sentiment-inflation-fears-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/consumer-sentiment-inflation-fears-iran-war.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/cpi-inflation-march-2026-breakdown.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/cpi-inflation-march-2026-breakdown.html))
*   **Global Oil Market Pressures:**
    *   Oil prices eased below $100/barrel (Brent at $96.51), but persistent stress remains in physical markets.
    *   Context: Strait of Hormuz largely closed despite ceasefire, disrupting global oil supply.
    *   Impact: Risk of stagflation rises in oil-dependent Asian economies, recalling 1997 crisis.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/oil-price-wti-brent-saudi-pipeline-attack-middle-east-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/oil-price-wti-brent-saudi-pipeline-attack-middle-east-war.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/what-this-real-world-oil-price-says-about-the-level-of-stress-in-the-energy-market.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/what-this-real-world-oil-price-says-about-the-level-of-stress-in-the-energy-market.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/asia-iran-oil-shock-1997-crisis-comparison-reserves-stagflation-hormuz-trump.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/09/asia-iran-oil-shock-1997-crisis-comparison-reserves-stagflation-hormuz-trump.html))
