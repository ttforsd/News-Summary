## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Peace Talks Fail to Reach Agreement:**
    *   US Vice President JD Vance announced the collapse of peace talks in Pakistan after 21 hours of negotiations.
    *   Iran refused to provide an "affirmative commitment" not to pursue nuclear weapons, a core US demand.
    *   The failure jeopardizes the fragile two-week ceasefire, with Iran's Tasnim news agency citing "excessive" US demands.
    *   The Iranian delegation also presented four "non-negotiable conditions" to mediators.
    *   **Impact:** Heightened geopolitical tension, uncertainty for global energy markets, and risk of renewed conflict.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/11/us-iran-talks-set-to-begin-in-islamabad-after-delegations-arrive.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/11/us-iran-talks-set-to-begin-in-islamabad-after-delegations-arrive.html)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/negotiating-points-hardest-us-iran-ceasefire-lebanon.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/negotiating-points-hardest-us-iran-ceasefire-lebanon.html))

*   **China's Diplomatic Role in Iran Ceasefire:**
    *   China made "active efforts" to facilitate the US-Iran ceasefire, driven by economic interests, particularly mitigating energy cost impacts.
    *   Beijing's circumspect approach focuses on brokering rather than direct mediation to protect its export-oriented economy.
    *   **Context:** China relies on the Strait of Hormuz for nearly half of its seaborne oil imports and seeks regional stability.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Consumer Sentiment Hits Record Low Amid Iran War:**
    *   US consumer confidence plunged to a record low of 47.6 in April, driven by rising energy prices and the Iran war.
    *   Inflation expectations spiked to 4.8% for the next year, the highest since August 2025.
    *   **Impact:** The war complicates the Federal Reserve's interest rate policy decisions.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/consumer-sentiment-inflation-fears-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/consumer-sentiment-inflation-fears-iran-war.html)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/cpi-inflation-report-march-2026.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/cpi-inflation-report-march-2026.html)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/cpi-inflation-march-2026-breakdown.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/cpi-inflation-march-2026-breakdown.html))

*   **Oil Prices Fluctuate, Strait of Hormuz Remains Restricted:**
    *   US oil prices slipped below $100 after Trump demanded the reopening of the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   Dated Brent, the real-world crude price, rebounded to $131.97/barrel, reflecting underlying market stress.
    *   **Context:** The Strait, a critical chokepoint for 20% of global oil, remains largely closed despite a ceasefire, keeping markets on edge. Saudi oil production is also cut by recent attacks.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/oil-price-wti-brent-saudi-pipeline-attack-middle-east-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/oil-price-wti-brent-saudi-pipeline-attack-middle-east-war.html)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/what-this-real-world-oil-price-says-about-the-level-of-stress-in-the-energy-market.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/10/what-this-real-world-oil-price-says-about-the-level-of-stress-in-the-energy-market.html))

    *   Europe is losing its position as a global pharmaceutical powerhouse due to aggressive US trade policies and China's biotech boom.
    *   **Context:** Europe's share of global R&D has halved in 35 years, with companies prioritizing investments in the US and China. New US tariffs and pricing models put pressure on drug launches in Europe.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/11/trump-pharma-tariffs-mfn-china-europe-biotech-stocks.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/11/trump-pharma-tariffs-mfn-china-europe-biotech-stocks.html))
