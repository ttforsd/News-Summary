## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **U.S. Implements Naval Blockade on Iran:**
    *   U.S. Central Command began blocking ships from entering or exiting Iranian ports in the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   This action followed the collapse of 21-hour peace talks in Pakistan, where Iran refused to abandon its nuclear ambitions.
    *   President Trump warned Iranian vessels against approaching the blockade, threatening severe retaliation.
    *   The UK and Germany have stated they will not join the U.S. blockade.
    *   France, alongside the UK, plans to co-host a conference to restore freedom of navigation in the Strait, independent of the belligerents.
    *   Iran's Revolutionary Guard Corps warned any military vessels approaching the Strait would be considered a ceasefire breach.

## Major Regional Events

*   **Hungary's Political Shift:**
    *   Péter Magyar's Tisza party achieved a landslide victory in Hungary's election, ending Viktor Orbán's 16-year rule.
    *   Magyar's win grants his party a constitutional majority, enabling potential reforms against corruption and a pivot towards closer EU ties.
    *   The outcome is seen as a significant positive for liberal democracy in Europe and a blow to Russia and the U.S., who considered Orbán an ally.
    *   Sources: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cd9vg782kx7o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cd9vg782kx7o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/13/europe-cheers-orban-defeat-as-a-bloody-nose-for-the-kremlin-but-hungarys-future-remains-contested.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/13/europe-cheers-orban-defeat-as-a-bloody-nose-for-the-kremlin-but-hungarys-future-remains-contested.html)

*   **Iran's Execution Rate Increases:**
    *   Iran executed at least 1,639 people last year, marking the highest number recorded since 1989.
    *   This represents a 68% increase from 2024, with most convictions related to drug-related offenses or murder.
    *   Human rights organizations warn that Iranian authorities may further escalate executions as a tool of oppression.
    *   Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly9ggzy3ngo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly9ggzy3ngo)

*   **Ukraine-Russia Easter Truce Violations:**
    *   An Orthodox Easter ceasefire between Russia and Ukraine saw over 2,000 alleged violations by Sunday morning.
    *   Ukrainian President Zelensky stated that Ukrainian forces would respond "strictly in kind" to any Russian actions.
    *   Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj0vn30574no](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj0vn30574no)

*   **Deadly Israeli Attacks in Lebanon:**
    *   Widespread Israeli air strikes killed over 300 people in Lebanon, including women, children, and the elderly.
    *   The attacks occurred hours after a ceasefire with Iran was announced, hitting previously untargeted civilian areas.
    *   Israel claimed it targeted sites linked to Hezbollah, while Lebanon reported an unprecedented civilian death toll.
    *   Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c0rx4ve0klyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c0rx4ve0klyo)

    *   The Nigerian Air Force has launched an investigation after an airstrike on a market reportedly killed dozens of civilians.
