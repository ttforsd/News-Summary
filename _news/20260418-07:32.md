## Major regional Events

*   **Hungary's Political Seismic Shift**
    *   Péter Magyar's Tisza party achieved a landslide victory, ending Viktor Orbán's 16-year rule with 52% of the vote and 140 of 199 parliamentary seats. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g40npz37lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g40npz37lo))
    *   Magyar plans swift government formation, retroactive term limits for PMs, and a strong anti-corruption drive, including joining the European Public Prosecutor's Office.
    *   The shift signals a potential foreign policy reorientation towards the EU, with strong youth support and chants of "Europa" and "Russians go home."
    *   This development could unlock €17bn in withheld EU funds contingent on judicial independence and media freedom. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g40npz37lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g40npz37lo))

*   **Turkey's School Shootings & Aftermath**
    *   Turkey is grappling with a new trauma after two deadly school shootings this week, with nine killed in Kahramanmaras and 16 injured in Siverek. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwyv21q9y1go](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwyv21q9y1go), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz9025d7jqzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz9025d7jqzo))
