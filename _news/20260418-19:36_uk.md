    *   An influential Cochrane review suggests that "breakthrough" Alzheimer's drugs, such as donanemab and lecanemab, may not offer significant patient benefit.

        *   Controversy: These findings have provoked a strong backlash from other prominent scientists who criticize the review's methodology.
