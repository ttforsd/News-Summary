## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Peace Talks Resume Amid Strait of Hormuz Disagreement**
    *   President Trump announced US negotiators would go to Pakistan for a second round of peace talks with Iran starting Monday.
    *   Iran's parliament speaker, Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf, stated talks continue but Iran is ready to resume conflict if US fails its obligations.
    *   Iran initially declared the Strait of Hormuz open for commercial vessels but later claimed it was closed due to US blockade.
    *   The Strait of Hormuz, crucial for global oil supply, saw multiple tankers come under fire from Iran's Revolutionary Guard, sparking confusion.
    *   Impact: Volatility in energy markets, as oil prices plunged due to perceived de-escalation then recovered slightly with renewed uncertainty.

## Major Regional Events

*   **Ukraine Police Chief Resigns After Officers Fled Kyiv Shooting**
    *   Ukraine's head of patrol police, Yevhen Zhukov, resigned after two officers allegedly fled a deadly mass shooting in Kyiv.
    *   Six people died and 14 were injured after a man opened fire in the Holosiivskyi district and took hostages.
    *   President Zelensky accused the officers of "inaction" and said a criminal case is underway.
    *   Impact: Raises concerns about the professionalism and readiness of Ukrainian security forces during wartime.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8ejn778j4do](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8ejn778j4do)), ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm25r1vkx4lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm25r1vkx4lo))

*   **Hungary's Orbán Era Ends with Landslide Election Defeat**
    *   Péter Magyar's Tisza party achieved a dramatic landslide victory, winning 53% of the vote and 141 seats in parliament.
    *   This ends Viktor Orbán's Fidesz party's 16 years of continuous rule, which slumped to 52 seats.
    *   Magyar plans to bring forward parliament formation and retroactively limit prime ministerial terms to two.
    *   Impact: A significant political shift in an EU member state, potentially altering EU internal dynamics and Hungary's domestic policies.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g40npz37lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g40npz37lo))

    *   Impact: Highlights critical environmental concerns, climate change effects on marine ecosystems, and the need for new conservation strategies.
