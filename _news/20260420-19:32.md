## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

        *   Significance: This aims to simplify and broaden the accessibility of CAR-T treatments, which traditionally involve complex *ex vivo* processes.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/20/eli-lilly-to-acquire-cancer-drug-maker-kelonia.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/20/eli-lilly-to-acquire-cancer-drug-maker-kelonia.html))
