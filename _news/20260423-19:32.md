## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Strait of Hormuz Standoff:**
    *   President Trump ordered the US Navy to "shoot and kill any boat" found laying mines in the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   US minesweepers are tasked with tripling clearing efforts, with Trump asserting "total control" over the vital oil route.
    *   Iran's chief negotiator reportedly resigned, contributing to a 3% surge in Brent oil prices amidst fragile ceasefire optimism.
    *   Iran's parliamentary speaker stated reopening the Strait of Hormuz is "impossible" while the US blockade persists.
    *   The IEA warns of the "biggest energy security threat in history," with 20 million barrels/day of oil disrupted.
    *   Iran's economy is in freefall, with IMF projecting a 6.1% contraction and 68.9% inflation for 2026.
