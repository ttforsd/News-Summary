## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil Prices Rise as Iran War Sustains Market Tensions**
    *   Brent and WTI crude oil futures climbed due to ongoing Middle East conflict and the closure of the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   IEA chief Fatih Birol warned of the "biggest energy security threat in history," highlighting major disruptions in vital commodities.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/oil-price-wti-brent-after-israel-lebanon-ceasefire-extension.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/oil-price-wti-brent-after-israel-lebanon-ceasefire-extension.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/oil-markets-prices-fuel-shortages-iran-war-iea-chief.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/oil-markets-prices-fuel-shortages-iran-war-iea-chief.html))
*   **Europe's Summer Travel Threatened by Jet Fuel Shortages**
    *   Airlines like Lufthansa are cutting thousands of flights as soaring jet fuel prices make routes unprofitable.
    *   Europe, heavily reliant on Middle East refineries for jet fuel, faces potential summer travel disruption if alternative supplies aren't secured.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cre1r4n5j5wo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cre1r4n5j5wo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/europe-jet-fuel-shortage-airlines-cut-flights.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/europe-jet-fuel-shortage-airlines-cut-flights.html))
*   **Iran's Economy in "Freefall" Due to War and Blockade**
    *   The Middle East conflict is causing severe economic contraction, with inflation exceeding 100% for food by March 2026.
    *   The Rial has lost 60% of its value, and the Strait of Hormuz blockade has cut off most international trade.
    *   Iranian officials warn it may take over a decade to rebuild the war-torn economy.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdrm8k7lzmko](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdrm8k7lzmko), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/iran-economy-war-charts-rial-oil-strait-hormuz-blockade.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/iran-economy-war-charts-rial-oil-strait-hormuz-blockade.html))
*   **China's Economy Grapples with Iran War Impact**
    *   Rising oil costs and Strait of Hormuz shipping disruptions are negatively affecting China's manufacturing and exports.
    *   China is competing with India for Russian oil supplies and seeking new buyers for its growing EV sector to mitigate trade risks.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn0ep28drllo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn0ep28drllo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/india-china-russian-oil-supply-strait-hormuz-disruption.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/23/india-china-russian-oil-supply-strait-hormuz-disruption.html))
*   **UK Stocks Outperform Wall Street Amid Geopolitical Risk**
    *   The FTSE 100 has gained 5.3% year-to-date, surpassing major US indices, driven by defensive sectors and attractive cash returns.
    *   However, the Iran war's impact on fuel prices poses a risk to this outperformance for the net energy importer.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/uk-stocks-are-beating-wall-street-but-the-iran-war-is-putting-the-winning-streak-at-risk.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/uk-stocks-are-beating-Wall-Street-but-the-iran-war-is-putting-the-winning-streak-at-risk.html))
