    *   (Sources: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz78x703lrvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz78x703lrvo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cde51y0zgjyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cde51y0zgjyo))
