## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Europe Tensions Over Iran War:**
    *   A leaked US Pentagon email suggested punishing Spain by suspending it from NATO.
    *   The report also proposed reviewing the US position on the UK's claim to the Falkland Islands.
    *   Punitive measures were suggested due to perceived insufficient allied support for the US-Israel campaign against Iran.
    *   NATO officials stated no treaty provision exists for membership suspension or expulsion.
    *   European leaders, including Spain's PM, strongly dismissed the US report and stressed alliance unity.
    *   Spain denied use of its air bases for attacks on Iran; UK allowed some use but limits further involvement.
    *   European allies are exploring the EU's mutual defense clause as a potential backup.
    *   Former NATO Secretary General warns the alliance's survival is not guaranteed amid disunity.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz78x703lrvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz78x703lrvo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj40r2gw24wo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj40r2gw24wo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cde51y0zgjyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cde51y0zgjyo))
*   **US-Iran Peace Talks Advanced:**
    *   US envoys Jared Kushner and Steve Witkoff will travel to Pakistan for direct talks with Iranian counterparts.
    *   The White House stated Iran requested in-person discussions and is expected to "make an offer."
    *   US President Trump reaffirmed the Strait of Hormuz blockade will remain until a deal.
    *   Iran's Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi is undertaking a regional tour for coordination.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/iran-war-pakistan-trump-hegseth.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/iran-war-pakistan-trump-hegseth.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm29m732vgno](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm29m732vgno))
*   **US-UK Trade Tensions:**
    *   President Trump threatened "big tariffs" on the UK if it does not drop its digital services tax on US tech companies.
    *   The UK's 2% levy on revenues from UK users generated £800 million last year.
    *   **Source:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/trump-tariffs-uk-tech-apple-google-meta.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/trump-tariffs-uk-tech-apple-google-meta.html))
*   **US Immigration Impact:**
