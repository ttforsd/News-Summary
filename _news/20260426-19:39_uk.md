## Major Regional Events

*   **US Presidential Security Incident**
    *   Gunfire erupted at the White House Correspondents' Dinner in Washington DC, leading to President Trump's evacuation.
      *   The suspect, Cole Tomas Allen (31, California), was arrested after exchanging fire with security.
      *   Preliminary findings suggest Allen was targeting administration officials, "likely" including President Trump.
      *   One Secret Service agent was injured but saved by a bullet-proof vest; no other injuries reported.
      *   Security concerns were raised regarding the event's screening protocols despite road closures.
      *   President Trump called for a new, more secure White House ballroom.
      *   **Impact:** Heightened focus on presidential security, potential implications for large public events.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cr717nglye0o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cr717nglye0o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdxd1x2qw2go](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdxd1x2qw2go), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2484nln8zeo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2484nln8zeo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/cgqkq18qqeeo](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/cgqkq18qqeeo))
    *   **King Charles III's US Visit:** Buckingham Palace confirmed the King and Queen's state visit to the US will proceed as planned.
      *   Decision followed discussions and minor adjustments are expected for security reasons.
      *   The King was "greatly relieved" President Trump and guests were unharmed.
      *   **Context:** The incident occurred just before the four-day visit, prompting a review of security measures.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz0e0rn179eo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz0e0rn179eo))

*   **Mali Conflict Intensifies**
    *   Mali's Defence Minister, Sadio Camara, was reportedly killed in a suicide truck bombing at his residence near Bamako.
      *   This occurred amidst a wave of coordinated attacks across the country by jihadist militants and separatists.
      *   Reports indicate Mali's military junta head, Gen Assimi Goita, was moved to safety after his home was targeted.
      *   Separatist Azawad Liberation Front (FLA) claimed Russian mercenaries agreed to withdraw from Kidal after clashes.
      *   **Impact:** Significant blow to Mali's government, marks largest coordinated jihadist attack in years, further destabilizes the Sahel region.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yvy7v66ndo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yvy7v66ndo))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Iran War's Economic Ripple Effect**
    *   UK minister Darren Jones warned of higher energy, food, and flight prices for at least eight months after the US-Israel war with Iran.
      *   Conflict causes global supply chain issues and price rises due to disrupted energy production and transportation.
      *   Government is planning for potential food shortages (e.g., chicken, pork) by summer if the war continues.
      *   **Impact:** Prolonged inflation for consumers, potential challenges for UK economic growth (IMF cut UK growth forecast to 0.8%).
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm29m98md2do](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm29m98md2do), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ckge5291152o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ckge5291152o))

*   **Global Stock Market Outlook**
    *   Bank of England Deputy Governor Sarah Breeden warned that global stock markets are "too high" and likely to fall.
      *   Asset prices are at all-time highs despite significant global economic risks.
      *   Concerns include potential macroeconomic shocks, private credit instability, and readjustment of AI valuations.
      *   **Impact:** Signals potential market correction, advising caution for investors and financial institutions.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c75kp1y43lgo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c75kp1y43lgo))

*   **Warner Bros Discovery - Paramount Takeover**
    *   Warner Bros Discovery shareholders approved Paramount's $111bn takeover bid.
      *   The deal, which includes major franchises and news network CNN, awaits regulatory approval in the US and Europe.
      *   **Impact:** Could drastically alter the media landscape, raising concerns about competition and news network independence.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgj09ny0dq6o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgj09ny0dq6o))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **US Reclassifies Cannabis**
    *   The Trump administration officially reclassified cannabis products under federal law to a Schedule III drug.
      *   This moves it from a Schedule I narcotic (like heroin) to parity with drugs like Tylenol with codeine.
      *   **Impact:** Major shift in US drug policy, increasing access and research opportunities for medical marijuana.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdxd0xxp0jko](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdxd0xxp0jko))

*   **US Accuses China of AI Technology Theft**
    *   The White House stated that foreign entities, mainly Chinese firms, are conducting "industrial-scale campaigns" to steal US AI advancements.
      *   These firms use "distilling" to copy US AI technology and "jailbreak" models to expose proprietary information.
      *   **Impact:** Escalating tech rivalry and intellectual property concerns between US and China, potential for new defensive measures.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpqxgxx9nrqo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpqxgxx9nrqo))

*   **Meta's AI Investment and Job Cuts**
    *   Meta plans to cut 10% of its workforce (approx. 8,000 staff) next month.
      *   This comes as the company significantly increases its investment in AI projects, allocating $135bn this year.
      *   **Impact:** Reflects a major shift in tech industry focus towards AI, potentially leading to fewer human jobs in areas AI can automate.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/crm1y89vek8o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/crm1y89vek8o))

*   **Breakthrough in Gene Therapy for Sight**
    *   A six-year-old girl in the UK had her sight restored in the dark through life-changing gene therapy.
      *   Saffie Sandford, suffering from Leber's Congenital Amaurosis (LCA), received Luxturna therapy.
      *   **Impact:** Demonstrates the potential of gene therapy to significantly improve vision in children with inherited eye conditions, especially if treated early.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce35x8759zzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce35x8759zzo))

*   **UK Biobank Data Listed in China**
    *   The UK government confirmed that health data from 500,000 UK Biobank participants was listed for sale on Alibaba in China.
      *   Data was de-identified (no names/addresses), but included sensitive information like age, gender, and lifestyle habits.
      *   **Context:** The data was legitimately downloaded by accredited research institutions but constituted a breach of contract.
      *   **Impact:** Raises data security and privacy concerns, especially regarding international research collaborations and public trust in biobanks.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpvxgl3n138o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpvxgl3n138o))

*   **China Car Giant BYD's Global Expansion**
    *   Chinese EV manufacturer BYD states it is thriving globally despite being largely shut out of the US market.
      *   The company is focusing on increased demand in other regions like Brazil, the UK, and Europe.
      *   BYD is betting on new "flash charging" technology for rapid EV charging.
      *   **Impact:** Highlights China's dominance in EV production and technology, intensifying global competition and shifting market dynamics away from traditional car markets.
      *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy01ele412yo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy01ele412yo))
