## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **UK Demographic Shifts**
    *   The UK is projected to see more deaths than births every year from 2026 onwards, according to the ONS.
        *   Population growth will slow, expected to peak in the 2050s, primarily due to declining fertility rates and reduced net migration.
        *   This trend will increase pressure on state pensions, health, and social care services.
        *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ckgpjd2zzl8o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ckgpjd2zzl8o))
    *   UK healthy life expectancy has fallen by two years over the past decade, now just under 61 for both men and women.
        *   This decline makes the UK one of only five of 21 richest countries to see a reduction.
        *   Significant disparities exist, with the wealthiest 10% experiencing 20 more healthy years than the poorest.
        *   Factors cited include poverty, poor housing, obesity, and the impact of the Covid-19 pandemic.
        *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c20q07w3gl9o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c20q07w3gl9o))
