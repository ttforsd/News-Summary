## Global Economic & Market Movers

    *   The UAE seeks greater flexibility to boost its oil production capacity to 5 million barrels per day by 2027.
    *   Analysts describe the exit as a significant blow to the cartel and potentially "the beginning of the end of OPEC."
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj4pxwlr52yo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj4pxwlr52yo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/uae-opec-oil-iran.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/uae-opec-oil-iran.html))
*   **Global Debt & Bond Market Warnings:** JPMorgan Chase CEO Jamie Dimon warned of a looming "bond crisis" due to rising global government debt.
    *   Dimon cited geopolitics, oil prices, and widening deficits as a dangerous mix that could trigger market stress.
    *   He urged policymakers to act proactively rather than waiting for markets to force a crisis.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/jamie-dimon-bond-crisis-global-debt-risks-build.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/jamie-dimon-bond-crisis-global-debt-risks-build.html))
