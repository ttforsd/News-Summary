*   **US Sanctions Warning on Iranian Oil Trade**

## Major regional Events

*   **Escalation in Ukraine War**
    *   Ukrainian drones hit Russia's Tuapse oil refinery for the third time this month, causing a major fire and local evacuations.
    *   Ukraine's military confirmed the strikes aim to reduce Russia's war-funding economic potential.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgzd4qk7qgo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgzd4qk7qgo))

*   **Pentagon Silence on Iran School Strike**
    *   The Pentagon faces criticism for its two-month silence on a deadly strike on an Iranian school in February, where US media reports suggest American forces were likely responsible.
    *   Former US officials cite a lack of transparency and accountability from the Pentagon.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy2482pn0lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy2482pn0lo))

*   **Mali Rebel Offensive and Russian Withdrawal**
    *   Mali experienced coordinated attacks by separatist (FLA) and al-Qaeda-linked (JNIM) groups, including in the capital Bamako.
    *   The attacks killed Mali's defense minister; Russian and Malian forces subsequently withdrew from Kidal, which is now controlled by FLA.
    *   Impact: Fuels doubts about Mali's military government and Russia's security partnership in the Sahel.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy818zdv831o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy818zdv831o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3w3wyq4v14o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3w3wyq4v14o))

*   **IS Claims Nigeria Attack**
    *   The Islamic State (IS) group claimed responsibility for a deadly attack in Nigeria's Adamawa state, killing at least 29 people at a football pitch.
    *   Militants opened fire indiscriminately and burned local properties, escalating regional security challenges.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62d43vdgmmo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62d43vdgmmo))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **UAE Exits OPEC**
    *   The United Arab Emirates will officially exit OPEC on May 1, aiming to increase its oil production capacity and pursue an independent energy policy.
    *   Impact: Weakens OPEC's global influence, could lead to increased oil price volatility and potential production conflicts with Saudi Arabia.
    *   "Flight risks" for other members like Kazakhstan, Nigeria, and Venezuela are noted due to quota dissatisfaction.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj4pxyklw1jo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj4pxyklw1jo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/29/uae-opec-exit-oil-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/29/uae-opec-exit-oil-iran-war.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/oil-uae-opec-saudi-arabia.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/oil-uae-opec-saudi-arabia.html))

*   **Oil Prices & Market Volatility**
    *   US crude oil prices neared $100/barrel after reports indicated President Trump is dissatisfied with Iran's proposal to reopen the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   The Strait of Hormuz remains largely closed, impacting a significant portion of global oil and LNG supplies.
    *   Citi UK CEO highlights "phenomenal" market resilience but warns that a prolonged Middle East conflict could push oil prices to $120-$150.
    *   Ryanair CEO warned that sustained high jet fuel prices could lead to the failure of other European airlines.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/oil-prices-us-iran-hormuz-negotiations-wti-brent-crude.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/oil-prices-us-iran-hormuz-negotiations-wti-brent-crude.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/citi-tiina-lee-iran-recession-war-ai-data-energy-markets-economy.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/citi-tiina-lee-iran-recession-war-ai-data-energy-markets-economy.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/ryanair-ceo-oil-europe-airlines-fail-jet-fuel.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/28/ryanair-ceo-oil-europe-airlines-fail-jet-fuel.html))

*   **Global Debt Crisis Warning**
    *   JPMorgan CEO Jamie Dimon warned that rising global government debt levels could trigger "some kind of bond crisis" if not proactively addressed.
    *   Cited geopolitical tensions, oil prices, and government deficits as key risk factors.

    *   Impact: Increases likelihood of a Reserve Bank of Australia interest rate hike; RBA cites Middle East risks.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/29/australia-inflation-q1-rba-rate-hike-outlook.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/29/australia-inflation-q1-rba-rate-hike-outlook.html))

*   **Corporate Earnings Report**
    *   **UBS** reported an 80% surge in Q1 net profit to $3 billion, beating estimates, and plans increased share buybacks despite Middle East conflict risks.
    *   **BP** profits more than doubled in Q1 to $3.2 billion, exceeding expectations, driven by "exceptional" oil trading performance amidst high oil prices from the Iran war.
    *   **Coca-Cola** topped Q1 earnings and revenue estimates, raising its full-year outlook as global beverage demand rose despite external volatility.
