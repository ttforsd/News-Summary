## Executive summary

*   **Oil Prices Surge Amid Iran Blockade:** Global oil prices, notably Brent crude, soared above $118 due to fears of an extended U.S. blockade of Iran and disruptions in the Strait of Hormuz, prompting U.S. action.
*   **Russia Scales Back Victory Day Parade:** Citing "terrorist threats" from Ukraine, Russia announced a scaled-back Moscow Victory Day parade, as Ukraine vows to escalate deep drone strikes on Russian territory.
*   **Major AI Litigation Begins:** Elon Musk is suing OpenAI and CEO Sam Altman, alleging breach of the company's non-profit founding mission.
*   **EU Targets Meta on Child Safety:** The European Commission accused Meta of violating EU law by failing to prevent underage users from accessing Facebook and Instagram, threatening substantial fines.
*   **U.S. Supreme Court on Electoral Maps:** The Court limited the use of race in drawing electoral maps, making it harder to challenge districts for diluting minority voting power.
