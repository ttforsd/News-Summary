## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US War Powers Resolution Deadline on Iran:**
    *   US Defence Secretary Hegseth claims the 60-day War Powers clock is paused due to an early April ceasefire.
    *   The deadline for Congressional approval for military action in Iran is Friday.
    *   Experts question the administration's interpretation, citing the US blockade of Iranian ports as active hostility.
    *   Iran has sent a new peace proposal via Pakistan, which President Trump indicated he is "not satisfied" with.
    *   The US is considering "short and powerful" strikes or a takeover of the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   US war operations against Iran have cost $25 billion to date.
