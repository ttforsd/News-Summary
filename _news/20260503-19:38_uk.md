## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **UAE's OPEC Exit:** The United Arab Emirates' decision to leave OPEC is viewed as a significant blow to the cartel's global influence.
    *   OPEC+ agreed to increase production by 188,000 barrels per day from June, without mentioning the UAE.
    *   The UAE was OPEC's fourth-largest producer in 2025 (3.1 million barrels per day).
    *   The closure of the Strait of Hormuz due to the Iran war continues to block a substantial portion of global oil supplies. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70vjpny0dno](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70vjpny0dno))
*   **Global Food Security at Risk:** The CEO of Yara, a major fertilizer producer, warned the Iran war threatens global food production.
    *   Interruption to fertilizer supplies could lead to a deficit of up to 10 billion meals weekly.
    *   Poorest countries are expected to be hit hardest, potentially triggering a bidding war for food.
    *   Fertilizer prices have surged 80% since the war began. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpwp50v4ye7o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpwp50v4ye7o))
*   **UK Airlines Prepare for Fuel Shortages:** The UK government announced contingency plans allowing airlines to cancel flights weeks in advance this summer.
    *   This aims to prevent last-minute disruptions for passengers and allows airlines to retain valuable airport slots.
    *   The measure addresses concerns over potential jet fuel shortages due to the Strait of Hormuz closure.
    *   The UK is seeking alternative fuel imports and boosting domestic refinery output. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c794zd1vqn4o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c794zd1vqn4o))
*   **U.S. Economy & Elections:** The U.S. economy grew 2% in Q1 2026 despite the Iran war's impact.
    *   Economic growth largely driven by tech and AI investment, not consumer spending.
    *   Inflation surged to 3.3% in March due to rising oil prices ($126/barrel high).
    *   Federal Reserve kept interest rates on hold, with future cuts potentially delayed until 2027.
    *   Stock markets (Nasdaq, S&P 500) saw gains despite economic pressures. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgepyv20vrpo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgepyv20vrpo))
*   **Spirit Airlines Shuts Down:** Spirit Airlines ceased operations after failing to secure a $500m bailout.
    *   The budget airline cited soaring jet fuel costs from the Iran war as the final trigger.
    *   U.S. Transportation Secretary disputed the fuel price impact, pointing to Spirit's prior financial issues.
    *   Other airlines are offering "rescue fares" for stranded Spirit customers. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqxlnrqjvzyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqxlnrqjvzyo))
