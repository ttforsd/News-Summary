## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **China Plane Crash Fuel Cut-off Theory**
    *   Newly released US National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) data suggests the 2022 China Eastern Boeing 737 crash was caused by a fuel cut-off.
        *   Detail: Fuel switches on both engines were moved to the "cut-off" position at cruising altitude, supporting an intentional act theory.
        *   Context: China has not released a final report, citing national security concerns, and previously denied pilot suicide speculation.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ckgpy223y9go](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ckgpy223y9go))

        *   Details: A UK national is critically ill; causes of other deaths under investigation; ship stranded off Cape Verde with 149 people.
        *   Context: Hantavirus is rarely transmitted human-to-human, primarily from rodents; WHO is monitoring the situation, stating low public risk.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy0294829ndo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy0294829ndo))
