## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Global Economy Risks Recession from Iran War Oil Shock**
    *   Analysts warn of a potential "big recession" as markets underplay the Iran war's impact.
    *   Oil prices surged over 50% since February, influencing global inflation and supply chains.
    *   Central banks face dilemma: raising rates to curb inflation risks recession.
    *   Australia's RBA hiked rates to 4.35%, anticipating sustained high inflation.
