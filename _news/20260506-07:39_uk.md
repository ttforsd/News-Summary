## Executive summary

*   The US paused its "Project Freedom" initiative in the Strait of Hormuz to facilitate talks for a deal with Iran.
*   Oil prices eased slightly after the US announcement, following earlier surges due to Middle East tensions.
*   Israeli air strikes in Lebanon on April 8 killed 361 people, despite a US-Iran ceasefire.

## Major regional Events

*   **Middle East Tensions & Shipping Crisis**
    *   The US paused "Project Freedom," an initiative to guide ships in the Strait of Hormuz. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g437depzpo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g437depzpo))
        *   This pause aims to allow negotiations for an agreement with Iran regarding the blocked waterway.
        *   Iran had reportedly fired missiles and drones at US and commercial ships in the Strait. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g437depzpo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g437depzpo))
        *   A captain described "mental pressure" from explosions near his stranded vessel in the Strait. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/czx2k3eprpgo](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/czx2k3eprpgo))

        *   This extensive bombing occurred despite a US-Iran ceasefire announced earlier that day.
        *   Lebanon disputes Israel's claim of targeting only Hezbollah operatives, reporting widespread civilian casualties.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **UK Economic Outlook**
