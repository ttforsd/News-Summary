## Major regional Events

*   **US-Iran Peace Deal & Strait of Hormuz**
    *   President Trump paused "Project Freedom" for ships in the Strait, citing progress on a deal.
    *   Reports suggest the US and Iran are nearing a preliminary agreement to end conflict.
    *   This deal would reopen the Strait, lift sanctions, and address Iran's nuclear program.
    *   Trump later threatened "much higher intensity" bombing if an agreement is not finalized.
    *   An Iranian parliamentarian dismissed the US proposal as a "wish list."
    *   The Strait of Hormuz closure has impacted global oil and gas shipments.

    *   Russia had also declared a unilateral truce for its upcoming Victory Day celebrations.
