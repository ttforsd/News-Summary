## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Peace Negotiations Progress with Caveats:**
    *   Iran is reviewing a US proposal aimed at ending the war and restarting nuclear talks.
    *   The proposal reportedly includes suspending Iranian nuclear enrichment, lifting sanctions, and reopening the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   US President Trump expressed optimism but also threatened intense bombing if a deal fails.
    *   The US had paused its "Project Freedom" to escort ships in Hormuz due to perceived progress.
    *   A key Gulf ally reportedly suspended US military access, forcing Trump's reversal.
