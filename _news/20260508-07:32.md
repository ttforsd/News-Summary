## Executive summary

*   **US-Iran Tensions Escalate:** Exchange of fire in the Strait of Hormuz reported by both US and Iran, each blaming the other, despite President Trump's claim of a ceasefire. Oil prices rose, and markets are reacting with skepticism about a quick resolution.
*   **Gaza Ceasefire Talks Stall:** Negotiations between Israel and Hamas reach a deadlock, with fears of renewed conflict in Gaza after an Israeli airstrike killed a Hamas leader's son.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Tensions in Strait of Hormuz**
    *   US and Iranian forces exchanged fire in the critical Strait of Hormuz, with both sides attributing the first attack to the other.
    *   President Trump maintained that a ceasefire is still in effect, calling the strikes a "love tap," but warned of harsher responses if a nuclear deal is not secured quickly.
    *   The UAE also reported missile and drone attacks from Iran.
    *   Impact: Elevated oil prices, increased regional instability, and skepticism in markets regarding a swift resolution.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c626zyywxjno?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c626zyywxjno?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/07/iran-war-hormuz-strait-ceasefire-trump.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/07/iran-war-hormuz-strait-ceasefire-trump.html))
*   **US-EU Trade Deal Ultimatum**
    *   President Trump issued an ultimatum to the EU, threatening "much higher" tariffs by July 4 if the bloc does not eliminate its levies on the US.
    *   The EU stated "good progress" is being made on tariff reduction, despite a US trade court ruling Trump's latest 10% global tariffs unjustified.
    *   Impact: Continued trade friction between major economic blocs.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp3pyk4nw3lo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp3pyk4nw3lo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

## Major Regional Events

*   **Gaza Ceasefire Talks in Deadlock**
    *   Negotiations between Israel and Hamas have stalled, raising fears of a return to full-scale war in Gaza.
    *   An Israeli airstrike killed the son of Hamas's chief negotiator, Khalil al-Hayya.
    *   Israel insists Hamas disarm according to a ceasefire deal, while Hamas alleges Israeli humanitarian violations.
    *   Impact: Dire humanitarian situation in Gaza, increasing calls for international pressure for a lasting peace.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cd6p5l3n2q3o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cd6p5l3n2q3o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))
*   **Ukraine: Civilians Trapped in Oleshky**
    *   Residents of Oleshky, a frontline city in southern Ukraine, are trapped by the ongoing war, a river, and heavily mined roads, including the "Road of Death."
    *   The city faces a humanitarian crisis with scarce food and medicine, relying on volunteers and aid groups.
    *   Impact: Ongoing civilian suffering, accusations of "deliberate terrorism" by Ukraine against Russia, and calls for a humanitarian corridor.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c893xj41zwdo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c893xj41zwdo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))
*   **Russia's Scaled-Back Victory Day Parade**
    *   For the first time in nearly two decades, Russia's annual Victory Day parade will not feature military hardware.
    *   The Kremlin cited "terrorist threats" from Ukraine and the need for tanks on the battlefield.
    *   Impact: Symbolic of the ongoing difficulties in the Ukraine war and growing public fatigue with the conflict.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy2gj2jlr8o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy2gj2jlr8o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))
*   **China's Military Purge**
    *   Two former Chinese defense ministers, Wei Fenghe and Li Shangfu, received suspended death sentences for bribery, commuting to life imprisonment.
    *   Impact: Part of President Xi Jinping's extensive anti-corruption campaign within the military, which critics view as a tool to consolidate power.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clypjx383j2o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clypjx383j2o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

    *   Impact: Heightened political tensions in the state after the BJP's historic victory and accusations of voter manipulation.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce8pjz7dv7ro?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce8pjz7dv7ro?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil Prices Surge Amid Hormuz Tensions**
    *   Oil prices rose as a new exchange of fire between the US and Iran in the Strait of Hormuz rattled an already fragile ceasefire.
    *   Brent crude futures increased to over $101.26 per barrel, with WTI futures also rising.
    *   "NACHO" (Not A Chance Hormuz Opens) trade reflects market's skepticism about quick resolution and prepares for prolonged disruption.
    *   Impact: Sustained high energy costs and volatility, driving profits for oil and gas giants (BP, Shell, TotalEnergies).
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/08/oil-prices-today-wti-brent-us-iran-fire-war-hormuz-ceasefire.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/08/oil-prices-today-wti-brent-us-iran-fire-war-hormuz-ceasefire.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/08/traders-nacho-trade-strait-hormuz-oil-markets-taco-trump-iran.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/08/traders-nacho-trade-strait-hormuz-oil-markets-taco-trump-iran.html), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce8pyyz5e0ro?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce8pyyz5e0ro?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)
