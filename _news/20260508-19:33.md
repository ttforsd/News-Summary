## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Hantavirus Outbreak on Cruise Ship:**
    *   A global effort is tracing passengers from the MV Hondius cruise ship after a hantavirus outbreak, with five confirmed cases and three deaths.
    *   The WHO states public risk is low; the Andes strain spreads through close human contact, not easily contagious.
    *   British passengers are being tested and repatriated from the Canary Islands, facing a recommended 45-day self-isolation.
    *   The outbreak's origin is unclear, but it marks the first known human transmission of the virus on a boat.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clyp1505p84o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clyp1505p84o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yr41vq2ero](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yr41vq2ero), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c98r199e195o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c98r199e195o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/cx218zp9897o](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/cx218zp9897o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c4g8pexx029o](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c4g8pexx029o))
*   **Global Cyberattack on Education Sector:**
    *   An international cyberattack, claimed by ShinyHunters, disrupted thousands of universities and schools using Canvas software in the US, Canada, and Australia.
    *   Many institutions reported outages and cancelled exams; some faced ransom demands.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce3pq0136eqo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ce3pq0136eqo))
*   **AI and Cybersecurity Risks:**
    *   Anthropic's Mythos AI model sparked "hysteria" over its ability to find software vulnerabilities, but experts say older AI models can achieve similar results.
    *   AI accelerates vulnerability discovery, yet slow patching processes create widening exposure for systems.
    *   The White House is considering new government oversight for future AI models, recognizing AI's initial advantage for offensive cyber capabilities.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/08/anthropic-mythos-ai-cybersecurity-banks.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/08/anthropic-mythos-ai-cybersecurity-banks.html))
*   **Cloud Infrastructure Outage:**
    *   An Amazon Web Services (AWS) data center in Virginia experienced a "thermal issue," causing an outage affecting platforms like FanDuel and Coinbase.
    *   Full recovery is expected to take several hours, underscoring critical reliance on major cloud providers.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/08/aws-outage-data-center-fanduel-coinbase.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/08/aws-outage-data-center-fanduel-coinbase.html))
