    *   This occurred after Russian missile strikes killed 24 people in Kyiv, including children.
    *   Ukrainian officials suggested the timing of the strikes was linked to the US-China summit.
    *   Russian President Putin is expected to visit Chinese President Xi Jinping soon.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y049w8nqwo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y049w8nqwo))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Market Sell-off Amid Inflation Fears:**
    *   Global bonds, equities, and precious metals saw a sell-off due to mounting inflation concerns.
    *   The US 10-year Treasury yield rose to its highest in nearly a year; UK gilts and Asian/European stocks were lower.
