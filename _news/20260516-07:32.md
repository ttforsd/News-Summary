## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-China Summit Concludes with Mixed Results**
    *   President Trump's two-day summit with China's Xi Jinping concluded with warm exchanges but few specific trade breakthroughs.
    *   Trump warned Taiwan against declaring independence, stating he is "not looking to have somebody go independent."
    *   Xi called the Taiwan question "the most important issue" in US-China relations, warning of potential "conflict" if mishandled.
    *   Trump said China agreed to buy 200 Boeing jets and billions in US soybeans, though China has yet to confirm these deals.
    *   Both leaders agreed to establish a "Board of Trade" to manage future relations and discussed common ground on Iran.

## Major regional Events

*   **Deadly Strikes and Prisoner Swap in Ukraine**
    *   Russia and Ukraine exchanged 205 prisoners of war as rescue efforts concluded in Kyiv after a deadly Russian missile strike.
    *   The strike on a Kyiv apartment block killed 24 people, including three children; Russia also reported Ukrainian drone attacks in Ryazan, killing four.
    *   Impact: The swap occurred despite heightened hostilities, highlighting the ongoing human cost of the conflict.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y049w8nqwo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y049w8nqwo))
*   **Middle East Conflicts Continue Amid Ceasefire Efforts**
    *   Israel claims to have killed Hamas commander Izz ad-Din al-Haddad in an air strike on Gaza City, targeting a residential building and a fleeing car.
    *   Impact: The strike occurred despite an ongoing ceasefire with Hamas, which has not confirmed Haddad's death.
    *   Lebanon reported six killed, including three paramedics, in an Israeli air strike in Harouf.
    *   Impact: The US announced a 45-day extension to the shaky Israel-Lebanon ceasefire, but daily exchanges of fire continue.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx21jjwz1k1o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx21jjwz1k1o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqlpzzl0d67o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqlpzzl0d67o))
*   **Rise of Russian Vigilante Group "Russkaya Obshina"**
    *   A nationalist vigilante group, Russkaya Obshina, is increasing raids against perceived "Western liberalism" and migrants in Russia.
    *   Members sometimes cooperate with law enforcement; one woman was convicted of "blasphemy" after a raid on her birthday party.
    *   Impact: The group is reportedly funded by Kremlin-linked figures, raising concerns about state-sanctioned vigilantism and human rights.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq5p306l7weo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq5p306l7weo))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Global Markets Slump on Inflation and Geopolitical Fears**
    *   Stocks, bonds, and precious metals globally sold off, with US 10-year Treasury yields reaching a one-year high.
    *   Impact: Mounting inflation fears, continued Iran war uncertainty, and UK political upheaval are driving investor caution.

*   **Middle East Oil Shock Mitigation and Future Supply**
    *   US and China have largely mitigated the oil supply disruption from the Strait of Hormuz blockade.
    *   The US increased exports by 3.5 million barrels per day (bpd), while China cut imports by 3.6 million bpd, partly using its strategic reserves.
    *   Impact: This prevented steeper crude price spikes, but US inventories are under pressure.
    *   The UAE is accelerating a second West-East pipeline to Fujairah, expected by 2027, to double export capacity and bypass Hormuz.
    *   US Energy Secretary Chris Wright expects China to increase US oil imports due to the ongoing Middle East conflict.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/china-us-oil-iran-war-strait-hormuz-trump-xi.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/china-us-oil-iran-war-strait-hormuz-trump-xi.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/uae-west-east-oil-pipeline-strait-hormuz-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/uae-west-east-oil-pipeline-strait-hormuz-iran-war.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/china-us-oil-import-xi-trump-chris-wright.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/china-us-oil-import-xi-trump-chris-wright.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/oil-prices-china-us-iran-strait-of-hormuz-middle-east.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/oil-prices-china-us-iran-strait-of-hormuz-middle-east.html))
