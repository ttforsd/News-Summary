## Executive Summary

*   **Global Security:** Ukraine launched large-scale drone attacks on Russia, killing three, while the UAE reported a drone strike near its nuclear plant amidst ongoing regional tensions.
*   **Middle East Conflict:** The US announced a 45-day ceasefire extension between Israel and Lebanon, but cross-border fighting persists. Trump issued a stark warning to Iran over stalled negotiations and the Strait of Hormuz closure.
*   **US-China Relations:** Trump's visit to Beijing saw mixed signals on trade, Taiwan, and Iran, while the US secured convictions against Chinese agents in the US.
*   **African Crises:** WHO declared an Ebola outbreak in DR Congo an international emergency; a senior IS leader was killed in Nigeria, but over 50 schoolchildren were kidnapped.
*   **Economic Outlook:** G7 finance ministers are meeting amid warnings of global economic consequences from the Strait of Hormuz closure, causing bonds, stocks, and precious metals to slump.
*   **Tech & Health:** Johnson & Johnson's new psoriasis pill shows blockbuster potential, and Cerebras Systems had a significant AI chip IPO.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Ukraine-Russia Conflict Intensifies**
    *   Ukrainian drones attacked the Moscow region, causing three deaths and several injuries.
    *   Targets included a residential building and an oil refinery.
    *   Ukraine's President Zelensky stated these strikes were "justified" retaliation for Russian attacks.
    *   Ukraine claims to have struck Russian oil facilities and air defense in Crimea.
    *   Russia responded with drone attacks and shelling in Dnipropetrovsk, injuring eight.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5ye480kxpmo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5ye480kxpmo))

*   **Middle East Tensions & Iran Conflict**
    *   The UAE reported a drone strike near its Barakah Nuclear Power Plant in Abu Dhabi.
        *   An electrical generator was hit, causing no injuries or radiological impact.
        *   The UAE denounced it as "unacceptable aggression," linking it to Iran.
        *   The UN nuclear watchdog voiced "grave concern" over threats to nuclear safety.
        *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy27pkj1l1o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy27pkj1l1o))
    *   The US announced a 45-day ceasefire extension between Israel and Lebanon, though clashes continue.
        *   An Israeli air strike killed six in southern Lebanon, including three paramedics.
        *   Hezbollah is increasingly using fiber-optic FPV drones, challenging Israeli defenses.
        *   Over one million people in Lebanon are displaced by the conflict.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqlpzzl0d67o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqlpzzl0d67o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1j2zwe9g5no](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1j2zwe9g5no))
    *   US President Trump warned Iran to "get moving" on negotiations or face severe consequences.
        *   Trump implied potential strikes on civilian infrastructure, considered a war crime.
        *   The Strait of Hormuz remains closed by Iran, severely impacting global oil flow.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/17/trump-warns-iran-wont-be-anything-left.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/17/trump-warns-iran-wont-be-anything-left.html))

*   **US-China-Taiwan Relations**
    *   Taiwan affirmed its independent nation status after US President Trump's cautionary remarks.
        *   Trump advised Taiwan against formal independence during his Beijing summit.
        *   He left "no commitment either way" on Taiwan's defense or an $11bn arms package.
        *   China considers Taiwan its territory and warned against mishandling the issue.
        *   US naval carriers have moved from the Indo-Pacific to the Middle East, affecting regional balance.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2132w81jqo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cx2132w81jqo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/16/trumps-meeting-with-chinas-xi-steers-the-us-away-from-taiwan-again.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/16/trumps-meeting-with-chinas-xi-steers-the-us-away-from-taiwan-again.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/trump-china-xi-taiwan.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/trump-china-xi-taiwan.html))
    *   Trump's Beijing summit with President Xi Jinping strengthened a fragile trade truce.
        *   China agreed to purchase US oil and granted access for advanced Nvidia AI chips.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/trump-xi-summit-the-3-big-takeaways-from-historic-meeting-in-beijing.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/trump-xi-summit-the-3-big-takeaways-from-historic-meeting-in-beijing.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/trump-leaves-china-after-talks-dominated-by-trade-oil-and-taiwan.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/trump-leaves-china-after-talks-dominated-by-trade-oil-and-taiwan.html))
    *   US authorities secured convictions against Chinese agents operating in the US.
        *   Cases involved a community group president and a California mayor for foreign agent activities and propaganda.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm2p8z261ylo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm2p8z261ylo))

*   **US-Cuba Relations**
    *   The US plans to indict former Cuban leader Raúl Castro over a 1996 plane downing.
        *   CIA Director visited Cuba, linking US aid to "fundamental changes" in its system.
        *   US blockade continues to worsen Cuba's fuel and energy crisis.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1w2gnd9xp2o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1w2gnd9xp2o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/cuba-cia-john-ratcliffe-oil-blockade-trump-blackouts.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/cuba-cia-john-ratcliffe-oil-blockade-trump-blackouts.html))

## Major Regional Events

*   **Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) Ebola Emergency**
    *   The WHO declared an Ebola outbreak in eastern DR Congo an international emergency.
        *   Approximately 246 suspected cases and 80 deaths have been reported.
        *   The Bundibugyo virus strain has no approved drugs or vaccines.
        *   Two confirmed cases have spread to neighboring Uganda.
        *   Conflict and population movement increase the risk of further spread.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2l2p0wwzzdo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2l2p0wwzzdo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9q311nj5r3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9q311nj5r3o))

*   **Nigeria Security Developments**
    *   Over 50 schoolchildren, including toddlers, were kidnapped from three schools in Borno state.
        *   Gunmen reportedly used children as human shields while fleeing.
        *   Suspected groups include Boko Haram or Islamic State West Africa Province (ISWAP).
        *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c202vrxlwqgo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c202vrxlwqgo))
    *   A senior Islamic State leader, Abu-Bilal al-Minuki, was killed in a joint US-Nigeria operation.
        *   Al-Minuki was considered the "second in command of ISIS globally."
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy72p2kpd03o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy72p2kpd03o))

*   **Afghanistan Civilian Casualties**
    *   A Pakistani airstrike tragically killed 269 civilians at a Kabul drug rehabilitation hospital.
        *   Pakistan's claim of targeting military infrastructure was refuted by the UN and BBC.
        *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/cp8pm97yde3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/cp8pm97yde3o))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Market Downturn Amid Inflation Fears**
    *   Global markets saw bonds, stocks, and precious metals slump sharply.
        *   The US 10-year Treasury yield hit its highest level in nearly a year.
        *   UK gilts and Japanese bonds also experienced significant yield increases.
        *   Spot silver fell 7%, reflecting broader precious metal pressure.
        *   Driven by mounting inflation concerns, geopolitical tensions, and "higher for longer" interest rate expectations.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/bonds-stocks-and-precious-metals-slump-as-inflation-fears-mount-silver-falls-7.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/bonds-stocks-and-precious-metals-slump-as-inflation-fears-mount-silver-falls-7.html))
    *   G7 finance ministers convened in Paris to address global economic challenges.
        *   The Eurogroup President highlighted the critical need to reopen the Strait of Hormuz.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/17/opening-strait-of-hormuz-is-of-utmost-importance-eurogroup-head.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/17/opening-strait-of-hormuz-is-of-utmost-importance-eurogroup-head.html))

*   **Energy Market & Supply Dynamics**
    *   Oil prices jumped following President Trump's warning to Iran.
        *   Brent crude rose over 3% to $109.26/barrel; US WTI increased over 4% to $105.42/barrel.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/oil-prices-china-us-iran-strait-of-hormuz-middle-east.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/oil-prices-china-us-iran-strait-of-hormuz-middle-east.html))
    *   US and China collaborated to ease the Middle East oil supply shock.
        *   The US increased exports, while China significantly reduced imports (combined 7.1M bpd).
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/china-us-oil-iran-war-strait-hormuz-trump-xi.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/china-us-oil-iran-war-strait-hormuz-trump-xi.html))
    *   The UAE is accelerating a second West-East oil pipeline to bypass the Strait of Hormuz by 2027.
        *   This strategic move follows its decision to leave OPEC, aiming for greater export flexibility.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/uae-west-east-oil-pipeline-strait-hormuz-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/uae-west-east-oil-pipeline-strait-hormuz-iran-war.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/16/uae-decision-to-leave-opec-was-not-a-political-move.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/16/uae-decision-to-leave-opec-was-not-a-political-move.html))
    *   President Trump will decide soon on lifting sanctions on Chinese firms purchasing Iranian oil.
        *   This decision could impact Iran's revenue and US-China relations.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/trump-china-sanctions-iranian-oil.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/trump-china-sanctions-iranian-oil.html))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Pharmaceutical Innovation**
    *   Johnson & Johnson (J&J) projects its new psoriasis pill, Icotyde, as a potential blockbuster drug.
        *   It's the first oral IL-23 inhibitor, positioned to succeed Stelara.
        *   Studies are ongoing for its use in Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/16/why-jj-thinks-its-new-psoriasis-pill-could-be-one-of-its-biggest-drugs-ever.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/16/why-jj-thinks-its-new-psoriasis-pill-could-be-one-of-its-biggest-drugs-ever.html))
    *   Bristol Myers Squibb (BMS) partnered with China's Hengrui Pharma for drug development.
        *   This reciprocal deal sends BMS experimental drugs to China for early trials.
        *   Reflects a trend of Western biopharmas leveraging China for faster, cheaper development.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/bristol-myers-squibb-turns-to-chinas-hengrui-to-replenish-pipeline.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/bristol-myers-squibb-turns-to-chinas-hengrui-to-replenish-pipeline.html))

*   **AI Chip Technology**
    *   Cerebras Systems' stock fell 10% on its first full trading day after a major IPO.
        *   The company raised $5.55 billion, the largest tech IPO since Uber in 2019.
        *   Cerebras develops large AI chips (Wafer Scale Engine 3) to accelerate AI model training.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/cerebras-stock-ipo-debut-ai.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/15/cerebras-stock-ipo-debut-ai.html))
