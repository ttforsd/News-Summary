## Executive summary

## Major regional Events

*   **Ukraine Conflict Intensifies**
    *   Ukraine launched a large-scale drone attack on the Moscow region, killing three people and injuring several.
      *   Context: President Zelensky described these strikes as "entirely justified" retaliation for earlier Russian assaults.
    *   Ukraine's security service (SBU) claimed to have struck Russian oil facilities, a semiconductor plant, and air defense systems in Crimea.
    *   Impact: Russia reports intercepting hundreds of drones and is reportedly regrouping for a potential summer offensive in the Donbas region.
    *   Front-line warfare is increasingly dominated by advanced drones, including fiber-optic controlled FPV drones, which challenge existing defenses.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5ye480kxpmo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5ye480kxpmo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3w2xqj9x13o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3w2xqj9x13o))
*   **Middle East Security Developments**
    *   Lebanon's health ministry reported over 3,000 deaths from Israeli strikes since March, despite a fragile ceasefire extension.
      *   Context: Over 400 deaths have occurred since the US-brokered truce began on April 17, with continued violations by both sides.
    *   Hezbollah has increased its use of small, fiber-optic FPV drones against Israeli targets in northern Israel and southern Lebanon.
      *   Impact: Experts note these drones are highly effective at evading Israeli electronic countermeasures, changing tactical warfare.
    *   Israeli forces intercepted a flotilla of over 50 pro-Palestinian aid boats bound for Gaza in international waters.
      *   Context: Israel's Prime Minister Netanyahu stated the action was to neutralize a "malicious plan," while Turkey and Hamas condemned it as "piracy" and "state terrorism."
    *   The UAE reported a drone strike caused a fire near its Barakah Nuclear Power Plant in Abu Dhabi.
      *   Detail: One of three drones, originating from the "western border direction," struck an electrical generator outside the plant's perimeter.
      *   Impact: No injuries or radiological safety impact, but the UAE labeled it an "unacceptable act of aggression."

      *   Context: Activists claim the death penalty is used to silence dissent, often following torture and secret trials.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Stagflationary Warning & Oil Shortages**
    *   The EU is preparing to downgrade its growth forecast and raise inflation forecasts, citing a "stagflationary shock" from the Iran war.
      *   Context: The ongoing closure of the Strait of Hormuz is keeping oil prices above $100 a barrel, exacerbating economic pressures.
    *   Energy strategists warn Europe could face physical oil shortages within weeks as global inventories deplete rapidly.
      *   Impact: Summer holiday demand will further strain supplies; prolonged closure could push oil prices to $150/barrel.
    *   G7 Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors are meeting in Paris to address the mounting debt pressure and economic shock.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/18/eu-stagflation-shock-iran-war-oil-prices.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/18/eu-stagflation-shock-iran-war-oil-prices.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/18/europe-oil-shortage-iran-war-price-shock-inventory-strait-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/18/europe-oil-shortage-iran-war-price-shock-inventory-strait-hormuz.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/18/cnbc-daily-open-g7-finance-ministers-meeting-economic-shock.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/18/cnbc-daily-open-g7-finance-ministers-meeting-economic-shock.html))
