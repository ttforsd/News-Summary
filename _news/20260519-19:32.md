## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Bond Market Sell-off Intensifies**
    *   US 30-year Treasury yield hit 5.198%, its highest since July 2007.
    *   The 10-year US Treasury yield climbed to 4.687%, highest since January 2025.
    *   Inflation fears, driven by rising oil prices from the Iran conflict, are causing investors to dump bonds.
    *   This suggests potential Fed rate hikes and may weigh on consumer spending and equity valuations.
    *   (Sources: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/19/treasurys-yields-inflation-traders-fed-interest-rates.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/19/treasurys-yields-inflation-traders-fed-interest-rates.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/19/cnbc-daily-open-bonds-sell-off-markets-dip-oil-worries-loom-and-trump-postpones-iran-attack.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/19/cnbc-daily-open-bonds-sell-off-markets-dip-oil-worries-loom-and-trump-postpones-iran-attack.html))
*   **Oil Prices Remain Elevated Amid Iran Conflict**
    *   Crude oil prices edged lower after Trump's postponement of Iran strikes but stayed above $100 per barrel.
    *   The continued closure of the Strait of Hormuz by Iran is causing persistent supply disruptions.

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Ebola Outbreak Worsens in DR Congo**
    *   A rapidly spreading Ebola outbreak has caused 131 deaths and 543 suspected cases in eastern DR Congo.
    *   The World Health Organization (WHO) warns the rare Bundibugyo strain may be spreading faster than detected.
    *   Conflict zones and traditional beliefs (witchcraft) hinder containment and prompt medical reporting.
    *   An American doctor exposed in the DRC was evacuated to Germany for treatment; one death reported in neighboring Uganda.
    *   WHO declared it a public health emergency of international concern, with minimal risk outside East Africa.
    *   (Sources: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgzj0pqpyyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgzj0pqpyyo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ceqp11gn1l8o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/ceqp11gn1l8o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz72p75zg4qo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cz72p75zg4qo))

    *   These releases intensify Google's competition with OpenAI and Anthropic in the AI development race.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/19/google-ai-ultra-gemini-spark-omni.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/19/google-ai-ultra-gemini-spark-omni.html))
