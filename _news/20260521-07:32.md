## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US Charges Raúl Castro:** The US charged former Cuban leader Raúl Castro with murder and conspiracy over the 1996 downing of two planes.
    *   Impact: This move escalates already high US-Cuba tensions; Cuba's President Díaz-Canel called the charges a "political manoeuvre."
    *   Context: The US also continues public surveillance flights near Cuba, citing a need to enforce an oil blockade and pressure the government.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3r219yxl5eo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c3r219yxl5eo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjep1wx7w0ko](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjep1wx7w0ko))
*   **Russia-China Summit & Global Positioning:** President Putin's visit to China emphasized strong bilateral ties with President Xi Jinping, but without a key energy deal.
    *   Energy Diplomacy: No final agreement was reached on the Power of Siberia 2 gas pipeline, highlighting China's leverage in the relationship.
    *   Geopolitical Stance: Xi Jinping, having recently hosted Donald Trump, used the back-to-back visits to project China's diplomatic centrality on the world stage.
    *   Taiwan: The issue of Taiwan, central to Xi-Trump talks, was notably absent from Xi's discussions with Putin, as China seeks to avoid conflating it with Russia's actions in Ukraine.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8r8me3nlllo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c8r8me3nlllo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjep12qx3xjo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjep12qx3xjo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/21/three-key-takeaways-from-putins-beijing-trip-and-what-they-reveal-about-china-russia-ties.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/21/three-key-takeaways-from-putins-beijing-trip-and-what-they-reveal-about-china-russia-ties.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/20/taiwan-was-central-in-xis-meeting-with-trump-but-not-with-putin.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/20/taiwan-was-central-in-xis-meeting-with-trump-but-not-with-putin.html))
*   **Trump's Greenland Envoy Mission:** US envoy Jeff Landry visited Greenland to build ties but encountered local skepticism regarding US intentions.
    *   Context: Greenland's Prime Minister reiterated the territory is "not for sale," despite US aims for a larger military presence in the Arctic region.
