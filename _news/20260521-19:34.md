## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Iran Expands Control over Strait of Hormuz; US Retaliates with Surveillance**
    *   Iran’s new "Persian Gulf Strait Authority" claims military oversight over 22,000 sq km, extending into Omani and UAE waters.
    *   This includes demanding authorization for all transit through the vital oil trade route.
    *   The UAE denounced Iran’s claims as "fragments of dreams," while the US and Gulf allies reject attempts to control the strait.
    *   Iran, which has not ratified the UN Law of the Sea, also allegedly conducted a "punishment" strike on a tanker.
    *   The US military has publicly tracked P-8A Poseidon surveillance jets and MQ-4C Triton drones near Cuba, some as close as 50 miles, to enforce its oil blockade on Iran.
    *   **Impact**: Heightened maritime security risks, severe disruption to global oil supplies, and escalating geopolitical tensions in the Middle East.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq5py64gvwzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq5py64gvwzo)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/21/iran-war-us-peace-talks-trump-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/21/iran-war-us-peace-talks-trump-hormuz.html)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/21/oil-price-today-iran-war-strait-hormuz-trump.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/21/oil-price-today-iran-war-strait-hormuz-trump.html)), ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/20/uae-pipeline-strait-hormuz-iran-war-oil.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/20/uae-pipeline-strait-hormuz-iran-war-oil.html)), ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjep1wx7w0ko](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjep1wx7w0ko))
