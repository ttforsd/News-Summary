## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Peace Talks & Strait of Hormuz Control**

## Major regional Events

*   **Ebola Outbreak in DR Congo Reaches "Very High" Risk**
    *   The WHO elevated the public health risk from Ebola in DR Congo to "very high."
    *   The outbreak involves the rare Bundibugyo species (750 suspected cases, 177 deaths), for which no proven vaccine exists.
    *   An angry crowd set fire to Ebola hospital tents in DR Congo, demonstrating widespread community mistrust.
    *   Impact: India-Africa Forum Summit postponed due to outbreak fears, showing regional and international concern.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cr7p30m1dn1o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cr7p30m1dn1o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp8p2g8yp8do](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp8p2g8yp8do), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp8p1l7gp1po](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp8p1l7gp1po))
*   **Alberta to Hold Referendum on Canadian Unity**
    *   Alberta will hold a non-binding referendum on October 19 regarding whether the province should remain in Canada.
    *   Premier Danielle Smith supports staying but acknowledges a growing separatist movement fueled by a sense of being overlooked by Ottawa.
    *   Impact: This marks the first significant test of Canadian unity in decades outside of Quebec.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgze8n5dxko](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgze8n5dxko))
*   **Turkish Opposition Leaders Ousted by Court Ruling**

    *   Impact: The opposition condemned the decision as a "judicial coup" by the Erdoğan government to consolidate power.
