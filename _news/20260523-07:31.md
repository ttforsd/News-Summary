## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Russia-Ukraine Conflict Escalates**
    *   Russia accuses Ukraine of a drone attack on a student dormitory in Starobilsk, Luhansk region, killing 6 and injuring 39.
    *   President Putin vows retaliation, while Ukraine claims responsibility for striking Russia's elite Rubicon drone military unit headquarters.
    *   Russian oil depot in Novorossiysk also reportedly hit by drones, injuring two.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y74lwx395o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y74lwx395o))

*   **US-Cuba Tensions Intensify**
    *   The US Department of Justice indicted former Cuban President Raúl Castro for murder related to the 1996 shootdown of two civilian planes.
    *   Cuba's Foreign Minister calls this a "political maneuver" to justify potential military aggression.
    *   US Secretary of State Marco Rubio stated diplomatic solutions are unlikely, citing Cuba as a national security threat.
    *   The US has imposed an oil blockade and sanctions, contributing to Cuba's severe economic crisis and blackouts.
    *   China and Russia have condemned the US pressure on Cuba.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yrkl96xwvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yrkl96xwvo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/the-cuba-situation-is-starting-to-resemble-a-pre-conflict-playbook.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/the-cuba-situation-is-starting-to-resemble-a-pre-conflict-playbook.html))

*   **US Reassures NATO Allies on Troop Deployments**
    *   US Secretary of State Marco Rubio addressed concerns among NATO allies regarding inconsistent US troop announcements.
    *   President Trump pledged an additional 5,000 troops to Poland, following earlier cancellations and a planned withdrawal from Germany.
    *   NATO Secretary General Mark Rutte confirmed members will increase defense spending to "hundreds of billions" annually, aiming for 5% of GDP by 2035.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cedpz9669deo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cedpz9669deo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/nato-will-spend-hundreds-of-billions-of-dollars-on-defense-says-rutte-as-trump-pledges-5000-american-troops-to-top-spender-poland.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/nato-will-spend-hundreds-of-billions-of-dollars-on-defense-says-rutte-as-trump-pledges-5000-american-troops-to-top-spender-poland.html))

*   **US Intelligence Director Resigns**
    *   Tulsi Gabbard announced her resignation as US Director of National Intelligence effective June 30, citing her husband's bone cancer diagnosis.
    *   Her tenure included reported disagreements with President Trump over foreign policy.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgj2gkv1x1o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgj2gkv1x1o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/tulsi-gabbard-resigning-as-trumps-intelligence-chief.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/tulsi-gabbard-resigning-as-trumps-intelligence-chief.html))

*   **Greenland Protests Against US Influence**
    *   Hundreds in Greenland protested outside the new US consulate in Nuuk, rejecting increased US influence.
    *   The protest follows demands from President Trump for greater control over the island.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy2gxvnq1zo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy2gxvnq1zo))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Iran War Fuels Inflation & Market Volatility**
    *   US and Iran peace talks are making limited progress, stalled by disagreements over Iran's enriched uranium stockpile and proposed tolls on the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   The ongoing conflict has driven global oil prices higher, impacting consumer sentiment and central bank policies.
    *   US consumer sentiment fell to a record low in May due to inflation worries, particularly rising gasoline prices.
    *   The 30-year US Treasury yield hit its highest level since 2007 amid bond market volatility and inflation concerns.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/iran-war-strait-of-hormuz-tolls-trump.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/iran-war-strait-of-hormuz-tolls-trump.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/consumer-sentiment-hits-fresh-record-low-in-may-as-iran-war-fuels-inflation-worries.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/consumer-sentiment-hits-fresh-record-low-in-may-as-iran-war-fuels-inflation-worries.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/treasury-yields-investors-week-bond-volatility.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/treasury-yields-investors-week-bond-volatility.html))

*   **US Federal Reserve Leadership & Policy Shifts**
    *   Kevin Warsh was sworn in as the new Federal Reserve Chair, with President Trump urging him to cut interest rates independently.
    *   Warsh aims to lead a "reform-oriented" Fed, potentially rethinking the management of its $6.8 trillion balance sheet and its role in financial markets.

*   **European Economic Outlook and Central Bank Policy**
    *   European stocks saw gains, though UK retail sales dropped 1.3% in April, with fuel sales hit by Middle East price volatility.
    *   Despite improving consumer confidence in Germany and manufacturing climate in France, a senior economist warns the ECB's expected rate hikes could be a "big mistake," risking recession amidst stagflation and weakening demand.

*   **Japanese Inflation Weakens Rate Hike Case**
    *   Japan's core inflation fell to a four-year low of 1.4% in April, below the Bank of Japan's 2% target, potentially delaying a rate hike.
    *   Energy prices declined, but the Iran war is expected to pressure inflation upwards in coming months.

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **SpaceX Starship V3 Test Flight Success**
    *   SpaceX successfully launched its massive Starship V3 rocket, deploying 20 dummy satellites before a planned splashdown and explosion in the Indian Ocean.
    *   Despite some engine failures, the test flight achieved most key objectives, boosting confidence for future NASA moon missions.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62d65y16nno](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62d65y16nno), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/spacex-launches-starship-test-flight-on-second-try.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/spacex-launches-starship-test-flight-on-second-try.html))

*   **Ebola Outbreak Risk Elevated in DR Congo**
    *   The WHO raised the public health risk of the Ebola outbreak in DR Congo to "very high" due to the rare Bundibugyo species, for which there is no proven vaccine.
    *   The outbreak has led to 177 suspected deaths and 750 suspected cases; local violence hinders response efforts.
    *   Scientists at Oxford University are developing a new vaccine, with clinical trials potentially starting within months.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cr7p30m1dn1o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cr7p30m1dn1o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj3p2rdx0l8o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj3p2rdx0l8o))

*   **AI Dominance & Challenges**
    *   Qualcomm's shares surged due to its pivotal role in AI devices (smartphones, cars) and a partnership with OpenAI for new AI chips, alongside expansion into data center chips.
    *   The US is actively promoting American AI solutions in China and Asia, including workshops in Chengdu, amidst restrictions on Chinese access to advanced US chips.
    *   China is intensely focused on training humanoid robots for the workforce, as part of a national strategy to dominate AI and robotics supply chains.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/qualcomms-stock-pop-shows-investors-waking-up-to-boom-in-ai-devices.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/qualcomms-stock-pop-shows-investors-waking-up-to-boom-in-ai-devices.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/us-pushes-its-ai-in-china-and-asia-after-trump-xi-meeting.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/22/us-pushes-its-ai-in-china-and-asia-after-trump-xi-meeting.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/21/china-robots-humanoid-job-training.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/21/china-robots-humanoid-job-training.html))
