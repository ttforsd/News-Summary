## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Deal Negotiations Continue Amidst Uncertainty**
    *   Iran's foreign ministry states a deal with the US is "not imminent," despite some progress in discussions.
    *   US Secretary of State Marco Rubio expressed hopes for an agreement on Monday, while President Trump urged negotiators not to rush.
    *   Proposed terms include a 60-day ceasefire, reopening the Strait of Hormuz, and future talks on Iran's nuclear program.
    *   Key hurdles remain over Iran retaining enriched uranium and levying tolls on the Strait.
    *   Ex-CIA director David Petraeus suggests Iran is "blinking" on the Strait, but warns against allowing Iranian control.
    *   US intelligence indicates Iranian Supreme Leader's injury delays communication.
