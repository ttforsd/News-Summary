## Major regional Events

    *   Israel announced increased attacks on Hezbollah targets across Lebanon, including the Bekaa Valley.
    *   Hezbollah retaliated with 22 drone and rocket attacks on Israeli positions.
    *   This escalation follows a fragile ceasefire and raises concerns of wider regional conflict.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cewppdk1187o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cewppdk1187o))

    *   Critics view this action as cementing President Erdoğan's power and undermining democracy.
    *   Clashes occurred between police, party members, and rival factions.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c87qwrqre83o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c87qwrqre83o))

    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm2ppv1r3xzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cm2ppv1r3xzo))
