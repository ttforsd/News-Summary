## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Middle East Tensions Flare with US-Iran Strikes and Ongoing Ceasefire Talks.**
    *   US Central Command executed "self-defense strikes" in southern Iran, targeting missile sites and mine-laying boats.
    *   Iran condemned this as a "gross violation" of the fragile April ceasefire, claiming it engaged a US drone and fighter jet.
    *   US President Trump acknowledged ongoing peace talks but warned of escalating military action if a "Great Deal" isn't reached.
    *   Iran's foreign ministry indicated navigation through the critical Strait of Hormuz "will have costs."
    *   Ex-CIA Director David Petraeus suggests Iran is "blinking" but warns against granting them control over the Strait.     *   Shipping through the Strait of Hormuz remains severely curtailed due to the Iran war, leading to price volatility.
    *   Medical supply companies report raw material cost increases of up to 30%, showing broad supply chain impacts.
