## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Ebola Outbreak in DR Congo Reaches "Deeply Alarming" Levels**
    *   The medical charity Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) warned of an unprecedented rapid spread of Ebola in the Democratic Republic of Congo.
    *   Over 1,000 suspected cases and at least 246 deaths have been recorded, with nine confirmed cases and one death in neighboring Uganda.
    *   Ongoing conflict and logistical constraints like border closures are significantly hindering containment and humanitarian efforts.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c99ldxykz4zo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c99ldxykz4zo))
*   **Nvidia Invests Billions in Photonics to Solve AI Bottlenecks**
    *   Nvidia has committed at least $6.5 billion to companies developing photonics technology to enhance AI infrastructure.
    *   Photonics uses light for data transfer, offering a more efficient and less energy-intensive alternative to traditional electrical signals over copper.
    *   This investment aims to address a major bottleneck in AI deployment, though manufacturing at scale presents challenges, with significant adoption expected from 2028.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/29/nvidia-photonics-investment-ai.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/29/nvidia-photonics-investment-ai.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/29/the-tech-download-photonics-nvidia-ai-bottleneck.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/29/the-tech-download-photonics-nvidia-ai-bottleneck.html))
