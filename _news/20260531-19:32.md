## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Israel Expands Lebanon Offensive:**
    *   Israel's military captured Beaufort Castle, a strategic site in southern Lebanon.
    *   PM Netanyahu termed it a "decisive shift" in the offensive against Hezbollah.
    *   Ground troops are moving deeper into Lebanese territory; new evacuation warnings issued for residents.
    *   Lebanon accused Israel of "collective punishment"; France requested a UN Security Council meeting.
    *   The temporary ceasefire between Israel and Lebanon is reportedly "in tatters," with negotiations continuing in Washington.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdep04kzz5wo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdep04kzz5wo))
*   **US Warns Oman Over Strait of Hormuz:**
    *   The Trump administration threatened Oman with sanctions and military action.
    *   This follows reports of Oman potentially cooperating with Iran on imposing tolls in the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   Oman is a longtime US ally and regional mediator, now thrust into geopolitical spotlight.
    *   Iran's Foreign Minister expressed "solidarity" with Oman against US threats.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/29/oman-trump-sanctions-iran-war-bessent.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/29/oman-trump-sanctions-iran-war-bessent.html))
*   **Iran War & Oil Exports:**
    *   Oil exports through the Strait of Hormuz may not fully recover to pre-war levels.
    *   Geopolitical risks and Iran's asserted control deter commercial shipping.
    *   This blockade has caused the largest oil supply disruption in history.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/30/oil-exports-through-hormuz-might-not-return-to-levels-before-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/30/oil-exports-through-hormuz-might-not-return-to-levels-before-iran-war.html))
*   **US-Iran Deal Stalls:**
    *   President Trump concluded a White House meeting without announcing a "final determination" on a deal to pause the Iran war.
    *   Trump outlined demands including Iran never having nuclear weapons and immediate, toll-free access to the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   Iranian state media pushed back, stating Trump's demands contradicted the agreement's text and insisted on immediate payment of frozen assets.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/29/trump-iran-deal-hormuz-nuclear-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/29/trump-iran-deal-hormuz-nuclear-war.html))
*   **Shangri-La Dialogue Highlights Defense & China:**
    *   The IISS Shangri-La Dialogue concluded with broad agreement on boosting defense spending.
    *   US Secretary of War pushed for allies to spend at least 3.5% of GDP on defense.
    *   China faced criticism for sending a low-level delegation, seen as missing a chance for dialogue.
    *   Japan's Defense Minister rebutted China's "new militarism" claim, citing China's lack of transparency.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/takeways-from-the-2026-shangri-la-dialogue-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/takeways-from-the-2026-shangri-la-dialogue-.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/30/germany-china-shangri-la-dialogue-defense-minister.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/30/germany-china-shangri-la-dialogue-defense-minister.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/japan-seeks-dialog-rejects-neo-militarism-label-defense-minister.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/japan-seeks-dialog-rejects-neo-militarism-label-defense-minister.html))
*   **US Reaffirms Taiwan Support:**

    *   This follows President Trump's remarks calling arms sales to Taiwan a "negotiating chip" with China.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/30/us-support-taiwan-reaffirmed-members-congress.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/30/us-support-taiwan-reaffirmed-members-congress.html))

## Major regional Events

*   **Colombia Presidential Election Underway:**
    *   Colombians are voting for a new president, with potential significant changes to US relations.
    *   Leading candidates include Iván Cepeda (left-wing, "total peace") and Abelardo de la Espriella/Paloma Valencia (right-wing, military crackdown).
    *   Security is high with 408,000 personnel deployed; a polling station was relocated due to a drone attack.
    *   A run-off vote is expected on June 21st.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2027g423glo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2027g423glo))
*   **Deadly Explosion in Myanmar Village:**
    *   At least 55 people were killed and dozens wounded in a blast in Kaung Tat village, Shan State.
    *   The area is controlled by the Ta'ang National Liberation Army (TNLA), which stated the explosion was caused by mining explosives.
    *   Raises concerns about safety regulations for explosives used by rebel groups funding their campaigns.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cglp7p787njo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cglp7p787njo))
*   **Romania Hit by Russian Drone:**
    *   A Russian drone struck an apartment block in Galati, Romania, injuring two people.
    *   Romania, a NATO and EU member, tracked a swarm of 43 drones, with one changing trajectory after being hit by Ukrainian defenses.
    *   The incident highlights the escalating risk of the Ukraine war extending beyond its borders.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgzyp93jq1o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgzyp93jq1o), [https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c4g5kxl9k1ro](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c4g5kxl9k1ro))
*   **Cuba Suffers Widespread Blackouts:**
    *   Constant power outages are affecting daily life in Cuba due to a US fuel blockade.
    *   High-rise residents are particularly impacted, highlighting severe economic strain.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c809nj57drxo](https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/c809nj57drxo))
*   **Australian Farmers Battle Mouse Plague:**
    *   A severe mouse plague is devastating grain crops across large parts of Australia.
    *   Farmers face significant financial burdens from re-planting and extensive baiting.
    *   The plague is worsened by economic pressures from the Iran war impacting fuel and fertilizer supplies.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c794g80lw74o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c794g80lw74o))
*   **Nigerian General Abducted:**

    *   Reflects challenges for luxury carmakers adapting to the EV market amidst Chinese competition.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1l2y7j7454o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c1l2y7j7454o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/29/ferrari-luce-ev-launch-backlash-stock-drop.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/29/ferrari-luce-ev-launch-backlash-stock-drop.html))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **SoftBank Boosts European AI Investment:**
    *   SoftBank plans €75 billion investment in AI infrastructure in France.
    *   Initial €45 billion over five years aims to build 5 GW of AI data center capacity in northern France.
    *   This is SoftBank's largest AI infrastructure investment in Europe, aiming to close the gap with the US and China.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/softbank-to-build-up-ai-data-centers-in-france-with-major-investment.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/softbank-to-build-up-ai-data-centers-in-france-with-major-investment.html))
*   **Mistral AI Explores Own Chip Design:**
    *   French AI startup Mistral AI is considering designing its own chips to control infrastructure and compete with US rivals.
    *   The company also announced a new data center in France for AI model inferencing, part of a €4 billion investment.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/28/mistral-arthur-mensch-design-chips-ai-data-centers.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/28/mistral-arthur-mensch-design-chips-ai-data-centers.html))
*   **Ukraine Leverages AI Drones for Supply Strikes:**
    *   Ukrainian military is increasing attacks on Russian supply convoys using new AI drone technology.
    *   Footage confirms strikes on at least 14 vehicles along critical routes in occupied Ukraine.
    *   Experts note drones like the AI-enabled Hornet system allow for greater range and accuracy.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdjp0n7rn41o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdjp0n7rn41o))
*   **AUKUS Nations Develop Underwater Drones:**
    *   The US, UK, and Australia (AUKUS) are developing uncrewed undersea vehicle (UUV) technology.
    *   UUVs are intended to protect undersea cables and enhance defense capabilities.
    *   The project is part of AUKUS's Pillar Two, seen as countering China's maritime presence.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y8wjvd1ypo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y8wjvd1ypo))
*   **Ebola Outbreak Alarms DR Congo:**
    *   Médecins Sans Frontières warned of a "deeply alarming" rapid spread of Ebola in DR Congo.
    *   Over 1,000 suspected cases and 246 deaths have been recorded, with 9 cases and 1 death in Uganda.
    *   Ongoing conflict in DR Congo significantly impedes containment efforts.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c99ldxykz4zo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c99ldxykz4zo))
*   **Trump Fast-Tracks Psychedelic Research:**
    *   President Trump issued an executive order to accelerate research into psychedelic drugs for mental healthcare.
    *   Aims to explore treatments for PTSD and depression, marking a shift from previous administration policy.
    *   Priority review vouchers were granted to three companies developing related therapies.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/why-trump-is-encouraging-psychedelics-for-mental-health.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/why-trump-is-encouraging-psychedelics-for-mental-health.html))
*   **New Lung Cancer Drug Shows Promise in China:**
    *   An experimental lung cancer drug, ivonescimab (Akeso/Summit Therapeutics), reduced the risk of death by 34% in a late-stage trial in China.
    *   The drug improved survival by four months for patients with squamous non-small-cell lung cancer.
    *   Global trials are ongoing, with questions about the applicability of these results to other populations.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/asco-summit-akeso-ivonescimab-improves-survival-in-harmoni-6-trial.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/31/asco-summit-akeso-ivonescimab-improves-survival-in-harmoni-6-trial.html))

    *   Foundation Future Industries, a Trump-linked startup, is developing humanoid robots for military and hazardous industrial work.
    *   Early versions of their Phantom MK-1 robots are undergoing tests in Ukraine for logistics.
    *   The company aims for US military deployment within 12-18 months, raising ethical concerns about autonomous combat decisions.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/30/humanoid-robots-ukraine-war-foundation-military-ai.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/30/humanoid-robots-ukraine-war-foundation-military-ai.html))
