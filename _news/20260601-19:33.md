## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Tensions and Ceasefire Strain**
    *   Iran warned Israeli attacks against Hezbollah in Lebanon could jeopardize its ceasefire with the US.

      *   Israeli Prime Minister ordered strikes on Beirut's southern suburbs in response to Hezbollah attacks.

        *   Damage includes state-of-the-art THAAD anti-ballistic missile batteries, E-3 Sentry surveillance planes, and extensive infrastructure.
        *   Iran's tactics have evolved towards more precise, directed attacks, raising concerns about US military complacency.
    *   **France Intercepts Russian Oil Tanker:**
      *   France, with UK support, seized a sanctioned Russian "shadow fleet" oil tanker, the *Tagor*, in the Atlantic.
        *   The tanker was reportedly flying a false flag as part of efforts to evade international sanctions on Russian oil exports.
        *   Russia condemned the seizure as "illegal" and bordering on "international piracy."

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Ebola Outbreak and Vaccine Development**
    *   Three new vaccines are in development to combat the rare Bundibugyo Ebola species.
      *   The outbreak in DR Congo has over 1,000 suspected cases and 246 deaths, with 9 confirmed cases in neighboring Uganda, posing a significant global health threat.
      *   University of Oxford, Moderna, and the International Aids Vaccine Initiative (IAVI) are accelerating vaccine research and trials.
    *   Brazil is monitoring two patients in São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro for possible Ebola infection, after arrivals from DR Congo and Uganda.
      *   One patient tested positive for meningitis, the other for malaria, but Ebola infection is not ruled out.
    *   Impact: Urgent global health concern and rapid mobilization of scientific resources to prevent a wider epidemic.
