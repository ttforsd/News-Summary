## UK Political & Social Landscape

*   **SNP Embezzlement Scandal Unfolds**
    *   Peter Murrell, former SNP chief executive and Nicola Sturgeon's estranged husband, pleaded guilty to embezzling over £400,000 from the party.
    *   Details: Funds used for personal luxury items over 12 years via fake invoices and bank transfers.
    *   Impact: First Minister John Swinney called it a "colossal breach of trust"; sentencing due in June. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgmp0pxyze8o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgmp0pxyze8o))

    *   Impact: Raises concerns about government transparency and due diligence in high-level appointments. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn4p9pv13kdo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn4p9pv13kdo))

*   **Post Office Scandal Victim Honored**

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **New Ebola Vaccines in Development**
    *   Three new vaccines are being developed to tackle the rare Bundibugyo species of Ebola.
    *   Threat: Outbreak in DR Congo killed nearly 250, with cases spreading to Uganda.
    *   Impact: Urgent global health effort aims to prevent the outbreak from becoming the worst ever. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn8pw93929wo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cn8pw93929wo))

*   **Pancreatic Cancer Survival Doubles with New Pill**
    *   A daily pill, daraxonrasib, nearly doubled the average survival time for advanced pancreatic cancer patients in trials.
    *   Mechanism: Targets the mutated KRAS gene, present in over 90% of pancreatic tumors.
    *   Impact: Potential "game changer" for a highly fatal cancer, offering longer survival with fewer side-effects. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy82l435171o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy82l435171o))

*   **DNA Test Reduces Chemotherapy for Breast Cancer**
    *   A new DNA test, Prosigna, can identify breast cancer patients who can safely forgo chemotherapy.
    *   Findings: Two-thirds of trial participants achieved similar survival rates with hormone therapy alone (93.7% five-year survival).
    *   Impact: Could spare thousands of patients annually from harsh chemotherapy side effects; hailed as "practice-changing." (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2325j0xk1vo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2325j0xk1vo))

*   **Expanded Prostate Cancer Screening Trial for Black Men**
    *   The UK will invest £18 million to expand a prostate cancer screening trial for black men aged 45-74.
    *   Rationale: Black men face double the risk of prostate cancer (1 in 4 lifetime risk).
    *   Objective: Develop a more effective screening system and address health disparities. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g91jrl349o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4g91jrl349o))

*   **Instagram AI Chatbot Hacked**

    *   Method: Hackers faked location and tricked the AI into providing verification codes.
    *   Impact: Meta states the issue is resolved; raises concerns about AI security and reliance on automated support. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c98rzr72dpyo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c98rzr72dpyo))
