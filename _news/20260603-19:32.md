## Executive Summary

*   **Middle East Tensions Escalate:** Iranian drone strikes hit Kuwait airport, killing one and injuring dozens, in retaliation for U.S. actions. Meanwhile, Israeli strikes killed nine in Lebanon, testing a partial ceasefire with Hezbollah.

*   **U.S. Imposes New Tariffs:** The U.S. proposed tariffs of 10-12.5% on 60 trading partners, including the UK, EU, China, and India, citing concerns over forced labor in supply chains.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Middle East Conflict Intensifies:**
    *   Iranian drone strikes on Kuwait International Airport killed one person and injured over 60, with Kuwait expelling two Iranian diplomats.
    *   Iran's IRGC claimed responsibility, stating retaliation for U.S. strikes on an Iranian oil tanker and Qeshm Island.
    *   The U.S. confirmed "self-defense" strikes on Iranian targets and intercepted missiles/drones aimed at Kuwait and Bahrain.
    *   **Impact:** Escalates tensions, threatens a fragile U.S.-Iran ceasefire, and influences global oil prices. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yx135yg53o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yx135yg53o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/03/us-iran-war-escalates-peace-talks-stalemate-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/03/us-iran-war-escalates-peace-talks-stalemate-.html))
*   **Israel-Hezbollah Ceasefire Under Strain:**
    *   Israeli strikes killed at least nine people in southern Lebanon, including two paramedics and a soldier.
    *   Hezbollah retaliated with rockets into northern Israel, challenging a partial ceasefire brokered by the U.S.
    *   **Context:** U.S. President Trump reportedly pressured Israeli PM Netanyahu to halt bombings on Beirut to de-escalate. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y01pdqvkgo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y01pdqvkgo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c936zvne0l6o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c936zvne0l6o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/03/oil-prices-trump-pushes-back-on-reports-us-iran-talks-collapsed.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/03/oil-prices-trump-pushes-back-on-reports-us-iran-talks-collapsed.html))
*   **Iran Nuclear Deal Claims:**
    *   President Trump stated Iran "already agreed" not to have nuclear weapons, though Iran's Foreign Ministry called this "misleading."
    *   **Context:** U.S. Secretary of State Rubio indicated ongoing talks could address Iran's nuclear program. (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/03/trump-iran-war-nuclear-weapon.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/03/trump-iran-war-nuclear-weapon.html))
*   **British Couple's Espionage Appeal Rejected in Iran:**
    *   A British couple jailed for 10 years in Iran on espionage charges lost their appeal, with no reason given.
    *   **Context:** They deny the charges and are on hunger strike; the UK Foreign Office calls their detention "unjustified." (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjrpwxe82ypo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjrpwxe82ypo))

## Major Regional Events

*   **Ukraine War Developments:**
    *   Ukrainian drones struck targets in Russia's St. Petersburg region, including an oil terminal and naval base.
    *   **Context:** The attacks occurred as Russia's flagship International Economic Forum opened, aiming to showcase the country to foreign investors. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0e2vqd3j1vo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0e2vqd3j1vo))
    *   Eight people were killed and 10 injured in a drone strike on a passenger bus in Russian-occupied Donetsk.
    *   **Context:** Russia blamed Ukraine, who neither confirmed nor denied, framing it as Russian propaganda. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp3peyykxd5o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cp3peyykxd5o))
    *   Public discourse in Russia shows signs of changing, with some in state-controlled media questioning the "unachievable" goals of the "special military operation." (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy82339793go](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cy82339793go))
*   **Nigeria Church Attack Verdict:**
    *   Four men were sentenced to death for the 2022 attack on a Catholic church in Owo, Nigeria, that killed 41 worshippers.
    *   **Context:** They were convicted of terrorism and murder, though the government denies persecution of Christians. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj0p57q1m36o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj0p57q1m36o))
*   **Shell's Nigeria Pipeline Pollution Allegations:**

    *   **Detail:** A senior executive raised risks in 2008, and sections were "red-flagged" for oil theft connections in 2012.
    *   **Impact:** Local communities are seeking $1 billion in compensation and cleanup costs in a UK lawsuit. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdrp8v7407ro](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cdrp8v7407ro))
*   **UK Political Risk and Markets:**
    *   A frontrunner for UK Prime Minister, Andy Burnham, cancelled a call with investors, raising concerns about potential fiscal shifts.
    *   **Impact:** Analysts warn of "very high" market risk if Burnham, perceived as left-leaning, wins, as the UK already has the highest borrowing costs in the G7. (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/03/uk-assets-face-underestimated-risk-event-analysts-warn-as-pm-hopeful-swerves-call-to-calm-investor-nerves.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/03/uk-assets-face-underestimated-risk-event-analysts-warn-as-pm-hopeful-swerves-call-to-calm-investor-nerves.html))

    *   **Context:** The RBA has already hiked rates to 4.35%, and the Middle East conflict's effect on energy costs could further slow growth. ## Global Economic & Market Movers

    *   **Impact:** The EU deemed the tariffs "unjustified"; an India analyst called them "pressure tactics."
