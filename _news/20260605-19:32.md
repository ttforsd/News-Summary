*   **Middle East Ceasefire Rejected, Escalation Seen:** Hezbollah rejected a US-backed ceasefire between Israel and Lebanon, labeling negotiations "futile," as an Iranian drone strike hit Kuwait International Airport, killing one. Shipping through the Strait of Hormuz remains disrupted.

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Ukraine-Russia Peace Talks Stalled:**
    *   Russian President Putin rejected Ukrainian President Zelensky's proposal for direct peace talks, calling the letter "rude."
    *   Putin maintained preconditions for peace, stating a ceasefire would only allow Ukraine to regroup.
    *   Zelensky expressed disappointment, saying Russia is "choosing war again."
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpqpqx5qe40o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpqpqx5qe40o))
*   **Middle East Ceasefire Falters Amid Escalation:**
    *   Hezbollah rejected a US-backed ceasefire deal between Israel and Lebanon, calling negotiations "futile" and "humiliating."
    *   The deal proposed "pilot" security zones in Lebanon from which Hezbollah operatives would be banned.
    *   An Iranian drone strike hit Kuwait International Airport, killing one person and injuring over 60, prompting Kuwait to condemn it as "criminal Iranian aggression."
    *   US President Trump indicated willingness to meet Iran's Supreme Leader Mojtaba Khamenei for a deal, despite ongoing hostilities.
    *   Dozens of commercial ships exited the Persian Gulf with US Navy coordination, but traffic through the Strait of Hormuz remains below pre-war levels.

    *   Aims to strengthen ties amid international sanctions on Pyongyang and its growing alliance with Russia.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **US Jobs Growth Exceeds Expectations:**
    *   The US economy added 172,000 jobs in May, significantly surpassing the 80,000 forecast, driven largely by leisure and hospitality.
    *   Unemployment remained steady at 4.3%; average hourly earnings rose 3.4%, below the 3.8% inflation rate.
    *   Strong job figures increased market predictions of a potential Federal Reserve interest rate hike this year.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/hospitality-jobs-boom-as-us-prepares-for-world-cup.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/hospitality-jobs-boom-as-us-prepares-for-world-cup.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/odds-of-a-fed-hike-this-year-jump-on-prediction-markets-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/odds-of-a-fed-hike-this-year-jump-on-prediction-markets-.html))
*   **Global Tech Stocks Decline:**
    *   A worldwide tech sell-off, triggered by Broadcom's weaker-than-expected earnings, spread to European and Asian markets.
    *   South Korea's Kospi index fell over 5%, with major chipmakers like Samsung Electronics and SK Hynix experiencing significant drops.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/global-tech-sell-off-euopean-markets.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/global-tech-sell-off-euopean-markets.html))
*   **Bitcoin Falls Below $60,000:**
    *   Bitcoin dropped below $60,000, reaching its lowest price since October 2024, set for a 17% weekly loss.
    *   This decline was influenced by MicroStrategy's Bitcoin sale, significant liquidations, and increased fears of Fed rate hikes.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/bitcoin-dismal-week-price-below-all-time-high-crypto-investors.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/bitcoin-dismal-week-price-below-all-time-high-crypto-investors.html))
*   **India's Economic Performance and Policy:**
    *   India's economy grew 7.8% in the first quarter (Jan-Mar), exceeding forecasts.
    *   The central bank raised its inflation forecast for FY27 to 5.1% and cut the growth outlook to 6.6%, citing risks from the Middle East conflict and rising energy prices.
    *   India exempted foreign investors from capital gains tax on government securities to attract foreign capital and stabilize the rupee amidst record outflows.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/india-gdp-economy-oil-middle-east-disruption.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/india-gdp-economy-oil-middle-east-disruption.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/india-rbi-rate-gdp-inflation.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/india-rbi-rate-gdp-inflation.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/india-tax-overseas-bond-investors.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/india-tax-overseas-bond-investors.html))

*   **Flesh-Eating Screwworm Outbreak in US:**
    *   The US confirmed a New World Screwworm outbreak in Texas, the first since 1966, detected in a calf.

*   **Mangrove Forests Show Recovery:**

    *   These vital ecosystems store carbon and protect coastlines, though some growth is linked to upstream environmental damage.
