## Major Regional Events

    *   Ukraine deployed over 140 drones targeting St. Petersburg and the surrounding Leningrad region, Russia's second-largest city.
    *   President Zelensky stated targets included Russian arsenals and a naval base in Kronstadt.
    *   Impact: Significant escalation in the Russia-Ukraine conflict, extended reach of Ukrainian strikes, and disruption during a major economic forum. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cg7498kz808o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cg7498kz808o))
*   **Escalation in Israeli-Lebanese Conflict:**
    *   An Israeli attack in southern Lebanon killed a Lebanese brigadier-general and two other soldiers.
    *   The IDF is investigating, stating the vehicle was in an "active combat zone" and troop movements require coordination.
    *   Impact: Heightened tensions and military confrontation between Israel and Lebanon, despite ongoing ceasefire talks between the governments. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj0g8jymg92o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj0g8jymg92o))
*   **Palestinian Baby Killed in West Bank:**

    *   The Israeli military stated soldiers "perceived a threat" from the family's car but acknowledged they were "uninvolved civilians."
    *   Impact: Fuels outrage and further instability in the already volatile Israeli-Palestinian territories. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly8lgn0x30o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly8lgn0x30o))
*   **Peru's Presidential Election Driven by Insecurity:**
    *   Peru is holding a tight presidential race with insecurity and crime as top voter concerns.
    *   Right-wing Keiko Fujimori promises hardline crime policies, while left-wing Roberto Sánchez advocates for state changes.
    *   Impact: Political polarization and uncertainty about future stability and economic direction in a nation with high government turnover. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9d21184e7yo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9d21184e7yo))
*   **Somalia's Ex-Child Soldiers Face Trauma Amidst Ongoing Conflict:**
    *   Adult survivors of child soldiering in Somalia continue to suffer from psychological scars, with limited mental health resources.
    *   The country remains embroiled in conflict, with armed groups still recruiting children, highlighting a "never-ending cycle of violence."
    *   Impact: Deep humanitarian crisis, long-term societal instability, and lack of support for affected populations in Somalia. (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c98r7zxdxe7o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c98r7zxdxe7o))
