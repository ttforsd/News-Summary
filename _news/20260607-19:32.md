## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Israel Strikes Beirut, Escalating Regional Tensions**
    *   Israel conducted air strikes in southern Beirut, killing two and injuring 17 in Iran-backed Hezbollah strongholds.
    *   This marks the first attack on the Lebanese capital since a US-brokered truce last week.
    *   The US expressed concern these strikes could jeopardize efforts for a wider peace deal with Iran.
    *   Iran vowed a "decisive and painful response"; Lebanon's Parliament Speaker rejected the truce, and Hezbollah reaffirmed its refusal to disarm.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0qyevk8139o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0qyevk8139o))
*   **Iran Threatens Red Sea Choke Point**
    *   Iran's Revolutionary Guard threatened to close the Bab el-Mandeb Strait, a vital Red Sea oil shipping route.
    *   Such a closure would severely disrupt global oil supplies, already strained by the Strait of Hormuz closure.
    *   The threat came after Hezbollah rejected a recent US-brokered ceasefire in Lebanon.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/iran-war-oil-price-strait-hormuz-bab-el-mandeb-chokepoint.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/iran-war-oil-price-strait-hormuz-bab-el-mandeb-chokepoint.html))
*   **Ukraine Seeks European Support Amidst Ongoing Conflict**
    *   Ukrainian President Zelensky arrived in the UK for talks with Prime Minister Starmer, French President Macron, and German Chancellor Merz.
    *   Discussions centered on Ukraine's defense, air defense cooperation, and diplomatic prospects.
    *   Russia rejected Zelensky's call for direct peace talks, stating there was "no point" in a meeting.
    *   Ukraine accused Russia of a drone strike on a spent nuclear fuel storage facility near Chornobyl, though radiation levels remained stable.
    *   Russian strikes killed at least three near Zaporizhzhia, while Ukraine conducted drone attacks on St. Petersburg.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cr7xr1g3nvvo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cr7xr1g3nvvo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9q2gp52rgro](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9q2gp52rgro))
*   **US Defense Secretary Criticizes European Migration Policy**
    *   US Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth criticized European nations during a D-Day anniversary speech in France.
    *   He described migrant arrivals on European shores as an "invasion."
    *   This follows similar comments from the US Vice-President, highlighting increased US pressure on European immigration policies.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c802e7jk458o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c802e7jk458o))
*   **Trump Renews Criticism of Iran Nuclear Deal**
    *   Former President Trump reiterated his criticism of the Obama-era Iran nuclear deal (JCPOA), from which he withdrew the US in 2018.
    *   He claims a future deal under his administration would be "far better," despite the ongoing Iran war and no current resolution.
    *   Many national security experts argue the JCPOA successfully limited Iran's nuclear activities and enabled effective monitoring.
    *   Since the US withdrawal, Iran has breached JCPOA limits, including increasing uranium enrichment to 60%.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/06/trump-iran-jcpoa-nuclear-deal-obama.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/06/trump-iran-jcpoa-nuclear-deal-obama.html))

    *   Strong job data is likely to deter the Federal Reserve from near-term interest rate cuts.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/jobs-report-may-2026.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/05/jobs-report-may-2026.html))
*   **AI Drives US Stock Market Highs, But Volatility Rises**
    *   AI investment continues to push US stock markets to all-time highs, despite geopolitical uncertainty and inflation.
    *   However, market volatility (VIX) surged as a major rally in semiconductor stocks reversed, sparking concerns over speculative excess.
    *   S&P 500 index options trading reached a record high, and bearish bets increased in the bond market after strong employment data.

    *   PCB prices already rose 40% between March and April, with few high-end resin alternatives available.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/07/inflation-iran-war-consumer-economy-electronics.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/07/inflation-iran-war-consumer-economy-electronics.html))
