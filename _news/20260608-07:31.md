## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Ukraine Peace Conditions Set by European Allies**
    *   UK, France, and Germany, met with President Zelensky, outlined five conditions for a "just and lasting" peace with Russia.
    *   Conditions include an end to fighting, negotiations based on current field positions, and robust security guarantees for Ukraine.
    *   Leaders stressed the need for US participation in the peace process.
    *   Impact: Continued diplomatic pressure for peace, while acknowledging battlefield realities.

*   **Xi Jinping to Visit North Korea Amid Shifting Alliances**

    *   China seeks to reassert influence over Pyongyang amid growing partnership between North Korea and Russia.
    *   North Korea may leverage the visit for economic concessions and tacit recognition of its nuclear status.
