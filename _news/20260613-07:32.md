## International Relations & Global Security

*   **US-Iran Peace Negotiations and Renewed Hostilities**
    *   Iran's Foreign Minister claims a deal to end fighting and reopen the Strait of Hormuz is close.
    *   US officials are more cautious, indicating an 80-85% chance of a Memorandum of Understanding being signed soon.
    *   President Trump denied Iranian state media's reported 14-point deal terms, calling them untrue.
    *   The deal involves lifting a US blockade and ending Iranian funding of proxy groups; Iran insists on immediate fund release.
