## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Deal Signed Amid Unresolved Issues:**
    *   A preliminary agreement between the US and Iran was signed, aiming to end four months of conflict.
    *   Details of the memorandum of understanding are not yet public, with a formal signing set for Friday in Geneva.
    *   US President Trump indicated willingness to share deal specifics with Congress, which is demanding a review.
    *   Iran's leadership frames the deal as a victory achieved through resistance, while many Iranians see it as a necessity due to severe economic pressure.
    *   **Strait of Hormuz:** The Strait's full reopening faces challenges including remaining mines, ongoing security concerns (Iran fired on ships, US naval blockade still in place), and disputes over potential future tolls.
        *   Only 7 vessels have passed since the announcement, with hundreds waiting.
        *   US expects a long-term "toll-free" passage, while Iran's state media suggests a 60-day toll-free period followed by "Iranian arrangements."
        *   Mine clearing operations, with UK support, could take months.
    *   **Lebanon Ceasefire:** A fragile quiet holds in Lebanon, but Israeli Prime Minister Netanyahu rejects withdrawing forces from southern Lebanon, complicating the truce.

## Security

*   **FBI Thwarts White House Attack Plot:**
    *   The FBI successfully disrupted a plot targeting a UFC event at the White House using snipers and explosive-laden drones.
    *   Five suspects linked to an "ultra-religious and antigovernment" group are in custody.
    *   The plot aimed to create panic and direct crowds towards a sniper team, with a "second wave" planned to storm the White House.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqx10xlje1lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqx10xlje1lo))
*   **Russian Warship Fires Warning Shots Near UK Yacht:**
    *   A Russian frigate, the Admiral Grigorovich, fired warning shots near a UK-registered yacht in the English Channel.
    *   The Russian Defense Ministry stated the yacht made a "dangerous approach" in foggy conditions.
    *   The incident is being investigated by the UK Ministry of Defence and follows a recent UK interception of a Russian "shadow fleet" oil tanker.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c20yzm84r7lo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c20yzm84r7lo))
*   **US B-52 Bomber Crashes in California:**
    *   A US Air Force B-52 bomber crashed after take-off from Edwards Air Force Base, killing all eight people on board (military and civilian crew).
    *   The aircraft was on a routine test mission for a radar modernization program.
    *   The cause is under investigation; the B-52 fleet has participated in recent bombing raids during the Iran conflict.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqx10yql319o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqx10yql319o))
*   **Air India Crash Investigation Continues:**
    *   One year after Air India Flight 171 crashed, killing 260, the investigation remains unresolved with key questions unanswered.
    *   Focus is on fuel-control switches moving to "cut-off" post-take-off, with speculation on pilot action vs. technical failure.
    *   **Sources:** ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c6214prydklo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c6214prydklo))
