    *   An experimental CAR-T cell therapy, which resets the immune system, has shown promising results in UK trials for lupus patients.
    *   Impact: This breakthrough offers a "significant step forward" towards a possible cure and has potential applications for other autoimmune diseases like multiple sclerosis.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gy2d9y5z3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gy2d9y5z3o))

    *   A one-off program will offer the Meningitis B vaccine to one million young people across the UK.
    *   Detail: Eligible groups include Year 13/S6 pupils and first-time university or residential further education entrants aged 18-25.
    *   Context: The initiative follows several "unusual" Meningitis B outbreaks in Kent, Dorset, and Reading.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c982352my98o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c982352my98o))

*   **Oral Weight-Loss Drug Wegovy Approved in UK**
    *   The pill version of Wegovy (semaglutide), an effective weight-loss drug, has been approved for use in the UK.
    *   Detail: This marks the first oral GLP-1 medication for weight management, offering a convenient alternative to injections.
    *   Context: While approved, it is not yet available on the NHS, pending assessment, but private pharmacies may soon offer it.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9d2pl10lqzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9d2pl10lqzo))

*   **New Drug Aims to Counter Muscle Loss from Obesity Jabs**
    *   Apitegromab, an experimental drug, is showing promise in trials for preventing muscle loss, a common side effect of GLP-1 obesity medications (e.g., Wegovy, Ozempic).
    *   Impact: Could mitigate issues like "Ozempic butt" by helping patients retain more lean mass while losing fat.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62r285l46eo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62r285l46eo))

*   **Advanced Radiotherapy for Prostate Cancer to Reduce Sessions**
    *   England will begin offering Stereotactic Ablative Radiotherapy (SABR) for low- and intermediate-risk prostate cancer.
    *   Impact: This precision treatment will cut the number of sessions from typically 20 to just five, improving patient experience and outcomes.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/crm02mmgr3ro](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/crm02mmgr3ro))
