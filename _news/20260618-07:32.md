## Executive Summary

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Ceasefire and Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) Signed**
    *   Presidents Trump and Pezeshkian digitally signed an MOU establishing an immediate and permanent ceasefire.
    *   The agreement sets a maximum 60-day period, extendable by mutual consent, to negotiate a final peace deal.
    *   Iran commits to not developing nuclear weapons and to "downblending" its enriched uranium under IAEA supervision.
    *   A $300 billion reconstruction fund for Iran is envisioned with regional partners, though the US is not directly required to contribute.
    *   The MOU includes an immediate end to military operations in Lebanon and states the US will remove its naval blockade and forces from Iran's proximity within 30 days of a final deal.
    *   Impact: Significant de-escalation of a major conflict, re-shaping Middle East dynamics.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgmqzr6p9mo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgmqzr6p9mo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgmd8dgklzzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgmd8dgklzzo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/trump-vance-iran-deal-nuclear-g7.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/trump-vance-iran-deal-nuclear-g7.html))

*   **Strait of Hormuz Reopening and Threat Level Downgrade**
    *   The US-Iran MOU mandates immediate, toll-free passage for commercial vessels through the Strait of Hormuz for 60 days.
    *   Iran will then collaborate with Oman and other Gulf states to define long-term administration, adhering to international law.
    *   The US is committed to lifting its naval blockade of Iranian ports within 30 days.
    *   The US-led Joint Maritime Information Center (JMIC) downgraded the threat level in the Strait to "substantial" from "severe."
    *   Impact: Expected to ease global oil supply concerns, though full shipping normalization may take months.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgmqzr6p9mo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgmqzr6p9mo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/iran-mou-deal-strait-hormuz-oman-toll.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/iran-mou-deal-strait-hormuz-oman-toll.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/iran-deal-strait-hormuz-threat-jmic-ukmto.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/iran-deal-strait-hormuz-threat-jmic-ukmto.html))

*   **Iranian Oil Exports Resume**
    *   Three Iranian tankers, owned by the sanctioned National Iranian Tanker Company, passed the US blockade line in the Gulf of Oman.
    *   These vessels are carrying 3.8 million barrels of crude oil, marking Iran's first oil exports in two months.
    *   Impact: Immediate commercial implication of the ceasefire, potentially increasing global oil supply.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpq37yxexd9o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cpq37yxexd9o))

*   **US-India Relations Improve with Announced Trump Visit**
    *   US President Donald Trump announced plans to visit India "sometime in the future," signaling a thawing of relations with Prime Minister Modi.
    *   The leaders met at the G7 summit and discussed progress on an impending trade deal.
    *   Impact: Potential for strengthened bilateral ties and economic cooperation.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c621glv9vnqo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c621glv9vnqo))

*   **UN Experts Call for Release of British Couple from Iranian Jail**

## Major Regional Events

*   **Japan Enhances Defense and Reconsiders Pacifist Constitution**
    *   Defence Minister Shinjiro Koizumi stated Japan must strengthen its defense capabilities and alliance with the US.
    *   The country is relaxing decades-old arms export rules to allow lethal weapon sales to 17 countries.
    *   Prime Minister Sanae Takaichi supports revising Article 9 of the constitution, which renounces war.
    *   China's military moves and North Korea's nuclear ambitions are cited as primary security challenges.
    *   Impact: A major departure from Japan's post-WWII pacifism, potentially reshaping Indo-Pacific security.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly8qd4595eo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly8qd4595eo))

*   **Escalating Tensions at Jerusalem's Al-Aqsa Mosque Compound**
    *   Israeli nationalist politicians are openly flouting rules and calling for a new Jewish temple on the al-Aqsa compound.
    *   The site, known to Muslims as al-Haram al-Sharif and to Jews as the Temple Mount, is currently reserved for Muslim prayer under the "Status Quo."
    *   Israeli National Security Minister Itamar Ben-Gvir has led Jewish prayers on the site, sparking outrage.
    *   Impact: Risks igniting widespread religious and political conflict, drawing international condemnation and concern.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy0nlv90jno](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwy0nlv90jno))

*   **Israel Conducts Fresh Strikes on Lebanon Amid US Criticism**
    *   Israeli forces carried out new strikes in southern Lebanon, with drone strikes injuring several and jets attacking towns.
    *   US President Trump publicly criticized Israeli Prime Minister Netanyahu, urging "more responsibility" and stating that "too many people are being killed."
    *   Hezbollah declared the recent US-Iran agreement a "great victory" for Lebanon.
    *   Impact: Continued regional instability, challenging the broader US-Iran ceasefire efforts.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c7vyn17g832o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c7vyn17g832o))

*   **Ebola Outbreak Worsens in DR Congo Amid Attacks on Hospitals**

    *   The incident highlights rampant suspicion and fear towards Ebola treatment centers in the region, fueled by misinformation.
    *   The current Bundibugyo Ebola outbreak has recorded almost 200 deaths and 840 cases.
    *   Impact: Exacerbates the public health crisis and complicates control efforts, especially in conflict-affected areas.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgep4l397eo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgep4l397eo))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Federal Reserve Maintains Rates, Warsh Signals Hawkish Stance**
    *   New Fed Chairman Kevin Warsh kept benchmark interest rates steady at 3.5%-3.75% in his first FOMC meeting.
    *   The "dot plot" revealed a 9-9 split among officials, with half projecting a potential rate hike later in 2026.
    *   Warsh, who abstained from his own forecast, emphasized "unambiguous and unanimous" resolve for "price stability" and announced task forces to overhaul Fed operations and communications.
    *   Impact: Markets reacted negatively to the hawkish tone, with the S&P 500 experiencing its worst "Fed day" performance for a new chair since 1994.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/here-are-the-five-big-takeaways-from-kevin-warshs-first-meeting-as-fed-chairman.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/here-are-the-five-big-takeaways-from-kevin-warshs-first-meeting-as-fed-chairman.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/warsh-fed-meeting-stock-market.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/warsh-fed-meeting-stock-market.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/fed-projections-call-for-a-rate-hike-in-2026-but-chairman-warsh-likely-abstained.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/fed-projections-call-for-a-rate-hike-in-2026-but-chairman-warsh-likely-abstained.html))

*   **Oil Prices Decline on Supply Glut Forecast Post-Iran Deal**
    *   Global oil prices fell after the US-Iran deal, amplified by the IEA's forecast of a significant supply overhang in 2027.
    *   The IEA downgraded its 2026 oil demand outlook but anticipates a strong rebound in supply post-conflict.
    *   Impact: Immediate relief for energy prices, but the full recovery of oil inventories and shipping lanes will take time.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/18/oil-prices-today-wti-brent.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/18/oil-prices-today-wti-brent.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/global-oil-demand-suppy-energy-prices-iea-inventories.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/global-oil-demand-suppy-energy-prices-iea-inventories.html))

*   **China's Cheap Money Attracts Foreign Borrowers**
    *   Wall Street banks and foreign governments are increasingly issuing "panda bonds" in China's domestic market.
    *   This surge is driven by China's significantly lower interest rates compared to dollar borrowing costs.
    *   Beijing's easing of capital restrictions facilitates easier repatriation of yuan proceeds, supporting the internationalization of its currency.
    *   Impact: Enhances the yuan's global financial role and provides attractive financing options for international entities.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/18/china-panda-bonds-yuan-funding-costs-foreign-borrowers.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/18/china-panda-bonds-yuan-funding-costs-foreign-borrowers.html))

*   **Japan's Exports Soar, Led by Semiconductors and Cars**
    *   Japan's exports grew 17% year-on-year in May, marking the fastest pace in over three years and exceeding market estimates.
    *   Strong global demand for semiconductors (+61.2%) and cars (+16.4%) were the primary drivers.
    *   Exports to China and the US saw notable increases, while those to the Middle East declined by 32% due to the Iran war.
    *   The Bank of Japan recently raised interest rates to 1%, its highest in over 30 years, amidst a weak yen.
    *   Impact: Positive economic indicator for Japan, but global growth slowdown and yen's weakness remain potential headwinds.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/japan-trade-data-may-exports-imports.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/japan-trade-data-may-exports-imports.html))

*   **India's National Stock Exchange Files for Major IPO**
    *   India's largest stock exchange, the National Stock Exchange (NSE), has filed papers for an initial public offering (IPO).
    *   The IPO, structured as an "offer for sale" by existing large investors, is anticipated to be one of the year's largest listings.
    *   Impact: Signals renewed investor confidence and a revitalization of India's IPO market, which was previously subdued due to regional conflicts.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/18/nse-ipo-india-jio-listing-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/18/nse-ipo-india-jio-listing-.html))

*   **UK Inflation Holds Steady in May**
    *   The UK's Consumer Price Index (CPI) remained at 2.8% in May, slightly below Reuters' forecast of 3%.
    *   Increased transport costs, including airfares and motor fuel, were the largest contributors to inflation.
    *   Impact: While below expectations, the Bank of England is still expected to hold interest rates this week, with potential for hikes later in the year.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/inflation-uk-cpi-interest-rates-bank-of-england.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/inflation-uk-cpi-interest-rates-bank-of-england.html))

*   **Central Banks Increase Domestic Gold Holdings Amid Geopolitical Risks**
    *   A World Gold Council survey reveals that central banks globally are increasing their gold reserves and opting for domestic storage or diversifying overseas holdings.
    *   This trend is driven by heightened geopolitical tensions and concerns over the accessibility of reserves held abroad.
    *   Impact: Reflects a strategic shift in reserve management, emphasizing national asset security and a potential move away from traditional reserve currencies.
    *   Source: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/central-banks-gold-reserves-domestic-storage.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/17/central-banks-gold-reserves-domestic-storage.html))

*   **Former Nigerian Oil Minister Acquitted in UK Bribery Trial**
    *   Diezani Alison-Madueke, ex-Nigerian oil minister, was cleared of bribery charges in the UK after a 13-year investigation.
    *   The defense cited missing documents and delays, arguing the prosecution's case was compromised.
    *   Impact: Concludes a high-profile corruption case, raising questions about international anti-graft efforts.
    *   Source: ([https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c872pwx4x2vo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c872pwx4x2vo))
