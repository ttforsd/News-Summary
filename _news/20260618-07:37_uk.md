## Major Regional & Geopolitical Events

*   **US and Iran Announce Ceasefire Agreement**
    *   President Trump signed a Memorandum of Understanding at the G7, now in effect.
    *   Agreement includes immediate termination of military operations, including in Lebanon.
    *   Iran committed to "downblending" its enriched uranium, but full nuclear weapon details are pending.
    *   US will lift its naval blockade and remove forces from Iran's proximity within 30 days.
    *   Strait of Hormuz to reopen for safe commercial vessel passage.
    *   A $300 billion fund for Iran's reconstruction is included, with the US not required to contribute.
    *   US to terminate all economic sanctions against Iran.
    *   A 60-day negotiation period for a final deal is set, with extension possible.
    *   The deal faces criticism from some US Republicans who call it a "foreign policy blunder."
    *   UK newspapers were critical, describing the deal as a "sellout" with "significant concessions."
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgmqzr6p9mo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgmqzr6p9mo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgmd8dgklzzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cgmd8dgklzzo), [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0qynv35pvzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c0qynv35pvzo))

*   **Ebola Outbreak Worsens in DR Congo, Misinformation Hinders Response**
    *   The Bundibugyo species of Ebola has killed nearly 200 people in Ituri province, DR Congo.
    *   Authorities are struggling against local myths and misinformation impeding safe burial practices.
    *   Hospitals are adapting safe burial protocols to respect cultural norms while preventing spread.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj3gx8jl6d5o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj3gx8jl6d5o))

*   **Europe and UK Brace for Major Heatwave**
    *   A "heat dome" over Europe is bringing temperatures above 30C to parts of the UK.
    *   France could see temperatures up to 40C.
    *   UK Health Security Agency issued heat health alerts for the Midlands and southeast England.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/weather/articles/c79y35nl719o](https://www.bbc.com/weather/articles/c79y35nl719o))

    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjdgl213dpzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjdgl213dpzo))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **ChatGPT Found Vulnerable to Generating Harmful Images**
    *   AI security researchers from Mindgard demonstrated that ChatGPT (GPT-5.4) can be prompted to create sexualized and violent images.
    *   Bypassing OpenAI's safeguards was achieved with relatively simple prompts.
    *   OpenAI has implemented new protections, but circumventions were still found.
    *   This highlights the ongoing challenge in preventing AI models from producing undesirable content.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c802ldjdklzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c802ldjdklzo))

*   **HPV Vaccine Eliminates Cervical Cancer Deaths in Young Women in England**
    *   A study in The Lancet confirms zero cervical cancer deaths in vaccinated women aged 20-24 between 2020 and 2024.
    *   Approximately 200 lives have been saved since the vaccine's introduction in 2008.
    *   Vaccination rates (76% of girls by age 15) remain below the WHO's 90% target.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c621z28z138o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c621z28z138o))

*   **Revolutionary Immune Therapy Puts Lupus in Remission**
    *   Experimental CAR-T cell therapy has achieved remission in early UK trials for lupus patients.
    *   The treatment works by resetting the immune system to stop it attacking the body.
    *   Shows potential for treating other autoimmune diseases like multiple sclerosis and rheumatoid arthritis.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gy2d9y5z3o](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c4gy2d9y5z3o))

*   **Advanced Radiotherapy for Prostate Cancer to Reduce Treatment Sessions**
    *   High-powered precision radiotherapy (SABR) will be offered to thousands of men with low- and intermediate-risk prostate cancer in England.
    *   The treatment cuts sessions from 20 to five, aiming for greater effectiveness and fewer side-effects.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/crm02mmgr3ro](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/crm02mmgr3ro))

*   **Wegovy Weight-Loss Drug Approved in Pill Form for UK Market**
    *   A pill version of semaglutide (Wegovy) has been approved for prescription in the UK.
    *   Offers a more convenient alternative to weekly injections, requiring no refrigeration.
    *   Currently for private prescription, awaiting NHS assessment for wider availability.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9d2pl10lqzo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c9d2pl10lqzo))

*   **New Drug Aims to Combat Muscle Loss from Obesity Jabs**
    *   Apitegromab shows promise in reducing muscle decline, or "Ozempic butt," associated with GLP-1 obesity medications like Wegovy.
    *   GLP-1 drugs can lead to significant muscle loss (up to a third of total weight reduction).
    *   The drug works by blocking a protein involved in muscle breakdown; currently in clinical trials.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62r285l46eo](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c62r285l46eo))
