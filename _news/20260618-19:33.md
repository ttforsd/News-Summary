## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Peace Deal Signed; Hormuz Reopens for 60 Days**
    *   US President Trump and Iranian President Pezeshkian signed a 14-point MOU to end the war that began in February.
    *   The agreement calls for an immediate end to military operations, including in Lebanon, and reopening of the Strait of Hormuz without tolls for 60 days.
    *   The US will lift its naval blockade and sanctions on Iranian oil exports, and release frozen funds.
    *   Iran reaffirms it will not develop nuclear weapons, with enriched material disposition to be mutually agreed upon.
    *   A $300 billion plan for Iran's reconstruction and economic development with regional partners is included, not directly funded by the US.
    *   Critics question the deal's effectiveness, citing unresolved issues like Iran's missile program and regional proxies.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Oil Prices Fall Amid Hormuz Reopening**
    *   Brent and West Texas Intermediate crude futures dropped around 2% after news of the Strait of Hormuz reopening.
    *   Vice President Vance reported 12.5 million barrels passed through the Strait overnight, the highest since the conflict began.
