## Major Regional Events

*   **Western Europe Endures Record Heatwave**
    *   France recorded its hottest day since 1947, with national average temperatures hitting 30C and Paris exceeding 40C.
    *   Tens of thousands of homes in western France experienced power outages due to heat-related incidents.
    *   Over 40 heatwave-related drownings have been reported in France since June 18.
    *   The heatwave is impacting Spain, Italy, the Netherlands, Belgium, and Germany, leading to public health measures and school closures.
    *   Cool-down spots, free cinema tickets, and even chalk on windows are among coping strategies across Europe.
    *   A political debate in France emerged over increased air-conditioning use, balancing comfort with environmental impact.

## Global Economic & Market Movers
