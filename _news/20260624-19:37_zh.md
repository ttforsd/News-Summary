## Major regional Events

*   **歐洲熱浪與基礎設施壓力：**
    *   法國錄得自1947年以來最熱一天，全國平均氣溫達30°C，部分地區超過40°C，西部數萬戶停電。
    *   影響：歐洲多國（西班牙、意大利、英國、荷蘭、比利時、德國）受熱浪影響；法國核電廠因冷卻水問題減產4.1吉瓦。
    *   應對：多地開設「降溫點」，法國部分學校用粉筆塗白窗戶。
    *   挑戰：氣候變化導致歐洲變暖加速，熱浪頻發加劇基礎設施壓力及山火風險。
    *   來源：([https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c78y4102n1zo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c78y4102n1zo), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c4gyqldl3p5o](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c4gyqldl3p5o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/24/france-power-outage-europe-heat-wave.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/24/france-power-outage-europe-heat-wave.html), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cz0j87vpz79o](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cz0j87vpz79o))
