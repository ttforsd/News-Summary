## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US Seeks Iran War Funding Amid Peace Talks Disagreement**
    *   The White House requested $87.6 billion from Congress for urgent Iran war needs and other measures.
    *   This funding faces opposition, especially after a recent congressional vote to curb presidential war powers.
    *   US President Trump criticized Republican senators who voted against his military action.
    *   Iran, however, rejected US claims of dictating how its unfrozen assets would be spent.

    *   Iran’s Deputy Foreign Minister stated access to damaged nuclear facilities depends on a final deal and sanctions lifting.

## Major regional Events

*   **Deadly Earthquakes Strike Venezuela**
    *   Two powerful earthquakes, measuring 7.2 and 7.5 magnitude, hit Caracas, leading to at least 32 deaths and 700 injuries.
    *   Buildings collapsed, prompting a state of emergency and widespread panic among residents.
    *   US President Trump pledged swift aid, indicating a diplomatic warming with the interim government.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/czj8vjvmz38o](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/czj8vjvmz38o), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/videos/cvgl42lm8dwo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/videos/cvgl42lm8dwo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/25/venezuela-earthquakes-casualties-trump-pledges-support-aids-oil-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/25/venezuela-earthquakes-casualties-trump-pledges-support-aids-oil-.html))
*   **Western Europe Suffers Record Heatwave**
    *   France experienced its hottest day on record, with temperatures reaching up to 43C; the UK also saw its hottest June day.
    *   The heatwave contributed to over 40 drownings in France, widespread power outages, and heightened wildfire risks.
    *   The intense heat has sparked a political debate in France over the necessity and environmental impact of air conditioning.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c78y4102n1zo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c78y4102n1zo), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c4gyqldl3p5o](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c4gyqldl3p5o), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/videos/c30y6qe2qqno](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/videos/c30y6qe2qqno))

    *   This follows ongoing efforts by Ukraine to disrupt logistics and create fuel shortages in Crimea.
