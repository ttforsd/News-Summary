    *   挑戰：醫院因長期資金不足而瀕臨崩潰，不斷上升的死亡人數也引發了公共衛生問題。(Source: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cp8l451420zo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cp8l451420zo), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cdjk8zwe7z3o](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cdjk8zwe7z3o), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/videos/cm2d0rjg4l3o](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/videos/cm2d0rjg4l3o), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/videos/cjdgrrvvvjgo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/videos/cjdgrrvvvjgo), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/videos/cm2d0vzp3ngo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/videos/cm2d0vzp3ngo))

## 全球經濟與市場動向

*   **科技巨頭面臨記憶體晶片短缺：**
    *   影響：全球記憶體晶片短缺導致蘋果（Apple）和微軟（Microsoft）等公司提高關鍵設備價格，小型電子製造商面臨「生存危機」。
    *   原因：人工智能（AI）熱潮使輝達（Nvidia）等晶片製造商消耗大量記憶體，推高了成本。
    *   贏家：記憶體晶片製造商美光（Micron）受益，其動態隨機存取記憶體（DRAM）平均售價同比增長260%，營收翻了四倍，毛利率翻倍至近85%。
    *   股市：晶片股普遍下跌，引發對科技公司利潤率的擔憂。(Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/27/memory-crunch-shaking-apple-and-microsoft-existential-for-small-guys.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/27/memory-crunch-shaking-apple-and-microsoft-existential-for-small-guys.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/26/nvidia-alphabet-sit-out-megacap-tech-bounce-as-chip-stocks-sink.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/26/nvidia-alphabet-sit-out-megacap-tech-bounce-as-chip-stocks-sink.html))
*   **太空經濟就業市場增長迅速：**
    *   增長：全球太空經濟年增長9%，過去十年私人領域就業增長27%，年輕勞工佔新增職位近半。
    *   待遇：該行業薪資較高（年薪中位數10萬至13.5萬美元），並提供股票期權。
    *   挑戰：儘管職位誘人，但由於對熟練製造業和STEM技能的需求，仍有數千個職位空缺，面臨勞動力供需失衡。
    *   公司：SpaceX承認招聘和留住人才面臨挑戰，而其本身已快速納入納斯達克100指數，預計將引發大量ETF買盤。
    *   投資：行業正加大對人才培訓項目的投資，以應對技能短缺問題。(Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/27/spacex-jobs-stem-careers-labor-economy.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/27/spacex-jobs-stem-careers-labor-economy.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/26/spacex-added-to-nasdaq-100.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/26/spacex-added-to-nasdaq-100.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/26/spacex-volatility.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/26/spacex-volatility.html))
