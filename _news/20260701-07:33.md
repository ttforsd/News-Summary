## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US Supreme Court Upholds Birthright Citizenship**
    *   The 6-3 decision rejected President Trump's executive order to limit 14th Amendment citizenship for children of undocumented immigrants.
    *   This ruling is a significant setback for Trump's immigration agenda.
    *   Sources: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cgmepnx1wzzo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cgmepnx1wzzo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/30/supreme-court-birthright-citizenship-ruling.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/30/supreme-court-birthright-citizenship-ruling.html)

*   **US Supreme Court Loosens Campaign Finance Rules**
    *   The court struck down limits on how much political parties can spend in coordination with candidates (6-3 ruling).
    *   This decision expands the role of money in American politics and is seen as a win for Republicans.
    *   Sources: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/30/supreme-court-campaign-finance-republican.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/30/supreme-court-campaign-finance-republican.html), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c4gyqeqe8xyo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c4gyqeqe8xyo)

*   **US-Iran Negotiations Underway in Doha**
    *   US envoys, including Jared Kushner, are meeting mediators in Qatar to discuss a preliminary peace deal.
    *   No direct talks with Iranian officials are scheduled, despite initial claims by President Trump.
    *   The interim agreement aims to end a four-month conflict and reopen the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   Sources: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cpd38x1dy4no](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cpd38x1dy4no), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/30/oil-prices-brent-wti-crude-trump-iran.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/30/oil-prices-brent-wti-crude-trump-iran.html)

*   **North American Free Trade Pact Review Expected to Miss Deadline**
    *   The US, Canada, and Mexico are likely to miss the July 1 deadline for their USMCA trade deal review.
    *   Annual reviews are expected, potentially creating business uncertainty for the $1.6 trillion trade pact.
    *   Sources: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/czdejmqmrdgo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/czdejmqmrdgo)

*   **US, Japan, and Australia Conduct Joint Military Drills**

## Major Regional Events

*   **Venezuela Earthquake Aftershocks and Ongoing Crisis**
    *   A three-year-old was rescued six days after twin earthquakes, but aftershocks continue to fray nerves.
    *   The death toll has surpassed 1,900, with over 10,000 injured and tens of thousands missing.
    *   Many devastated areas lack government aid, with residents resorting to self-rescue efforts.
    *   The UN warns of urgent need for food and shelter; US pledges $300m, China $15m.
    *   Sources: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c1jykwk8n18o](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c1jykwk8n18o), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cqj1210rj0yo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cqj1210rj0yo)
