## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US Supreme Court upholds birthright citizenship**
    *   Decision rejects President Trump's executive order to limit the 150-year-old policy for US-born babies.
    *   Ruling based on the 14th Amendment's Citizenship Clause (6-3 decision).
    *   Trump called the decision "too bad" and vowed to pursue legislative action.
    *   Impact: A significant setback for the Trump administration's immigration agenda, welcomed by civil rights groups. (Source: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cgmepnx1wzzo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cgmepnx1wzzo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/30/supreme-court-birthright-citizenship-ruling.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/30/supreme-court-birthright-citizenship-ruling.html))

    *   No direct meetings with Iranian officials are scheduled at this time.
    *   Talks aim to solidify a preliminary agreement and address recent exchanges of strikes.

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **US Fed to re-evaluate inflation metrics**
    *   Federal Reserve Chairman Kevin Warsh announced a "new course" for monetary policy, including a re-evaluation of how inflation is measured.
    *   Five task forces will explore new technologies and data points, moving beyond traditional CPI and PCE.
    *   Treasury yields rose after Warsh stated that "prices are too high," indicating potential for future rate hikes.
    *   Impact: Aims for more real-time economic understanding, potentially leading to different monetary policy decisions. (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/01/warsh-faces-multiple-alternative-inflation-signs-as-fed-charts-new-course.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/01/treasury-yields-us10y-kevin-warsh-fed.html))

*   **US employment slowdown**
    *   Private sector employment in the US increased by 98,000 in June, below the forecasted 110,000.
    *   Health and education services were the primary drivers of job growth.
    *   Impact: Suggests a slowdown in job creation, with potential implications for future Fed policy decisions. (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/01/private-payrolls-rose-by-98000-in-june-less-than-expected-adp-reports.html](https://www.cnbc.co.uk/news/articles/cn4r8n3ek80o))

    *   Impact: Creates uncertainty for North American trade relations, potentially leading to renewed bilateral negotiations. (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/01/trump-usmca-canada-mexico-trade-treaty.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/01/trump-usmca-canada-mexico-trade-treaty.html))
