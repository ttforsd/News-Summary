## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **US Labor Force Participation Hits 50-Year Low**

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Extreme Heatwaves Grip Northern Hemisphere**
    *   Europe and the UK experienced record-breaking heatwaves in May and June, with another projected.
        *   Details: Temperatures surpassed previous June highs by 2C or more, with many "tropical nights" (above 20C).
        *   Context: Scientists attribute increased frequency and intensity to human-induced climate change, noting Europe's rapid warming.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c8e2j0j87reo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c8e2j0j87reo))
    *   Super Typhoon Bavi is approaching Guam and the Northern Mariana Islands, prompting emergency evacuations.
        *   Forecast: Expected landfall with winds up to 257km/h (160mph) and "catastrophic" damage.
        *   Context: Bavi is the 11th Category 4 or 5 tropical cyclone in US territories in a decade, linked to climate change and El Niño.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cr7xpgx50jxo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cr7xpgx50jxo))
    *   An extreme heatwave threatens US power grids and Independence Day travel across central and eastern regions.
        *   Impact: Temperatures up to 105F (40.5C) are surging electricity demand, leading to power price hikes and travel disruptions.
        *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/03/extreme-heat-us-power-grids-july-4-travel.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/03/extreme-heat-us-power-grids-july-4-travel.html))

*   **AI's Evolving Role in the Workforce**
    *   Some employers are reversing AI-driven layoffs and rehiring human workers.
        *   Examples: Ford is rehiring experienced engineers for quality issues; Commonwealth Bank of Australia reversed customer service cuts after AI shortcomings.
        *   Trend: Highlights the limitations of AI and the growing recognition for human-AI collaboration in business growth.
