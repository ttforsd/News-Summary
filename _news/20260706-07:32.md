## Executive summary

*   **Ukraine Conflict Intensifies:** Russian missile strikes again hit Kyiv, killing 10 people, while Ukrainian drone attacks on Crimea target Russian supply lines, escalating the war.
*   **Iran's Leadership Transition:** A six-day funeral for former Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei underscores a shift to a more pragmatic and assertive new regime in Tehran.
