## Executive summary

## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Iran Tensions Escalate; Talks to Continue:**
    *   US President Trump declared the ceasefire "over" after Iran attacked commercial ships in the Strait of Hormuz.
    *   Despite renewed hostilities, both sides have agreed to continue "technical talks" to find solutions.
    *   US reimposed oil sanctions; Iran accused the US of breaching their preliminary deal.
    *   Iran seeks control over the northern Strait of Hormuz shipping route, while the US protects the southern.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/us-iran-war-strikes-strait-of-hormuz-technical-talks-progress-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/us-iran-war-strikes-strait-of-hormuz-technical-talks-progress-.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/09/trump-iran-war-strikes-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/09/trump-iran-war-strikes-hormuz.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/08/trump-iran-nato-summit-war-hormuz-strait.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/08/trump-iran-nato-summit-war-hormuz-strait.html), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c5yzm8m4peeo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c5yzm8m4peeo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/how-the-us-iran-deal-set-the-stage-for-renewed-fighting-over-the-strait-of-hormuz.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/iran-strait-hormuz-oil-tanker-mou-trump.html))

## Major regional Events

*   **Deadly Wildfire in Southern Spain:**
    *   A wildfire in Andalusia has killed at least 12 people and left 23 missing, with suspected British victims.
    *   The fire's cause is debated; regional leader cited a downed power line, while the electricity company denied fault.
    *   It is part of a summer of sustained heatwaves (40C) causing widespread fires across Southern Europe.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c8e2382jk7jo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c8e2382jk7jo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))
*   **Typhoon Bavi Threatens East Asia:**
    *   Typhoon Bavi is sweeping towards Taiwan and southeastern China after causing deadly landslides in the Philippines.
    *   At least 15 people died in landslides on Mindanao; the region braces for heavy rainfall, flight cancellations, and school closures.
    *   This follows recent Typhoon Maysak, which left 39 dead and caused significant damage in China.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c04y6wr03gxo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c04y6wr03gxo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))
*   **Fatal ICE Shooting in Houston Prompts Outcry:**
    *   An undocumented Mexican national was fatally shot by US immigration agents, who later stated he was not the intended target.
    *   DHS claims self-defense, but the incident has sparked protests and demands for an independent investigation.
    *   Mexico announced plans to file criminal complaints regarding citizen deaths in US custody.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cm2rm66xd17o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cm2rm66xd17o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))

## Global Economic & Market Movers

*   **Global Oil Demand Forecast to Decline:**
    *   The International Energy Agency (IEA) predicts the first annual drop in global oil demand since 2020.
    *   Disruption in Middle East production/exports, particularly the closure of the Strait of Hormuz, is a primary factor.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/iea-world-oil-demand-declines-iran-war.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/iea-world-oil-demand-declines-iran-war.html))
*   **US Stock Market Outperforms Muted Economic Growth:**
    *   US stock indices saw strong gains in H1 2026 (S&P 500 up nearly 10%), despite modest GDP growth (1.9%).
    *   AI-driven tech sector largely responsible for the market boom, creating a "K-shaped" recovery.
    *   The Fed established a task force to assess AI's economic impact, with members largely optimistic about its potential.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/why-the-stock-market-and-economy-may-seem-out-of-sync.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/why-the-stock-market-and-economy-may-seem-out-of-sync.html), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/09/kevin-warsh-names-members-of-his-federal-reserve-task-forces-including-marc-andreessen-doug-mcmillon.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/09/kevin-warsh-names-members-of-his-federal-reserve-task-forces-including-marc-andreessen-doug-mcmillon.html))
*   **SK Hynix Nasdaq Debut Boosts AI Chip Market:**
    *   South Korean chipmaker SK Hynix surged on its Nasdaq debut, raising $26.5 billion in the largest US listing by a foreign firm.
    *   The company, a key Nvidia supplier, highlights "enormous" demand for AI memory chips.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/sk-hynix-skhy-stock-nasdaq.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/sk-hynix-skhy-stock-nasdaq.html), [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c4gym70r0y4o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c4gym70r0y4o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))
*   **South Korea's Kospi Enters Bear Market:**
    *   The Kospi index fell into bear territory (down >20% from its peak) despite being the world's best-performing market earlier this year.
    *   This sharp reversal is attributed to AI skepticism and extreme market concentration in chipmakers.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/09/kospi-bear-territory-ai-samsung-skhynix-chipmakers.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/09/kospi-bear-territory-ai-samsung-skhynix-chipmakers.html))
*   **Volkswagen Announces Major Restructuring:**
    *   The automotive giant plans to cut its model lineup by up to half and reduce production capacity to 9 million vehicles/year.
    *   The restructuring aims to enhance competitiveness amid intense competition and regulatory pressures.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/volkswagen-vw-autos-job-cuts-germany.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/volkswagen-vw-autos-job-cuts-germany.html))
*   **EasyJet Considers Rival Takeover Bid:**
    *   EasyJet is weighing a $7.7 billion takeover offer from Apollo, sparking a bidding war.
    *   The European airline sector faces headwinds from high fuel costs and Middle East instability.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/easyjet-apollo-takeover-bid-castlelake-share-price.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/easyjet-apollo-takeover-bid-castlelake-share-price.html))
*   **Rotterdam Port Under Pressure to Decarbonize:**
    *   Europe's largest port faces an environmental lawsuit demanding a faster transition from fossil fuels.
    *   This highlights the dilemma between maintaining significant economic activity and achieving green sustainability goals.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c39y78rwrmno?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c39y78rwrmno?at_campaign=rss))
*   **Stablecoin Issuer Circle Approved as Trust Bank:**
    *   US regulators granted Circle approval to operate as a trust bank, enhancing its management of USDC reserves.
    *   This reflects a growing trend in the crypto industry seeking integration as regulated financial infrastructure.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/circle-gets-an-occ-bank-charter-as-stablecoin-competition-heats-up-shares-surge-14percent.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/circle-gets-an-occ-bank-charter-as-stablecoin-competition-heats-up-shares-surge-14percent.html))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **China Achieves First Reusable Rocket Landing:**
    *   China's Long March 10B rocket successfully landed vertically on a floating platform after separating from its upper stage.
    *   This marks a significant advancement in reusable space technology, aiming to reduce launch costs and challenge global competitors.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cm2rmmx86pdo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cm2rmmx86pdo?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss))
*   **Micron Boosts US Chipmaking Investment:**
    *   Micron announced billions in new investments to strengthen the US semiconductor supply chain.
    *   This includes $500 million for GlobalWafers and increases total planned US investment to $250 billion through 2035.
    *   The move aims to meet surging demand for memory chips essential for AI infrastructure.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/09/micron-stock-us-chipmaking.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/09/micron-stock-us-chipmaking.html))
*   **EU Targets Meta's "Addictive" Social Media Designs:**
    *   The EU Commission found Meta in preliminary breach of the Digital Services Act over Instagram and Facebook's design features.
    *   Features like infinite scroll and autoplay are deemed "addictive" and pose risks, especially to minors.
    *   Meta faces potential fines up to 6% of its total annual turnover if the findings are confirmed.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/meta-instagram-facebook-addictive-design-breach-eu-laws.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/meta-instagram-facebook-addictive-design-breach-eu-laws.html))
*   **Concerns Rise Over Teen Dependence on AI Chatbots:**
    *   A new addiction to AI chatbots is emerging among teenagers, with half of US teens reportedly using them.
    *   Experts warn current social media bans and legislation overlook this growing digital safety issue.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/tech-download-social-media-bans-ai-chatbots.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/10/tech-download-social-media-bans-ai-chatbots.html))
