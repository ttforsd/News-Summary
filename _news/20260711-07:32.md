    *   Typhoon Bavi, one of the strongest in decades, is sweeping towards Taiwan, Japan, and southeastern China.
    *   Landslides in the southern Philippines, triggered by storms, killed at least 15 people.
    *   Flights cancelled, schools suspended, and residents stocking up on supplies.

## Global Economic & Market Movers
