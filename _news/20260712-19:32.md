## International Relations & Diplomacy

## Global Economic & Market Movers

    *   Tech executives report "almost unlimited" demand for AI infrastructure, with energy as the primary limiter.
    *   Enterprises are increasingly focusing on return on investment ("valuemaxxing") from AI spending.
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/12/ai-demand-chips-data-centers-stock-volatility.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/12/ai-demand-chips-data-centers-stock-volatility.html))
