## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US Escalates Standoff in Strait of Hormuz**
    *   President Trump announced a 20% toll on all cargo and reimposed a blockade on Iranian ports.
    *   This follows escalating US-Iran strikes and conflicting claims over the strait's status.
    *   Oil prices jumped nearly 8% in response to these developments.
    *   The UN International Maritime Organization stated no legal basis exists for mandatory tolls.

*   **UK Secures Landmark Trade Deal with Switzerland**
    *   A new services trade agreement is valued at £5.2bn annually in exports.
    *   Benefits include scrapped mobile roaming charges and e-gate access for Britons at Swiss airports.
    *   Aims to enhance post-Brexit ties and service mobility.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c621p9yjz1zo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c621p9yjz1zo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/13/uk-switzerland-free-trade-deal-brexit.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/13/uk-switzerland-free-trade-deal-brexit.html))

## Science, Tech, & Health (Major Developments)

*   **Indian Scientists Create Detailed Brainstem Atlas**
    *   The Sudha Gopalakrishnan Brain Centre produced Anchor, the world's most detailed 3D brainstem atlas at cellular resolution.
    *   Combines over 500 tissue sections, linking MRI scans to individual nerve cells.
    *   Expected to advance understanding of neurological disorders like Alzheimer's and Parkinson's.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cg53l737v1qo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cg53l737v1qo))

*   **TSMC Reports Strong June Revenue Surge**
    *   The world's largest contract chipmaker, TSMC, saw a 68% year-on-year jump in June revenue.
    *   Indicates robust demand for AI chips, with TSMC "sold out" on advanced N3 chips.
    *   Sources: ([https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/13/tsmc-june-revenue-rises-percent-ahead-second-quarter.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/13/tsmc-june-revenue-rises-percent-ahead-second-quarter.html))
