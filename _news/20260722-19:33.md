## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **US-Saudi Nuclear Deal Expected**
    *   The US is set to announce a deal allowing Saudi Arabia a civilian nuclear program.
    *   Deal may permit Saudi uranium enrichment on its soil, raising proliferation concerns.
    *   Saudi Arabia stated it would develop nuclear weapons if Iran does.
    *   The agreement is not contingent on Saudi-Israel diplomatic normalization.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cj03r59z73po](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cj03r59z73po))

*   **US-Iran Tensions Escalate with Infrastructure Threats**
    *   President Trump threatens to bomb Iranian bridges and power plants, including in Tehran, if Iran attacks Strait of Hormuz shipping.
    *   Iran warns of retaliation against US interests if its nuclear facilities, like "Pickaxe Mountain," are targeted.
    *   US Central Command conducted its 11th consecutive night of strikes to degrade Iran's shipping threat capabilities.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cdrv0p37k8jo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cdrv0p37k8jo), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/22/us-iran-war-trump-hormuz-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/22/us-iran-war-trump-hormuz-.html))

*   **Netanyahu Arrest Warrant Discussions in New York**
    *   NYC Mayor Zohran Mamdani retracted his pledge to arrest Israeli PM Netanyahu, citing lack of legal authority.
    *   Mamdani calls on the US federal government to enforce the ICC war crimes warrant.
    *   President Trump has asserted Netanyahu would not be arrested in the US.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c204p64pqzno](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c204p64pqzno), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/20/trump-netanyahu-arrest-mamdani-new-york-israel.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/20/trump-netanyahu-arrest-mamdani-new-york-israel.html))

*   **US-China AI Talks and Potential Sanctions**
    *   US and China are planning AI talks for September, preceding Chinese President Xi Jinping's White House visit.
    *   US Treasury Secretary Bessent warns of potential sanctions on China for "AI model theft."
    *   (Source: [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/22/cnbc-daily-open-ai-race-potential-tariffs-and-iran-conflict-heat-up-.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/22/cnbc-daily-open-ai-race-potential-tariffs-and-iran-conflict-heat-up-.html))

*   **Trump's Generic Drug Tariffs Proposed**

## Major regional Events

*   **Middle East Shipping Disruptions Intensify**

    *   The EU Naval Force advises vessels linked to US, Israel, or Saudi interests to avoid the Red Sea.

    *   The change follows widespread protests after the removal of popular Defence Minister Mykhailo Fedorov.

    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cyvl35z3917o](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cyvl35z3917o), [https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/22/ukraine-zelenskyy-protests-russia-war-putin.html](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/22/ukraine-zelenskyy-protests-russia-war-putin.html))

*   **Ukrainian Drones Strike Russian Retailer Warehouses**
    *   Ukrainian drones hit warehouses of Russia's largest online retailer, Wildberries, in two south-west cities.
    *   One person was killed and 14 injured in the second such strike within four days.
    *   Ukraine claims these hubs supply the Russian army with drone components.
    *   (Source: [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c36de9n4pxpo](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c36de9n4pxpo))
