## International Relations & Diplomacy

*   **Middle East Tensions Escalate**:
    *   Yemen's Houthis claim attacks on two Saudi oil tankers in the Red Sea; one, *Encelia*, confirmed hit.
    *   US carried out its 12th consecutive night of strikes on Iran, reportedly killing two people.

    *   President Trump threatened to bomb Iranian infrastructure, including a nuclear facility, if Iran attacks ships.
