## Executive summary

*   **US-Iran Tensions Escalate**: Both countries trade strikes in the Gulf. US President Trump threatens a "massive attack" on Iran while also making a potential nuclear deal with Saudi Arabia contingent on Israeli recognition.
*   **Global Trade Under Strain**: The US imposes new tariffs on 60 trading partners citing forced labor, drawing international criticism. Additionally, the US threatens the EU with tariffs over tech giant fines.
*   **Middle East Oil Volatility**: Oil prices briefly surged above $100 per barrel due to Houthi attacks on Red Sea shipping, then dropped on reports of Pakistan-led peace talks between the US and Iran.

*   **Humanitarian Crises & Protests**: India faces massive youth protests over exam paper leaks, met with police action. Indonesia deals with the aftermath of a baby trafficking conviction involving Singapore. Tanzania charges a university lecturer with terrorism amid anti-government crackdowns.
